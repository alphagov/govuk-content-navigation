Detail

This guidance describes the statutory assessment and reporting requirements for key stage 2 ( KS2 ) during the 2014 to 2015 academic year. It is for:

  • headteachers
  • governing bodies
  • local authority assessment co-ordinators
  • anyone else involved in assessing and reporting KS2

Statutory guidance is issued by law; you must follow it unless there’s a good reason not to.

The sections together make up the assessment and reporting arrangements ( ARA ) for the 2014 to 2015 academic year.
