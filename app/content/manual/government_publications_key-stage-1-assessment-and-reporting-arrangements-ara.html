Detail

This statutory guidance describes the statutory assessment and reporting requirements for key stage 1 ( KS1 ) during the 2014 to 2015 academic year. It is for:

  • headteachers
  • governing bodies
  • local authority assessment co-ordinators
  • anyone else involved in assessing and reporting KS1

The sections together make up the ‘Assessment and reporting arrangements’ ( ARA ) for the 2014 to 2015 academic year.
