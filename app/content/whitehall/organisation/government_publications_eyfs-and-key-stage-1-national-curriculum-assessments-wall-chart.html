Detail

Please note: in 2016 there will be no requirement to administer the KS1 English grammar, punctuation and spelling test or to use the result as part of that assessment.

The teacher assessment submission deadline for key stage 1 has been changed. All submissions must now be made to your local authority by Thursday 30 June 2016. The wall chart has been updated to reflect the new date.

This wall chart is for schools taking part in the 2015 to 2016 key stage 1 national curriculum assessment cycle.

Headteachers and teachers should use it to help them complete important tasks including:

  • administering the phonics screening check
  • administering the end of key stage 1 assessments
