Detail

Following the approach taken last year we will continue to issue indicative results prior to the final data being published as official statistics on the FE choices comparison website.

In addition to the end of survey report being issued in May 2015, 2 in-survey reports will also be issued to eligible colleges and training organisations to inform them of their survey responses to date. These additional reports are available from the provider extranet .

For those colleges and training providers also in scope for the community learning trial, a final indicative report will also be available In May 2015 on the provider extranet .

The above documents will help guide you through your results in these reports.
