The Counter-Terrorism and Security Act 2015 contains a duty on specified authorities to prevent people from being drawn into terrorism. This is known as the ‘ Prevent Duty ’.

This document sets out how BIS Prevent advisers will monitor compliance with the Prevent Duty in non-publicly funded and unregulated further education providers.
