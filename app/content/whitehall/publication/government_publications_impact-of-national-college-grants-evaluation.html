Details

This impact evaluation looks at a number of National College for Teaching and Leadership (NCTL) grants including:

  • national and local leaders in education deployment fund
  • national leaders of education bursary funds
  • professional partner grants
  • building capacity grants
  • targeted support succession planning grants

The project:

  • assesses the impact of grants in terms of outcomes for schools, leaders and pupils over the 2 years from April 2012 to July 2014
  • evaluates the grants, concentrating on the inputs, outputs and outcomes
