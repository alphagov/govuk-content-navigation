Details

Data View was relaunched on 1 December 2015. It is now at https://public.tableau.com/views/Dataview/Viewregionalperformanceovertime Users can now identify individual providers by their overall effectiveness and link directly to the provider page on Ofsted’s reports website.

Data View shows Ofsted inspection data in simple graphs that make the data quickly and easily accessible. You can compare and contrast performance in inspections between regions, local authority areas and parliamentary constituencies.

Please note that Data View presents point-in-time data. Providers may have been re-inspected since the data was last updated. Always refer to Ofsted’s reports website for the latest inspection grade.

To ask questions or provide feedback please email: dataview@ofsted.gov.uk .
