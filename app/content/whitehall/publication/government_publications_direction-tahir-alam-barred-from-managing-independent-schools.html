Decision

Direction: Tahir Alam barred from managing independent schools

From:
Department for Education and The Rt Hon Nicky Morgan MP
First published:
9 September 2015
Applies to:
England

A decision from the Secretary of State to direct that Tahir Alam should be prohibited from the management of independent schools.

Document

Regulation of school managers and governors: prohibition direction

PDF , 100KB , 1 page

Detail

Nicky Morgan, the Secretary of State for Education, finds Tahir Alam unsuitable to take part in the management of an independent school (including an academy or free school) under section 128 of the Education and Skills Act 2008.

The barring decision also has the effect of disqualifying the person from being a governor at a maintained school.
