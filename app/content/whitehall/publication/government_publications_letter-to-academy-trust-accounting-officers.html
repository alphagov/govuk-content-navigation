Detail

These letters are sent from time to time and at the start of each academic year. Peter Lauener writes in his capacity as the Accounting Officer of the Education Funding Agency (EFA) to all accounting officers of academy, free school, UTC and studio school trusts.

These letters sent in May and September 2014 cover key responsibilities of financial management and governance, and highlight any changes that may have taken place.
