Details

The 5 new headline measures in the performance tables , covering 16 to 19 education in 2016 and 2017 are:

  • progress
  • attainment
  • progress in English and maths
  • retention
  • destinations

The technical guide explains how the headline measures are calculated.

The briefing note presents a summary of the measures.
