Details

Peter Mucklow, National Director of Young People at Education Funding Agency, has written to post-16 institutions explaining funding for the academic year 2017 to 2018 for students aged 16 to 19 and students aged 19 to 25 with an education, health and care plan.

Areas included in the letter are:

  • funding for academic year 2017 to 2018
  • national funding rates
  • formula protection funding
  • maths and English
  • student support (including free meals)
  • high needs students
  • traineeships
  • lagged student numbers
  • 19+ continuing students
  • disadvantage block 2
  • allocations process and timeline
