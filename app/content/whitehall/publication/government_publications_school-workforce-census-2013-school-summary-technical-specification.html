Detail

This school summary technical specification is for suppliers of management information systems ( MIS ) to schools and local authorities completing the 2013 school workforce census .

This document specifies requirements for the school summary, which should be generated automatically by MIS software. The school summary:

  • allows schools to check the accuracy and completeness of the return
  • helps local authorities check school returns
