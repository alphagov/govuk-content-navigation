Detail

This report sets out the STRB ’s analysis of evidence given by relevant organisations and makes proposals on teachers’ pay. The government responded to the report in Parliament.

The STRB provides independent advice to the Prime Minister and Secretary of State for Education on school teachers’ pay and conditions in England and Wales.
