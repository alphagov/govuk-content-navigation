This official statistics release (OSR) provides the earliest information on the achievements of young people taking diplomas in the academic year 2010 to 2011. This information is based on data collated for the Department for Education by the Diploma Aggregation Service (DAS) and covers all learners who achieved a 14 to 19 diploma course of study during the academic year 2010 to 2011.

Achievement information is provided for England by subject, diploma level and key personal characteristics.

A consultation is currently taking place on whether this publication should be discontinued in future years. You can take part in this consultation until the end of December 2011.
