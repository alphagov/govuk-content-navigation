Detail

This document is designed to guide local authority users through the various aspects of COLLECT (Collections Online - Learners, Education, Children and Teachers) as related to the children in need census return, including initial submission, data validation and final approval by the Department for Education.

The COLLECT system is used by local authorities and the department for processing data collection returns.
