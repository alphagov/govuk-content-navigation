Details

The guide gives an overview of round 2 of the practice and improvement fund.

The following organisations can apply for this round:

  • voluntary adoption agencies ( VAAs )
  • adoption support agencies ( ASAs )
  • other voluntary sector organisations ( VSOs )

VAAs and ASAs are organisations that recruit, approve and support people interested in adopting a child.

How to apply

Fill in the application form and email it to adoption.PIF@education.gov.uk by midday on 16 January 2017.
