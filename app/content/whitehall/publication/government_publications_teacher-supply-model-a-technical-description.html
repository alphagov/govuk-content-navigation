Detail

The Department for Education uses the teacher supply model ( TSM ) to calculate the optimum number of initial teacher training ( ITT ) places required. The TSM aims to match the future supply of qualified teachers to the estimated future demand within the state-funded sector in England. Outputs from the model are used by the National College of Teaching and Leadership ( NCTL ) when allocating ITT places to training providers.

Future demand for teachers is estimated using projections of pupil numbers in the state-funded sector, this is then adjusted by the estimated number of teachers required to implement the departmentâ€™s new policy initiatives.
