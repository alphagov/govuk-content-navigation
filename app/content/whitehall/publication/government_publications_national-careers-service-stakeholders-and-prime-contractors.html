The Skills Funding Agency is responsible for the commissioning, contracting and performance management of the National Careers Service .

The National Careers Service provides access to independent, professional advice on careers, skills and training. The service is available online, through advisers on the telephone and, face to face in the community.

The National Careers Service also works in close partnership with Jobcentre Plus to provide intensive support for their customers.
