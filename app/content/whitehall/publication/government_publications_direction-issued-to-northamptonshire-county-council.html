Details

Ofsted inspected Northamptonshire County Council’s:

  • arrangements for the protection of children and published a report on 20 March 2013
  • adoption service and published a report on 2 May 2013
  • looked-after children’s services and published a report on 23 August 2013

With the resulting reports Ofsted found the council to be ‘inadequate’ overall.

In response, ministers issued a statutory direction on the 2nd October 2013.
