Detail

GCSE and equivalent attainment for pupils attending state-funded schools in England. Data is broken down by different pupil characteristics, specifically:

  • gender
  • ethnicity
  • eligibility for free school meals (FSM)
  • disadvantage
  • special educational needs (SEN)
  • English as a first language.
