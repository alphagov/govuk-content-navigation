Data is given for schools open at December 2010, as well as outturn measures for December 2008 and 2009. This is an annual publication and is the third one in the series. This year we have extended the coverage from secondary schools only, and this publication now includes figures for primary schools, special schools and pupil referral units

The figures are derived by combining annual inspections data produced by Ofsted to determine the latest inspection judgement for each school as at December of each year. Therefore this publication differs from Ofsted’s published annual data, which reports inspections within an academic year. Ofsted published the latest annual inspection judgements on 23 November 2010, accessible via Inspection Judgements 2009/10 for Maintained Schools .

OSR 08/2011 was issued on 28 April 2011. This statistical release contained errors. The errors affected the local authority level figures for primary schools in 2010. OSR 08/2011 was re-issued on 10 May 2011 with corrected figures in place of the errors.
