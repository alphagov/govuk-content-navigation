From:

This document outlines the steps that initial teacher training providers should follow when migrating accreditation to another legal entity.

Documents

Migration of initial teacher training accreditation process

Ref: NCTL-20016-2016 PDF , 210KB , 14 pages

Details

In exceptional circumstances you may seek to migrate your initial teacher training (ITT) accreditation to another legal entity. This is when you wish to stop holding responsibility and accountability for provision on behalf of the partnership.

You should take account of the advice document accompanying the Secretary of State’s ITT Criteria when seeking to migrate your accreditation.
