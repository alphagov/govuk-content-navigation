Microsoft memorandum of understanding: education cloud transition agreement

Ref: DFE - 00012 - 2016 PDF , 236KB , 11 pages

Details

This MoU between Microsoft and the Department for Education (DfE) allows all schools in the UK to take advantage of discounts and better licensing terms across a range of Microsoft academic software.

This agreement ends on 1 July 2018.

The MoU with Microsoft is not contractually binding and schools are under no obligation to use Microsoft products.
