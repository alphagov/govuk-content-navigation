Detail

Research that looks into the outcomes of 16,000 pupils with SEND through data we collected during the first year of the AfA project.

We collected the data through:

  • teacher surveys
  • academic sources
  • interviews with strategic people
  • longitudinal case studies of 20 AfA schools
  • mini case studies of 100 pupils and their families
  • AfA events
