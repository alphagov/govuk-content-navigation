Details

For SKE funding to be released, School Direct lead schools and initial teacher training ( ITT ) providers must have a signed grant offer letter in place.

All highlighted sections in the grant offer letter must be completed and annex A must be signed by the accounting officer.

The grant offer letter should be returned to the SKE team at ta.ske@education.gov.uk .
