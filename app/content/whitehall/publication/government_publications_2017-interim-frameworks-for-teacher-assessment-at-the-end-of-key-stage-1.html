2017 Interim teacher assessment frameworks at the end of key stage 1

Ref: ISBN 978-1-78644-434-9 , STA/17/7877/e PDF , 270KB , 11 pages

Details

These interim frameworks are to support teachers in making robust and accurate judgements for pupils at the end of key stage 1 in 2017. The interim teacher assessment frameworks are for 2016 to 2017 only. The Department for Education is evaluating options for future years.

The interim frameworks set out the standard(s) a pupil will be assessed against at the end of the key stage for reading, writing, mathematics and science.

To show that pupils have met the standard, teachers will need to have evidence that a pupil demonstrates consistent attainment of all the statements within the standard.
