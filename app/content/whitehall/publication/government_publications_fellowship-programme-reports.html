Detail

The National College for Teaching and Leadership offered the fellowship programme to national leaders of education ( NLEs ). NLEs could then apply and were selected for the programme to develop their leadership skills and experience.

The selected NLEs were asked to develop and produce policies and reports during their time in the programme. These reports are from 2012 and 2013 respectively.
