Details

Information on all schools in England, including the:

  • unique reference number
  • local authority
  • school name
  • address
  • type of school
  • age range
  • phase (nursery, primary or secondary)
  • contact information

This data comes from EduBase , the Department for Education’s register of schools. We update this information every month.
