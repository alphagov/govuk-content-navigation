A guide to new studio school revenue funding: 2013 to 2014 academic year

PDF , 218KB , 10 pages

Details

This guide explains how revenue funding for studio schools is allocated in the 2013 to 2014 academic year.

It includes details of the following types of funding available to studio schools:

  • local pre-16 formula funding
  • national 16 to 19 formula funding
  • pupil premium
  • educational services grant
  • insurance grant
  • business rates grant
  • special educational needs (SEN) commissioner top-up funding
  • post-opening grant
