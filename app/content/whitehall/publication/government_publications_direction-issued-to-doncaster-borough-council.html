Direction issued by DfE to Doncaster metropolitan borough council about children's social care services.

Direction issued to Doncaster borough council

PDF , 568KB , 14 pages

Details

Confirmation that services for children with disabilities previously run by Doncaster metropolitan borough council will now be the responsibility of the Doncaster Children’s Services Trust.

We also issued a direction in September 2014, requiring the council to enter into a services agreement with Doncaster Children’s Services Trust.
