Documents

School admission appeals data collection 2015: COLLECT guide

Ref: DFE-00623-2014 PDF , 858KB , 20 pages

Details

This document helps local authority users complete and submit their 2015 returns using COLLECT (Collections Online - Learners, Education, Children and Teachers), the department’s centralised data collection and management system.

The guide includes information on:

  • accessing COLLECT
  • adding a return
  • editing a return
  • exporting a return
  • error queries
