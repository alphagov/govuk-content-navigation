Information for parents: 2016 national curriculum test results at the end of key stage 1

Ref: ISBN 978-1-78644-258-1 , STA/16/7714/e PDF , 1.62MB , 4 pages

Details

This leaflet is for parents with a child in year 2 at primary school.

It explains the arrangements for the 2016 academic year, highlighting what parents need to know about the test results, as well as explaining how teacher assessment judgements work.

Schools may want to refer to the leaflet in their own reports to parents.
