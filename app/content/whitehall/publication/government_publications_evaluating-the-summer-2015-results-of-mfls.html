Details

This report explains what we found when we looked at the 2015 assessments in these qualifications and how the exam boards responded to our findings. It sets out some of the wider work we have been doing to address stakeholders concerns about the current MFL A level qualifications and explains how we have used the findings from the 2014 research to inform the reform of these qualifications.
