Details

This document is for inspection to 31 March 2017. For inspection from 1 April 2017 please use the social care common inspection framework (SCCIF) .

Ofsted inspects:

  • residential provision in all independent boarding and residential special schools, except those that are members of associations affiliated to the Independent Schools Council
  • boarding and residential provision in all maintained and non-maintained schools, academies, free schools and pupil referral units
