How to apply to set up a free school: guidance

Ref: DFE-00175-2016 PDF , 4.09MB , 50 pages

Details

This guide explains the law around setting up a free school and gives information about the application process.

You’ll also need to:

  • register your interest before you apply to open a free school
  • complete a suitability and declaration form for each member, trustee and director of your academy trust

More information on opening a free school or studio school is available.

Contact the New Schools Network if you need more advice on how to set up a free school.

Contact the Studio Schools Trust if you need more advice on how to set up a studio school.
