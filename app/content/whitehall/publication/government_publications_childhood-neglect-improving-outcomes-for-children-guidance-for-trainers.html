Details

Neglect is known to be damaging for children in the short and long-term: it can seriously impair children’s emotional, physical, cognitive and behavioural development.

This resource is intended to complement existing training materials and programmes of relevance to neglect. Local Safeguarding Children Boards already run extensive programmes of inter-agency training and these materials can be used to develop courses that focus more specifically on neglect.

Includes

  • key messages for effective practice with neglect
  • training framework
  • about presentations and notes
  • about exercises and guidance
  • family case studies
  • training methods
