Details

Information to assist schools in planning for key stage 2 assessments during the 2016 to 2017 academic year.

The planner will also be of use to local authorities and academy trusts.

The dates include actions and deadlines for the following:

  • ordering test materials
  • applying for access arrangements
  • receiving test materials
  • submitting teacher assessment data
  • reporting to LAs and parents
