Details

How learners can opt-out of sharing their personal learning record ( PLR ) data

Learners may choose not to share their PLR data and can opt-out:

  • as part of the enrolment process with their learning provider.
  • by contacting the Learning Records Service ( LRS ) customer helpdesk on 0345 602 2589. They will need to provide some personal details to confirm their identify, which may include their Unique Learner Number ( ULN ), if known.

Learning providers

You must ensure you provide a copy of the privacy notice to your learners so they understand how their data is used.

Awarding organisations

The privacy notices are for those organisations who have a direct relationship with learners. All learners should be given a copy of the privacy notice to ensure they understand how their qualification data is used, and if they choose to allow others to view their PLR .

If you do not have a direct relationship with learners, you must instruct your centres to ensure they provide a copy of the privacy notice to their learners so that they understand how their data is used.
