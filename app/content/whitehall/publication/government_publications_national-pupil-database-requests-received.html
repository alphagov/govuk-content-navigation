Data Management Advisory Panel (DMAP): terms of reference

PDF , 364KB , 14 pages

Details

The ‘completed third-party requests’ spreadsheet lists third-party requests for data from the national pupil database ( NPD ) that we completed between April 2012 and December 2016.

The ‘third-party requests in the pipeline’ spreadsheet lists third-party requests that are open and have received no data up to 31 January 2017. The detail for these requests is subject to change as they progress through the application process.

The spreadsheets are available in Excel and OpenDocument formats.

The terms of reference document explains the purpose of the Data Management Advisory Panel ( DMAP ) and its role in deciding whether to approve or reject a third-party request for data from the NPD .

Information on how to apply for a data extract from the national pupil database is also available.
