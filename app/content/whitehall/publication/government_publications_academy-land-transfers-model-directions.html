Details

Ask your solicitor or legal adviser to use this document if the owner of the freehold of your school site is any of the following:

  • a foundation
  • a trust
  • the governing body

The document will help you transfer the freehold to the academy trust.

If a foundation or trust owns your school’s land, use the model directions to a foundation or trust that relate to the type of academy you are opening.

If your school’s governing body owns your school’s land, use the model directions to a governing body that relate to the type of academy you are opening.
