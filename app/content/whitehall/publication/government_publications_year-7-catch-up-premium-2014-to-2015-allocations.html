Details

The allocations tables show £500 for each pupil in a state-funded school who did not achieve at least level 4 in reading and/or mathematics at key stage 2. The final figures have been calculated using the 2014 key stage 2 data and the autumn 2014 school census data.

Figures are not shown in cases where the number of eligible pupils is 5 or fewer.

The conditions of grant document outlines the terms and conditions on which this grant is payable.

The funding was announced by Schools Minister David Laws on 11 November 2014.
