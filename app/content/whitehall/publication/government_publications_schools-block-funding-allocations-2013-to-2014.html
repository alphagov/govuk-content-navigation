Details

The schools included in this dataset are all maintained schools and academies in England with “schools block pupils”, which are pupils from reception to year 11 in mainstream schools that are not in high needs units or resourced provision.

A detailed data file in accompanies this report and contains the funding formula figures.

The schools block funding formulae are also available from this website.
