Details

The Education Funding Agency (EFA) business cycle sets out important, useful information for the 12 months from January to December 2015 to help institutions plan ahead. This includes information about when we require institutions to provide information to us, and when we plan to make announcements or provide information to institutions.

The cycle can be viewed and printed as a wall planner, with versions for academies and free schools, local authorities, schools, commercial and charitable providers, further education colleges and sixth-form colleges.

Although most timings are fixed, some (where indicated) may by subject to change, for example as announcements are made throughout the year. We have provided links to guidance where relevant in the wall planner, and this will be updated automatically to reflect such announcements.

We will also communicate any updates to the milestones in the cycle in the weekly EFA e-bulletin . Revised versions of the wall planners and timeline will be published at the beginning of each term.
