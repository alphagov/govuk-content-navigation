The funding in scope includes:

  • Adult Skills Budget
  • 16 to 18 Apprenticeships (including Agency-funded 16 to 18 traineeships)

The completed requests will be reviewed internally and the outcome communicated back to colleges, employers and training organisations by the end of May 2014.
