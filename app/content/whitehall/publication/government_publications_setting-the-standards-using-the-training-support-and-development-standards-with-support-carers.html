Setting the standards: using the training, support and development standards with support carers

Ref: DFE-32015-2012 PDF , 497KB , 44 pages

Details

This guide is also for supervising social workers, link workers and training officers.

It contains information on how the training, support and development standards apply to support carers.

Support care is where local authorities provide a series of short breaks away from home for vulnerable children alongside support for the children’s families.
