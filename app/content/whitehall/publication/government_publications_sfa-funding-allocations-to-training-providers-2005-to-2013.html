Details

The spreadsheets for 2005/06 to 2012/13 provide information on:

  • funding allocations to each provider for each of the years 2005/06 to 2008/13 for the main Learning and Skills Council funding programmes
  • the allocations summarised are by local authority
  • provider factors were used to calculate 2008 to 2009 allocations (the first year of demand-led funding)
