Details

Known issues are problems that affect an individualised learner record ( ILR ).

These include:

  • errors with the calculation of derived tables
  • systematic incorrect recording of data by providers
  • other data quality issues

Data collection for further education providers

Data library, individual learner record (ILR) guides, community learning, learning aim reference application (LARA).

  • ILR guides and templates
  • ILR guides, e-learning modules and templates for 2013 to 2014
  • ILR specification, validation rules and appendices 2015 to 2016
