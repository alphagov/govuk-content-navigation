Outward-facing schools: the Sinnott Fellowship

Ref: DFE-RR139 PDF , 901KB , 28 pages

Details

In 2009 the then Department for Children, Schools and Families ( DCSF ) introduced the Sinnott Fellowship to learn from some of the best practitioners in schools in outward‐facing work.

This report examines what an outward‐facing school looks like and their work to:

  • link everyone who might enhance students’ learning, wellbeing and opportunities
  • raise student aspirations and broaden their horizons
  • ensure that the school is a valuable resource for pupils and community
  • raise student attainment and school improvement

It builds on the case studies written about the fellows’ projects. Visit the outward-facing schools website for more information.
