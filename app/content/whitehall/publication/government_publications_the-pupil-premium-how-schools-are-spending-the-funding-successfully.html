Detail

This Ofsted survey report follows visits to 68 primary and secondary schools in autumn 2012 to see how effectively they were spending their pupil premium funding to maximise achievement.

It highlights effective practice and is accompanied by documents to help schools analyse gaps in achievement and plan their actions effectively.
