Documents

School workforce census 2014: guide for centrally employed staff

Ref: DFE-00577-2014 PDF , 1.69MB , 68 pages

Details

This guide provides school workforce census 2014 preparation instructions for children’s services staff in local authorities so that they:

  • understand the rationale behind, and purpose of, the census
  • are able to prepare for the census
  • are able to complete census returns during 2014
