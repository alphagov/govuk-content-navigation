Detail

This letter and calendar were sent to responsible officers in awarding organisations. We sent the organisations that we regulate a calendar of any known regular data we will require from them.

The aim is to reduce burden on those we regulate by making them aware of our needs in advance; this will help them to manage resources. The calendar provides a short explanation for each request - setting out the reasons for it, and whether we intend to publish the results of any subsequent analysis.
