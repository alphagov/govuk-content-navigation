On 13 December 2013, Education Funding Agency (EFA) wrote to Weald of Kent Academy Trust and Valley Invicta Academy Trust with a decision on their proposals for grammar school provision in Sevenoaks, Kent.

The assessment is that the proposals do not represent an expansion of the existing schools and therefore cannot be approved.
