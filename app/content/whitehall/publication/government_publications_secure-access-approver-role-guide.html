Detail

These guides are for secure access approvers and end users in:

  • schools
  • academies
  • academy trusts
  • further education colleges
  • local authorities

Approvers can create and administer secure access accounts for end users in their organisations.

End users can log into the secure access website and access systems (applications) that the approver has given them access to.

The Department for Education uses secure access to give external users in education institutions and local authorities access to:

  • COLLECT (Collections Online - Learners, Education, Children and Teachers)
  • school to school (S2S)
  • key to success (KtS) - for schools only
  • Teacher Services (including the Database of Qualified Teachers)
  • EFA Information Exchange
  • Edubase
  • Post-16 Portal
