Details

These documents describe in detail the data we have used to calculate the 2014 to 2015 education services grant ( ESG ) rates. We provide details on the transfer from local government revenue funding in 2014 to 2015 and the relevant pupil numbers.

We include calculations of the ESG rates and the ESG allocations to local authorities.
