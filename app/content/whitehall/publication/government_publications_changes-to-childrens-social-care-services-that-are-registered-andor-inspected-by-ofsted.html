Details

This guidance covers:

  • the changes a registered provider and where appropriate, registered manager, have to tell Ofsted about
  • how to make requests for changes to any conditions of registration
  • changes Ofsted may impose

It also sets out the changes a local authority fostering or adoption service needs to tell Ofsted about.
