Details

Ofsted inspected Sandwell metropolitan borough council and issued 3 reports on:

  • arrangements for safeguarding and looked-after children services
  • arrangements for the protection of children and young people
  • looked-after children services

Ofsted found the council to be ‘inadequate’ in each report.

In response the Secretary of State for Education issued a direction to Sandwell metropolitan borough council.

This direction has closed; a direction issued on 7 January 2016 replaces this direction.
