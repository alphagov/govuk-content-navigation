Provides policy and supporting guidance to overseas commands on the provision and governance of the early years foundation stage (EYFS).

Document

Delivering early years foundation stage (EYFS) in overseas settings (archived 23 August 2016)

Ref: JSP 819 PDF , 269KB

Detail

The main intention of this document is to provide direction and supporting guidance to overseas Commands on the minimum funded entitlement to Early Years provision for the 3 to 5 age group and the prioritisation criteria to be applied to determine eligibility to access non-funded places for the 0 to 3 age group. It also provides direction on the governance for EYFS , the registration, regulation and inspection of settings and the training and development of the workforce.
