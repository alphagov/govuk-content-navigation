A direction issued by DfE to Buckinghamshire county council due to its inadequate performance in children's social care services.

Direction issued to Buckinghamshire county council

PDF , 134KB , 3 pages

Details

This direction confirms that an adviser will be appointed to oversee the council’s improvements.

It follows an Ofsted report , published on 8 August 2014, which found the council’s leadership, governance and children’s services to be ‘inadequate’. Adoption and care-leaver services were found to ‘require improvement’.
