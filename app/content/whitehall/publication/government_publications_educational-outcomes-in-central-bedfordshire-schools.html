Ofsted’s Regional Director for the East of England, Andrew Cook, has written to express his concerns about educational outcomes for pupils in Central Bedfordshire schools. These include the low standards that pupils achieve at the end of Key Stage 2 and poor outcomes for disadvantaged children.
