School experience programme: information for schools

Ref: NCTL-20048-2016 PDF , 158KB , 11 pages

Details

The school experience programme provides central access to school experience for candidates wanting to apply for initial teacher training ( ITT ).

The information in this guide is for ITT providers, school-centred initial teacher training providers ( SCITTs ) and School Direct lead schools.

If you’re a candidate wishing to access the school experience programme, please go to the Get into Teaching website .

If you wish to register your school or have any queries, please contact:

School experience programme team

Telephone 0800 085 0962

If you have a complaint relating to the school experience programme please contact nctl.sep@education.gov.uk
