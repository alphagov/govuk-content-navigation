How we calculate and allocate revenue funding for alternative provision (AP) free schools in the 2016 to 2017 academic year.

Documents

A guide to new alternative provision free school revenue funding: 2016 to 2017

Ref: DFE-00112-2016 PDF , 268KB , 15 pages

Details

This guide explains how much revenue funding alternative provision ( AP ) free schools will receive.

A financial template that will help AP free schools plan their budget is available.

AP settings provide education for children who can’t attend a mainstream school.
