Detail

An informed analysis looking at creating an inclusive apprenticeship offer for young people aged 16 to 24 who have learning difficulties and/or disabilities (LDD).

This report was commissioned 2011 to 2012 by the apprenticeships unit which is a cross department unit working across the Department for Education and Department of Business, Innovation and Skills.
