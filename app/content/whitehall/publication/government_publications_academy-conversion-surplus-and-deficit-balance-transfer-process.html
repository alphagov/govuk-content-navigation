Treatment of surplus and deficit balances when maintained schools become academies

Ref: DFE-00421-2014 PDF , 210KB , 9 pages

Detail

This guidance is for:

  • local authorities
  • school leaders
  • school staff
  • governing bodies

It explains what happens if a school has a budget surplus or deficit when it becomes an academy and stops being maintained by the local authority.

This guidance relates to the Academies Act 2010 and the Academy Conversions (Transfer of School Surpluses) Regulations 2013 .
