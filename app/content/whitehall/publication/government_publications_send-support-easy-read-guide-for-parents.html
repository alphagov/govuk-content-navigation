Guide for parents with learning disabilities, explaining changes to the special educational needs and disabilities (SEND) system.

Document

Changes to the special educational needs and disability support system: easy-read guide for parents

https://www.mencap.org.uk/advice-and-support/children-and-young-people/sen-resources-easy-read-guides

Detail

You will find this guide useful if you are a parent and you want to understand how the changes will affect you and your child.

It has been jointly developed by the Department for Education and Mencap, with help from parents and young people with learning disabilities.

An easy-read guide for children and young people is also available.
