Details

Ofsted inspected Rochdale’s arrangements for the protection of children and published a report on 21 December 2012. Ofsted judged the council as ‘inadequate’ overall.

Inadequate performance has also been identified through adoption scorecards for 2012.

The improvement notice we issued on 10 April 2014 replaces this one.
