An improvement notice issued to Surrey county council due to poor performance in children's social care services.

Improvement notice issued to Surrey county council

Details

This improvement notice requires Surrey county council to take steps to improve its children social care services. It follows publication on 3 August 2015 of a report by Ofsted which judged the overall effectiveness of children’s services to be inadequate.
