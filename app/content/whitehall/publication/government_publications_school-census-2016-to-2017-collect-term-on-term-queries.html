School census 2016 to 2017: user guide for COLLECT term-on-term queries

Ref: DFE-00220-2016 PDF , 195KB , 9 pages

Details

You should use this guide while completing the school census in COLLECT , DfE’s centralised data collection and management system.

COLLECT automatically checks your data for substantial increases or decreases between the current term and the previous term and generates term-on-term queries.

More school census guidance is available.
