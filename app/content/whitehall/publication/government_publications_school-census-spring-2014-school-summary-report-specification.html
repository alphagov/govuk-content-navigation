School census spring 2014: school summary report technical specification

Ref: DFE-00066-2013 PDF , 508KB , 44 pages

Details

This specification is for suppliers developing software for use in management information systems that automatically generate the school summary report for the school census spring 2014.

The school summary report allows local authorities and schools to check the accuracy of school census returns before they are submitted to the Department for Education.
