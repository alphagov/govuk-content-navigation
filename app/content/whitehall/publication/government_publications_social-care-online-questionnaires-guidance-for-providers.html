Detail

The 2016 social care online questionnaires are now closed.

From 11 January to 1 April 2016 Ofsted’s social care online questionnaires are open and available for completion.

The questionnaires are Ofsted’s chance to hear what children and young people, parents, staff, social workers and other professionals say about children’s homes, adoption agencies, fostering services and residential family centres.

Providers distribute links to the questionnaires on Ofsted’s behalf: the guidance explains how providers distribute these links.

Providers can add the name of their service to the poster and use it to promote the online questionnaires.
