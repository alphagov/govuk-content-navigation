Details

This good practice example shows how St Edmund’s Catholic Primary School supports Traveller pupils throughout the transition from primary to secondary school. This is part of a set of examples showcasing good practice in raising the achievement and attendance of Gypsy, Roma and Traveller pupils, as well as reducing their exclusion rates.
