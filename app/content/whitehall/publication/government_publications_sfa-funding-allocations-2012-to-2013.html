Details

The information sets out the total amount of funding that has been paid and compares this to what was delivered.

The information includes:

  • Adult Skills Budget, including Additional Learner Support and Discretionary Learner Support
  • Community Learning
  • 16-18 Apprenticeships

In 2012/13 all providers delivering the Adult Skills provision were funded for their delivery up to the value of their allocation. The Agency operated performance management arrangements to increase allocations during the year to meet the demand from employers for high quality Apprenticeships.
