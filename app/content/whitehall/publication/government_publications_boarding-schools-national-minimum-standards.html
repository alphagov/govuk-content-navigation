The national minimum standards that boarding schools must meet to safeguard and protect the children in their care.

Document

Boarding schools: national minimum standards

Ref: DFE-00120-2015 PDF , 305KB , 20 pages

Detail

This guidance is for:

  • school leaders
  • school staff

It applies to:

  • boarding schools maintained by the local authority
  • independent boarding schools

It contains a statement of national minimum standards for boarding schools that provide accommodation for children.

National minimum standards for residential special schools are also available.
