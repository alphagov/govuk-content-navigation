Detail

This data file contains provisional revenue funding allocations for the 2016 to 2017 academic year for each school, based on the January 2016 school census.

The file also contains final funding allocations for the 2015 to 2016 academic year.

Final funding allocations for academic year 2016 to 2017 will be calculated in July 2017 from the October 2016 and January 2017 school censuses. The funding calculation is explained in the conditions of grant document.

More guidance on UIFSM funding is available.
