Details

This publication holds the template documents for privately-financed Priority School Building Programme (PSBP) projects. They are based on standard forms from previous programmes and are published for information rather than consultation.

The project-specific documents for each batch will be based on these templates. The project-specific versions will be issued to pre-qualified bidders on each project.

The project templates include:

  • standard form project agreement
  • standard form project agreement comparison with BSF version
  • standard form project agreement comparison with consultation version
  • standard form payment mechanism
  • standard form payment mechanism comparison with BSF payment mechanism

PSBP service output specifications are available on GOV.UK.
