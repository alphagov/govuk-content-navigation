Details

This document is for inspection to 31 March 2017. For inspection from 1 April 2017 please use the social care common inspection framework (SCCIF) .

This document sets out Ofsted’s framework for inspecting residential provision for young people under 18 in further education colleges. It applies to all general further education colleges, sixth forms colleges and independent specialist colleges that we inspect. It does not apply to any colleges registered as care homes, where the residential provision is inspected by the Care Quality Commission.
