Detail

This guide contains information for local authorities to help them collect and submit their annual alternative provision ( AP ) census.

AP is education arranged for learners who, because of exclusion, illness or other reasons, would not otherwise receive suitable education.

This guide includes details on:

  • changes to the census since the 2014 collection
  • the scope of the census
  • preparation advice
  • what child-level data needs to be submitted
  • data protection
  • guidance on how to maintain census details throughout the year
