Details

These documents set out a summary of the results and feedback from the Skills Funding Agency’s college and training organisations survey.

The surveys asked for views and perceptions of the SFA in 3 key areas:

  • our role
  • the way we operate
  • the way we communicate with colleges and training organisations

We issued the survey to all colleges and training organisations with whom we hold a contract.
