Details

These scorecards show the progress each local authority in England has made in providing new primary school places between the academic year 2009 to 2010 and the academic year 2012 to 2013.

They also show local authorities’ plans to meet demand for new school places by September 2015, along with details of the quality and cost of new places created to date.

More recent data showing progress up to the academic year 2013 to 2014 is available on GOV.UK.
