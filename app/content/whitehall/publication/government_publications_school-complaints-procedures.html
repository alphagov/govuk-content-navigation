Best practice advice for school complaints procedures 2016

Ref: DFE-00001-2016 PDF , 442KB , 21 pages

Detail

This guidance is for:

  • school governing bodies
  • school leaders
  • school staff
  • local authorities
  • diocesan boards

It applies to schools and nurseries maintained by local authorities.

It gives best practice on creating and revising complaints procedures and how to deal with complaints.
