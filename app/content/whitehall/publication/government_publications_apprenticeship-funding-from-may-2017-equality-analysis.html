Equality analysis: apprenticeship funding policy from May 2017

Ref: DFE-00262-2016 PDF , 587KB , 28 pages

Detail

An assessment of the effect that the new apprenticeship funding system will have on groups with certain ‘protected characteristics’.

The protected characteristics include:

  • disability
  • gender reassignment
  • pregnancy and maternity
  • race
  • religion or belief
  • sex and sexual orientation
  • age
  • gender re-assignment

Read the ‘Apprenticeship funding from May 2017’ policy paper if you want more information.
