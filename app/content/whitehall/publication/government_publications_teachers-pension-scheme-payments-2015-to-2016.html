Teachers’ pension scheme payments: technical note

PDF , 91.4KB , 1 page

Details

Teachers’ pension scheme payments are routine daily cash transfers to Capita to fund the payments they make to pensioners and others on behalf of DfE .

We aim to publish the data for the previous month by the last working day of the current month.

The technical note explains what data we’ve included and why.
