Details

Relevant for all providers of education and skills training who need to make a funding claim return for:

  • adult education budget
  • adult apprenticeships
  • discretionary learner support and advanced learning loans bursary
  • community learning
  • community learning mental health research project

Submitting your funding claim on the Hub

Funding claims are submitted through the data collections tab on the Hub and must be completed within a 4 day window.

2016 to 2017 mid-year claim

The 2016 to 2017 mid-year claim form will be available in the HUB from 9am Tuesday 7 February for four days and will close at 4pm Friday 10 February. The form will be pre-populated with provider’s earnings following the R06 data collection return. The provider must then add their forecasts for the remainder of the 2016 to 2017 funding year.

Providers will not be able to complete the claim form after the deadline of 4pm on 10 February.
