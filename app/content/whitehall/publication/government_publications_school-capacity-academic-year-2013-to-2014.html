Detail

The annual school capacity survey 2014 provides information for May 2014 on the numbers of:

  • primary school places
  • secondary school places
  • unfilled school places
  • pupils in excess of school capacity

It also provides pupil number forecasts to:

  • 2018 to 2019 academic year at primary level
  • 2020 to 2021 academic year at secondary level

Data is provided at national and local authority level for schools as deemed (therefore figures for middle deemed primary schools are included within the figures for primary schools and middle deemed secondary schools are included within the figures for secondary schools).
