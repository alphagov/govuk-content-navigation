Details

This budget planning tool is for:

  • groups proposing to open a special free school for children with special educational needs or disabilities
  • open special free schools

It gives an indicative funding allocation based on the size, phase and location of the school.

If you applying to open a special free school, you should also complete the application forms .

You can also read the special free schools revenue funding guide.
