Details

Information to help pupil referral units ( PRUs ) find out about future changes, as well as identify upcoming activities that may benefit students.

We’ve tried to be as precise as possible but some of the dates may change.

A timeline to help PRUs plan for the legal requirements they must meet is also available.

PRUs are places that provide education for children who can’t go to a mainstream school.
