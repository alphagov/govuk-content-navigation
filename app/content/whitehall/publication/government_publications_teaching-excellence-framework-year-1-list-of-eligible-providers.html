Detail

This list details both publicly-funded and private providers of higher education who meet the eligibility requirements for participation in year 1 of the TEF .

The eligibility requirements are set out in the government’s white paper ‘ Success as a knowledge economy: teaching excellence, social mobility and student choice ’.
