Details

These documents set out rules and regulations for all GCSEs (9 to 1).

Read the associated guidance to understand how to comply with these rules.

You should also read the relevant subject-level conditions and our requirements for all qualifications.

February 2017 update

Our standard setting Condition and requirements came into force on 16 February 2017. This followed our consultation on Requirements on setting GCSE (9 to 1) grade boundaries .

We have also published our Requirements for key dates which come into force on 1 May 2017. These are requirements which an awarding organisation must comply with from that date, when it specifies deadlines for requesting reviews of marking, reviews of moderation and appeals. For more information see our announcement .
