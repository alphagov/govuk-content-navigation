Details

Children and Families Minister Edward Timpson writes to Slough borough council, with an accompanying direction. The direction confirms the appointment of a commissioner to oversee improvement and establish a trust.

This follows an improvement notice issued by the Secretary of State to Slough borough council in July 2011 following an Ofsted inspection which rated arrangements for the protection of children ‘inadequate’.

After a second Ofsted judgement of ‘inadequate’ in February 2014, an independent review of Slough children’s social care services recommended transferring them to an independent organisation.
