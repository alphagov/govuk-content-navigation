Details

This lists ESF directly-funded contracts that the SFA procures on behalf of the Local Enterprise Partnerships.

The information will be of interest to the further education sector, the ESF Managing Authority, other beneficiaries of ESF, the European Commission and other Member States of the European Union.

The file is published in accordance with EU Regulation 1303/2013. This is why the headings of the data fields are in another official language of the Union, so the secondary set of row headings is in Polish.
