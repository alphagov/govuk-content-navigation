Details

This document sets out the funding rules for apprenticeship standards that start between 1 August 2016 and 31 July 2017.

The rules form part of the terms and conditions of the funding agreement between the Skills Funding Agency (SFA), and providers and employers who receive funding directly from the SFA for apprenticeship standard provision. This includes both those involved in the training and the assessment of apprentices.

This document is supported by, and must be read in conjunction with, the following publications.

  • Skills Funding Agency common funding rules
  • apprenticeship common funding rules
  • funding rules for higher educational institutions 2016 to 2017
  • 2016 to 2017 ILR guidance
  • SFA funding rules 2016 to 2017: summary of changes
