Detail

RAISEonline allows you to look in detail at pupil attainment and progress. This level of analysis is available to schools and non-school users and does not require data management. A second data set - School’s Own Data - can be edited by the school’s RAISEonline administrator using data management. Data management allows a school to make use of a wider range of reporting and analysis features in RAISEonline.
