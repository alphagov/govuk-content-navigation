Details

This annex to Michael Gove’s written ministerial statement sets out the government’s detailed response to the 23rd report of the School Teachers’ Review Body published on 13 February 2014, accepting the recommendations in full.

The Secretary of State invites comments from the statutory consultees on the STRB report and his response to the recommendations by 13 March 2014.
