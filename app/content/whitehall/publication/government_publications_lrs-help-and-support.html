General enquiries

  • Email: lrssupport@sfa.bis.gov.uk

  • Telephone: 0345 602 2589

Important Information

From the 1 January 2017, the Naming and Approvals Committee (NAC) intends to remove any link to the MIAP domain. Web Service Users, organisations who access LRS via their own MI system, will see an error if the organisation is still using the old domain. Please take immediate action and replace the MIAP domain with a non-MIAP URL. For more information go to LRS : replacement of the MIAP domain (August 2016) .

Service desk opening hours:

08:00 - 18:00 Monday to Friday
08:00 - 18:00 Christmas Eve
08:00 - 13:00 New Year’s Eve

The service desk is closed on Saturday, Sunday and on English public holidays (inc Christmas and New Year’s Day).

If you have any queries out of hours please email us lrssupport@sfa.bis.gov.uk

Website enquiries

Contact the communications team if you wish to:

  • notify us of a correction required on the website
  • have a suggestion
  • raise a query about this website
  • request a copy of the LRS logos
  • form part of a user group for testing improvements to the LRS organisation portal

Please ensure you pay the correct postal charges when sending documents to the Learning Records Service.

The price of postage depends on:

  • size and weight of your item
  • when you want your item to arrive
  • if you wish to track your item or get confirmation delivery

We will reject and return post if the correct postal charge(s) is not paid.

Information requests

The LRS is part of the Skills Funding Agency and the information it holds can be requested from the main SFA page under the Freedom of Information Act 2000.

Please use the form to submit any questions about the service to the customer helpdesk. They will respond to your query within five working days.
