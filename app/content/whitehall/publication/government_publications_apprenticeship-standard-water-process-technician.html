Detail

This document is relevant to:

  • those looking to apply for an apprenticeship
  • employers wanting to recruit an apprentice
  • training organisations working with employers to make the apprenticeships available

This standard is ready to use

Under our reforms, employer-designed standards will replace frameworks, and will be clear and concise. From 2017 to 2018 as many apprenticeship starts as possible will be on the new standards.

Employers involved in creating this standard

If you’d like to get involved and contribute to the development of the water process technician standard, please read the guidance for trailblazers , and email the trailblazer contacts: Lisa.lingard@euskills.co.uk or Jacqueline.Hall@euskills.co.uk .

  • Viridor
  • Welsh Water with the Institution of Engineering and Technology
