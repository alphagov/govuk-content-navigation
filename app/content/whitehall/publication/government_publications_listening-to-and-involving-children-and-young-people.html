Detail

This statutory guidance is for:

  • headteachers
  • school leaders
  • local authorities

It applies to local-authority-maintained schools.

The legislation is underpinned by the general principles of the United Nations Conventions on the Rights of the Child ( UNCRC ) .

This is a minor update of the April 2012 guidance.
