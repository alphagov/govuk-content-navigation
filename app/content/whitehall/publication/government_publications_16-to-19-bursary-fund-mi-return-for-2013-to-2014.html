Details

The 16 to 19 Bursary Fund MI return form is concise and straight forward to complete. The information provided by institutions tells the EFA how they used 16 to 19 Bursary funding during the 2013 to 2014 academic year; and could help shape future policy developments.

The 16 to 19 Bursary Fund: MI Return for 2013 to 2014 document leads institutions to the location of the form and how to get started with the return. This guide provides an overview of the form content and functionality; explaining how users will be able to plot their way successfully through the form. It includes more detailed information on the specific questions asked; and highlights some issues to be avoided.
