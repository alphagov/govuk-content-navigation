Details

Ofsted gathers the views of boarders or residential pupils on the boarding provision at their school by sending a survey by email to the school at a specific point in time during the inspection year. The school is asked to distribute the survey to the boarders or residential pupils.

Ofsted have adapted the survey into Makaton, Widgit and Picture Communication Symbols for pupils who may need them. There are 2 sets of these, 1 omitting the question about food. For guidance on how Ofsted uses the responses to the surveys, please refer to:

  • Inspecting boarding and residential provision in schools - guidance for inspectors
  • Inspecting boarding and residential provision in schools - guidance for schools
