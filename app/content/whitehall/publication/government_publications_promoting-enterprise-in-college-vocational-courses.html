Detail

This Ofsted survey report looks at how 15 high-performing colleges have successfully promoted enterprise-related skills.

The promotion of enterprise culture as a means of stimulating economic growth and improving economic prosperity is a key focus of government and industry. The further education sector has a major role in helping foster an enterprise culture.
