Detail

Primary schools that are converting to academy status can apply for this grant if they’re:

  • planning to set up a multi-academy trust ( MAT ) made up of at least 3 schools

  • part of a group of 2 or more primary schools planning to join an existing MAT at the same time

Primary schools that are converting to academy status and have fewer than 210 pupils can also apply for the small school supplement grant .
