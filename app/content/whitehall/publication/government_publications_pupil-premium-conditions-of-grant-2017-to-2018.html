The pupil premium is additional funding for publicly funded schools in England to raise the attainment of disadvantaged pupils. More guidance is available on the pupil premium , including how schools should spend it and how we hold them accountable.

The ‘Pupil premium 2017 to 2018: conditions of grant’ shows the conditions that academies and local authorities must meet to receive the pupil premium.

Watch a short video on pupil premium funding for answers to some of the most common questions about pupil premium funding from academies and free schools.
