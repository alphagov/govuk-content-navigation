This good practice example shows how Cranford Community College uses partnership links with the feeder primary school and the local Traveller site to support Gypsy, Roma and Traveller pupils. This is part of a set of examples showcasing good practice in raising the achievement and attendance of Gypsy, Roma and Traveller pupils, as well as reducing their exclusion rates.
