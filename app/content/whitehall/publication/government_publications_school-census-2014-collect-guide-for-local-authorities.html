School census 2014: COLLECT guide for local authorities

Ref: DFE-00369-2014 PDF , 2.23MB , 33 pages

Details

This document is designed to help local authority users complete and submit their school census 2014 returns using COLLECT (Collections Online - Learners, Education, Children and Teachers), the department’s centralised data collection and management system.

The guide includes information on:

  • accessing COLLECT
  • uploading and submitting census data
  • error queries
