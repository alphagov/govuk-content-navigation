Detail

Summary of the FE Commissioner David Collins’ findings and recommendations following his intervention visit to City of Liverpool College.

The May 2016 letter from Minister for Skills Nick Boles sets out the outcome of the intervention assessment and confirms next steps.

The October 2016 letter from Minister of State for Apprenticeships and Skills Robert Halfon explains the government’s view of the college’s position.

The intervention report is in line with our commitments in ‘Intervention in further education – the strengthened intervention process’ .
