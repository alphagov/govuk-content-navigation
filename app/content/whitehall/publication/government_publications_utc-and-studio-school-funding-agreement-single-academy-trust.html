Details

The funding agreement provides the framework for your university technical college ( UTC ) or studio school to operate in.

If your UTC or studio school is going to have a single academy trust, you should use the documents on this page to draw up your funding agreement.

You’ll need to complete your articles of association alongside your funding agreement.

More guidance on opening a UTC and opening a studio school is available.
