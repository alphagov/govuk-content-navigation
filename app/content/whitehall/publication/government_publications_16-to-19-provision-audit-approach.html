Details

This publication sets out the audit approach for 16 to 19 provision funded by the Education Funding Agency ( EFA ).

The audit approach applies directly to charitable and commercial providers ( CCPs ). The principles are also relevant to specialist post-16 institutions ( SPIs ), academies, local authority schools and college providers.
