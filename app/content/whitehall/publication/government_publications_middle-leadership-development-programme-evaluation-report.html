Detail

This report on the Middle Leadership Development programme (MLDP) summarises:

  • what the programme was
  • how it was evaluated
  • what impact it had
  • important learnings

The National Professional Qualification for Middle Leadership (NPQML) has replaced MLDP. The findings in this report will be useful to NPQML providers and there are ideas for best practice in leadership development for clusters and alliances of schools.
