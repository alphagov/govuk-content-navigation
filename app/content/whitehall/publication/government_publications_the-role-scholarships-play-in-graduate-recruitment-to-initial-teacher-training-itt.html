The role scholarships play in graduate recruitment for initial teacher training (ITT)

Ref: ISBN 978-1-78105-426-0 , DFE-RR383 PDF , 401KB , 39 pages

Details

This research was conducted by the National College for Teaching and Leadership in the summer of 2014.

The professional bodies for chemistry, mathematics, science and computing sent a survey to all scholarship applicants. The findings suggest that:

  • availability and interest of scholarships can turn an interest in teaching into an application for teacher training
  • for some the availability of scholarships is a hook into application for teacher training
  • some respondents cited the additional benefits offered by professional bodies as the reason for applying for a scholarship

The report provides evidence that scholarships are important in attracting applicants for teacher training.
