Details

Assurance review documentation for the funding year 2015 to 2016

This zip file contains documentation that the SFA’s provider financial management and assurance team and other appointed auditors will use to audit the adult skills budget, 16 to 18 provision (apprenticeships and traineeships) and EFA funded 16 to 19 provision for a sample of providers and to give their opinion on whether they comply with the funding rules. It includes an overview of the assurance approach and guidance notes for PDSAT users on how to review PDSAT report output.

Selecting colleges for funding assurance reviews 2012 to 2013

Sets out proposals for how the Skills Funding Agency will select colleges for funding assurance reviews of their 2012 to 2013 data. It will be of interest to all colleges.

16 to 19 and adult skills budget final claims and 16 to 18 apprenticeships assurance review documentation 2012 to 2013

Documentation that will be used by the Agency’s appointed funding auditors to audit a sample of providers’ funding claims and to give their opinion on whether the providers’ 16 to 19 and adult skills budget and 16 to 18 apprenticeships funding claims comply with funding requirements.

An overview of the audit process is included within the E1 Assurance Overview and Planning Guidance document.

Adult skills budget funding assurance review documentation

Documentation that will be used by the Agency’s auditors to audit a sample of providers’ adult skills budget provision and to give their opinion on whether this provision complies with the funding requirements.
