Details

This guide explains what reports are available to local authorities, using children in need census data submitted through the Department for Education’s centralised data collection and management system COLLECT (Collections Online - Learners, Education, Children and Teachers).

It specifies:

  • a detailed description of each report
  • what data is used to calculate each report
  • what information each report displays
