Detail

This publication gives information about how to complain about post-16 institutions funded by Education Funding Agency ( EFA ). It covers complaints about

  • sixth-form colleges
  • commercial and charitable training providers where the complaint relates to EFA funded provision for students aged 16 to 18 and up to 25 who are subject to a learning disability assessment

For other complaints about

  • academies and free schools, please see the guidance about making a complaint about an academy or free school
  • general further education colleges, commercial and charitable training providers where the complaint relates to Skills Funding Agency provision for post-19 students, and apprenticeship programmes, please contact the Skills Funding Agency
  • schools (including non-maintained special schools), please see the guidance about making a complaint about a school
