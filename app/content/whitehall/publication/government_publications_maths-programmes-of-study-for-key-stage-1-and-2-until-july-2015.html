Details

These are the statutory programmes of study and attainment targets for mathematics at key stage 1 and 2.

They must be taught to certain year groups in all maintained schools in England until July 2015.

In the 2014 to 2015 academic year:

  • pupils in years 1, 3, 4 and 5 must study the new national curriculum mathematics programmes of study
  • pupils in years 2 and 6 will continue to use these programmes of study for mathematics
  • pupils in years 2 and 6 will take the current key stage 1 and 2 tests respectively

From September 2015 all pupils will study the new national curriculum mathematics programmes of study .

New key stage 1 and 2 tests will be introduced in July 2016.
