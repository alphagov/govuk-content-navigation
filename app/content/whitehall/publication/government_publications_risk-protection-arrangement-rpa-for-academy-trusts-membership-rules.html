Details

These documents describe the risk protection arrangement ( RPA ) rules for academy trusts, which members must follow.

There is a version for church academies.

Further information about RPA is available on the ‘ Academies risk protection arrangement ’ guidance page.

The RPA is an alternative to commercial insurance for academy trusts. Under the RPA , the UK government covers losses instead of commercial insurance.
