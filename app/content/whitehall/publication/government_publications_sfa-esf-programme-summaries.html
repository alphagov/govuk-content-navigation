Details

We award contracts to training providers to deliver training and support through ESF, instead of making annual allocations (like we do for the Adult Skills Budget). We award these contracts to providers that are successful in an open and competitive tendering procurement exercise. The contracts are valid for a specific length of time, normally more than one year.

These lists supplement our funding allocations for providers .
