Details

Ofsted inspected the Isle of Wight’s child protection services on 26 November 2012. The resulting report , published on 15 January 2013, judged the council as ‘inadequate’ overall. In response, ministers issued a statutory direction on 6 June 2013 which includes a requirement for the council to establish a strategic partnership with Hampshire County council.

An update to the direction issued on 28 November 2016 reflects the agreed position between the Isle of Wight and Hampshire councils.
