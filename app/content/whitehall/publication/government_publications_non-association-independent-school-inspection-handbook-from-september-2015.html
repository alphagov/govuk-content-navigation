Details

This handbook describes the main activities Ofsted inspectors undertake during inspections of non-association independent schools. It sets out the evaluation criteria that inspectors use to make their judgements and on which they report.

It is to be used in inspections carried out under the ‘Common inspection framework: education, skills and early years’ .
