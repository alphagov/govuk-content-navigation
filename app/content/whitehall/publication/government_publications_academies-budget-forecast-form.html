Details

The documents on this page are for use by those academy trusts that need to complete their 2016 to 2017 budget forecast return by 31 July 2016. You will need to use the online form and the guide on this page.

We have also published an Excel workbook and guidance to help you prepare for the new online form. The workbook allows you to see what information is required so you can prepare your forecast before you complete the form. It is also available in an open document format for those who do not have access to Excel.

Remaining 2015 to 2016 budget forecasts

Academy trusts whose 2015 to 2016 budget forecasts remain due will also need to complete the new online form using the guide on this page.
