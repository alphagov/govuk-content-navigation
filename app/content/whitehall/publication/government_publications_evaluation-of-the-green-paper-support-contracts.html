Details

Green paper support contracts were commissioned by the Department for Education to support the pathfinder teams working on reforms to the special educational needs and disability ( SEND ) system.

SEND reform proposals were trialled in 20 SEND pathfinder sites covering 31 local authority areas. Pathfinder activity included:

  • the development of an improved assessment process
  • an education, health and care plan ( EHCP ) for use with children and young people from birth to 25 years old

It also aimed to improve:

  • engagement with the voluntary and community sector and families and young people
  • multi-agency working
