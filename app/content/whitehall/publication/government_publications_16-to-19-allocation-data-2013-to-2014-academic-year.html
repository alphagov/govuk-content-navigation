Colleges, providers, schools and academies receive annual funding allocations from the Education Funding Agency (EFA).

This spreadsheet tells you how funds were allocated for 16 to 19 education in the 2013 to 2014 academic year. There is also a document explaining the spreadsheet and how to use it.
