Capital transactions: sixth-form colleges

Ref: EFA-00146-2013 PDF , 284KB , 13 pages

Detail

This statutory guidance is for sixth-form colleges and refers to the following legislation:

  • Further and Higher Education Act 1992
  • Charity Law

It also explains the relevant responsibilities of the Education Funding Agency (EFA). It should be read in conjunction with:

  • the EFA funding agreement
  • Rigour and Responsiveness in Skills

This guidance is scheduled for review in 2015.
