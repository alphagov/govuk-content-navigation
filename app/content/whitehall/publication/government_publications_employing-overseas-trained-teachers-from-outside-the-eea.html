Details

This is for:

  • school staff
  • school leaders
  • governing bodies
  • local authorities

It applies to:

  • local-authority-maintained schools
  • pupil referral units
  • non-maintained special schools

This guidance explains the rules about employing overseas-trained teachers to teach in schools in England.

Overseas-trained teachers are people who have qualified as teachers in a country outside of the European Economic Area ( EEA ) and Switzerland.

Pupil referral units provide education for vulnerable children unable to attend a mainstream school.
