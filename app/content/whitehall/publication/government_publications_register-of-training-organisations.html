Detail

To be eligible to compete for funding, organisations must complete our market entry pre-qualification process. This includes completing our due diligence questions and capacity and capability questions. Entry on the register does not automatically entitle an organisation to funding, but it means that they will be eligible to be invited to compete for future funding opportunities.
