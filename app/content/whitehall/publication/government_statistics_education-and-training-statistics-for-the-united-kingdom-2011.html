This is the 15th edition of education and training statistics for the United Kingdom. It provides statistics relating to education and training in the UK and includes chapters and annexes relating to:

  • schools
  • post-compulsory education
  • training and qualifications
  • destinations
  • education expenditure
  • UK population numbers

The 2010 edition was published on 31 March 2011.

Tables 1.9, 2.2 and TS2 were updated on 23 February 2012 following updates received from data suppliers.
