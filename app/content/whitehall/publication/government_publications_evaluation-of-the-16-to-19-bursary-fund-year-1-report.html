Details

The 16 to 19 Bursary Fund was introduced in September 2011 and provides financial support to young people who face significant financial barriers to participation in education or training post-16.

These reports provide annual evaluation of the effectiveness of the fund in encouraging young people to remain within post-16 education or training.
