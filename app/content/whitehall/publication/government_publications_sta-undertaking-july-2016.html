Safety Training Awards (STA) Assurance that qualifications comply with the Conditions Undertaking accepted Closed 7 November 2016

On 26 July 2016, STA, gave Ofqual an undertaking which sets out the steps it will take to secure compliance with our ‘General Conditions of Recognition’, specifically:

  • E5.1 – Assurance that qualifications comply with the Conditions

STA has undertaken to carry out an audit of its regulated qualifications to ensure that they all comply with its conditions of recognition. It will devise and implement a system for qualification development and review, and for its new qualifications to be formally signed off before being submitted to the register of regulated qualifications.

In line with general condition B8, STA must now take all the steps identified in the undertaking. We may take further action if it doesn’t follow through on its commitments.
