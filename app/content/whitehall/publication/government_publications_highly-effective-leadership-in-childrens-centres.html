Details

The National College for School Leadership commissioned this research to look at the nature of effective leadership in children’s centres and the development of system leadership.

The study included:

  • a review of research and policy
  • case studies of 25 centre leaders
  • practitioner workshops

The report includes the:

  • main challenges for children’s centre leaders
  • core behaviours of highly effective leaders and system leaders
  • important messages for policy and practice
