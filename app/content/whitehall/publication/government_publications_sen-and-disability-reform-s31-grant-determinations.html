Details

Details of the amount we have allocated each local authority to implement the SEND reforms. We have allocated this under section 31 (S31) of the Local Government Act 2003. We have included this information, together with details of how to use it, in the ‘S31 grant determination letter for the SEND preparation for employment grant 2014 to 2015’.

We have also included details of funding for the 9 local authorities acting as regional leads for the reforms in the 2015 to 2016 financial year. These details are in the ‘S31 grant determination letter for the role of regional lead for the SEND reforms in 2015 to 2016’.

More information about preparation for employment and supported internships is available on the Preparing for Adulthood website .
