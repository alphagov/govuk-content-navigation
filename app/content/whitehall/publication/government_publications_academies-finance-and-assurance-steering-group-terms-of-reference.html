Details

The academies finance and assurance steering group enables the Education Funding Agency ( EFA ) and academies to develop finance and assurance publications for academies together. The steering group includes representatives from different types of academies and academy representative bodies.

The steering group has 3 working groups to bring together academy finance directors, business managers and EFA staff. The working groups are chaired by experienced practitioners working in academies and develop the detailed content of finance and assurance documents.

You can find the steering group’s terms of reference on this page. The minutes from the group’s meetings are in the academies finance and assurance steering group collection .
