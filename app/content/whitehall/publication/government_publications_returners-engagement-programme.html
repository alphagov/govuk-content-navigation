Details

The application round is open until 20 February 2017 at 5pm.

Please use the attached application form to apply.

The National College for Teaching and Leadership would like to invite school partnerships to become a Returners Engagement Programme pilot school. This programme is open to the following in the North West and South East:

  • School Direct lead schools
  • higher education institutions (HEIs)
  • multi-academy trusts (MATs)
  • teaching school alliances (TSAs)
  • school-centred initial teacher training providers (SCITTs)

The programme is available to former teachers wishing to return to mathematics, physics, or a modern foreign language in the North West and South East.
