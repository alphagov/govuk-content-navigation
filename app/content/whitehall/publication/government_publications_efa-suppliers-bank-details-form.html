Details

The Education Funding Agency’s ( EFA ) and Skills Funding Agency’s ( SFA ) suppliers should complete this form to notify Funding Agencies Shared Services Team (FAS2T) of new bank details or changes to their bank details and email it to FASST.standingdata@education.gov.uk .

Academies should not complete this form, but should instead complete the BAN1a form .

The form is available in open document format as well as in Excel.

Bank details change requests are subject to a verification process that may take 15 working days.

EFA and SFA standing data amendments

Email FASST.standingdata@education.gov.uk

For notifying the Education Funding Agency ( EFA ) and/or skills Funding Agency ( SFA ) of any new bank details or changes to existing bank details.
