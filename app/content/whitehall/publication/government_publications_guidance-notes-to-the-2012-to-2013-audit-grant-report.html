This is guidance for initial teacher training (ITT) providers when completing the annual grant report.

Documents

2012 to 2013 audit grant report guidance notes

Ref: NCTL-00211-2013 PDF , 858KB , 18 pages

Details

All initial teacher training providers must submit independently audited accounts each year, this includes an audit grant report.

An accounting officer contact must be set up, and they should submit the completed and signed report to their auditors.

Accounts guidance notes are also available from this website.
