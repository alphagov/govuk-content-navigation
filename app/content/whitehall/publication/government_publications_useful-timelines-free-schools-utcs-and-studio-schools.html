Information to help free schools, university technical colleges ( UTCs ) and studio schools find out about future changes, as well as identify upcoming activities that may benefit students.

We’ve tried to be as precise as possible but some of the dates may change.

A timeline to help free schools plan for the legal requirements they must meet is also available.
