Details

As part of the independent training providers adult education budget procurement exercise, announced on Monday 23 January 2017, we have published a draft of the Adult Education Budget funding and performance management rules 2017 to 2018

This document sets out the draft rules that will apply to all adult education budget funded provision for the 2017 to 2018 funding year.

If you have feedback on how we can make the funding rules clearer or better understood then please email AEBrulesfeedback@sfa.bis.gov.uk by 24 February 2017.
