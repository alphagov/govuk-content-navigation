Detail

The technical and business specification describes the alternative provision census 2015 . It will allow local authorities and those working on their behalf, such as software suppliers, to prepare the necessary data and processes.

The documents cover the expected census return to the Department for Education and the validation rules which will be applied to the data submitted.
