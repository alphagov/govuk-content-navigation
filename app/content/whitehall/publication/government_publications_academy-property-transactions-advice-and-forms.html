Details

These property information notes (PINs) and forms are for academies to use when conducting property transactions. EFA is developing online forms for property transactions. The first online form is for academies seeking permission to buy land or a building or to take on a lease of land or a building. This replaces PIN C and form C. Other online forms will be available later in 2015.

Which form to use

Academies seeking permission to buy land or a building or to take on a lease of land or a building should refer to property information note 2 and use academy property transactions form 2.
