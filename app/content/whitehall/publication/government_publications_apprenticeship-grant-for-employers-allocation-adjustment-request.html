Details

The apprenticeship frameworks funding and AGE performance-management rules describes the process to request an increase to your Apprenticeship Grant for Employers (AGE) allocation for the 2017 to 2018 financial year (for starts 1 January to 30 April 2017).

You must request an increase if you are likely to exceed your allocation. You can also use the form to request a new allocation for the 2017 to 2018 financial year if you have not previously been allocated one, but you must already have a contract with us for 16 to 18 apprenticeships or adult skills budget funding.The form will be available until 27 April 2017.

Version 3 of the apprenticeship frameworks funding and AGE performance-management rules gave a deadline date for requesting growth for the 2016 to 2017 financial year as 16 December 2016.

The opportunity to request growth has been extended to 27 April 2017.

Growth requests are subject to budget availability. If you have additional queries, please contact your Business Operations Provider Manager.

You can find more information about the Apprenticeship Grant for Employers on GOV.UK.
