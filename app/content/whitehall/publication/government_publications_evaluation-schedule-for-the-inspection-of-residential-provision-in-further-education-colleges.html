Detail

The evaluation schedule describes the evidence that Ofsted inspectors will consider when inspecting residential provision for learners – aged under 18 – in further education colleges and sixth form colleges. The document sets out the grade descriptors inspectors will use to make their principal judgements from January 2015.

The evaluation schedule should be used with the framework and inspection handbook
