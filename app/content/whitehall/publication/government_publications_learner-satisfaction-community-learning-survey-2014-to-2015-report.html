Details

This report provides a summary of the findings for the 2014 to 2015 community learning learner satisfaction survey.

The survey ran from 3 November 2014 to 29 March 2015. Learners from a total of 185 colleges and other training organisations took part in the survey, with most completing the questionnaire online.

This report was undertaken by Ipsos MORI on behalf of the Skills Funding Agency.
