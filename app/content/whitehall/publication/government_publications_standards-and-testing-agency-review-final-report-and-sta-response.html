Detail

In April 2016, Minister Nick Gibb announced a ‘root and branch’ review of the operations of the Standards and Testing Agency, following security breaches during the administration of the 2016 key stage 1 and key stage 2 national curriculum tests. The review took place between July and September 2016. The final report summarises the review’s key findings and recommendations and STA’s response outlines what the Agency is doing in order to address those findings.
