Details

This research looks at university technical colleges ( UTCs ), studio schools and free schools. It looks at a sample of these school types, established in 2011 and 2012.

The research looks specifically at:

  • how these schools recruit and select their leaders
  • the skills and experience of the school leaders
  • the specific challenges involved in establishing new schools
  • how these schools operate to achieve success
  • the support that benefits these schools the most
