Details

This publication includes data on:

  • the number of statements of SEN and EHC plans in place in January 2016
  • the number of statements and plans made for the first time during 2015
  • the percentage of statements and plans that were completed on time
  • transfers from statements and learning difficulty assessments to EHC plans
