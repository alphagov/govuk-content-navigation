Detail

Detail on KS4 destination measures data in the school and college performance tables for pupils who finished key stage 4 in the 2013 to 2014 academic year, including:

  • the history of destination measures
  • sources of the data
  • how we calculate the measures
  • the categories of destinations

The destination measures show the success of schools in helping all their pupils take the best qualifications for them.
