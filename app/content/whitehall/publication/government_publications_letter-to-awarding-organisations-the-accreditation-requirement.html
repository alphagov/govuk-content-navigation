Details

This letter sent out to responsible officers in awarding organisations explains Ofqual’s proposal to lift the general accreditation requirement and to re-impose an accreditation requirement for GCSEs, AS and A levels.

Having considered the responses to the consultation, it was decided to proceed as proposed and the general accreditation requirement was lifted at midday on 3 November 2014. An accreditation requirement was immediately re-imposed for GCSEs, AS and A levels.
