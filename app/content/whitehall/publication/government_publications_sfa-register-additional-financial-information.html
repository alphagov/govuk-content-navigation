Detail

The Skills Funding Agency assesses a providerâ€™s financial health to understand the degree of risk in contracting with these organisations.

This sets out supplementary documentation an organisation can submit as business plan information, if they have previously failed their financial health assessment on the basis of their financial statements, and new financial statements are unavailable at the point of reapplying to the register of training organisations.

All providers and prospective providers included on the register of training organisations are subject to an assessment of their financial health .
