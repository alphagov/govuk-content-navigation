Details

A policy statement to explain the government amendment to the Children and Social Work Bill to introduce statutory:

  • relationships education in primary schools
  • relationships and sex education in secondary schools
  • PSHE in all schools

Statutory guidance sets out what schools and local authorities must do to comply with the law. You should follow the guidance unless you have a very good reason not to.
