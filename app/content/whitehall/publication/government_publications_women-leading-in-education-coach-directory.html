Details

Leadership coaching pledge is one of the measures announced by government to support women in teaching.

Coaching is at no cost to participants.

This directory lists the details of coaches that have volunteered. Please use it to identify a coach. You can search by locality and school phase. We’ll update the directory regularly.

All coaches have volunteered their time and have specified their availability in the directory.

If you’re interested in becoming a coach read Women Leading in Education: become a coach .

If you would like to find out more about receiving coaching read Women Leading in Education: get leadership coaching .
