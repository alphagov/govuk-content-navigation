Detail

This report provides a summary of the findings for the 2015 to 2016 community learning learner satisfaction survey.

The survey ran from 2 November 2015 to 1 April 2016. Learners from a total of 177 colleges and other training organisations took part in the survey, with most completing the questionnaire online.

This report was undertaken by Ipsos MORI on behalf of the Skills Funding Agency.
