Mainstream free schools: assessing the need for a new school in a local area

Ref: DFE-00092-2015 PDF , 197KB , 7 pages

Details

This document is for mainstream schools, which are defined as:

  • primary
  • secondary
  • all-through providers

It excludes:

  • 16 to 19 academies
  • alternative providers
  • special free schools

It sets out the criteria used to assess the need for a free school in a local area.
