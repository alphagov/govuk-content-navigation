This good practice example shows how Herringthorpe Infant School has used enterprise throughout its curriculum. The headteacher says, ‘working in this structured and entrepreneurial manner has supported a transformation of teaching and learning.’ The school’s policy is that every child experiences an enterprise activity every term.
