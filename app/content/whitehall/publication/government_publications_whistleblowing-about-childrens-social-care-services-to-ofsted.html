Detail

Ofsted’s previous whistleblowing policy only covered safeguarding in local authority children’s services.

This policy covers all matters relating to the inspection and regulation of children’s social care:

  • establishments
  • agencies
  • providers

See also ‘Whistleblowing about safeguarding in local authority children’s services’.

Further education and skills
