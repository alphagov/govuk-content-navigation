Detail

This statistical first release ( SFR ) sets out the proportion of academic and vocational courses completed by 16- to 19-year-olds at school and academy sixth forms.

The qualifications it covers include:

  • A levels
  • AS levels
  • a range of other academic and vocational qualifications
