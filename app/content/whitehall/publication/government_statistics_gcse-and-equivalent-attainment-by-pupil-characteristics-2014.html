Detail

GCSE and equivalent pupil attainment in England. It is broken down by pupil characteristics including:

  • gender
  • ethnicity
  • eligibility for free school meals
  • disadvantage
  • special educational needs ( SEN )
  • English as a first language

Read the ‘ Measuring disadvantaged pupils’ attainment gaps over time ’ statistical working paper for information about our proposed methodology for measuring attainment gaps for disadvantaged pupils.
