The operational and good practice guide provides local authority officers and school forum elected members with advice and information about the operation of schools forums.

There are also quick guides to schools forums structure and schools forums powers and responsibilities 2015 to 2016.

The self-assessment toolkit provides local authority officers and elected members with a framework for assessing the strengths and weaknesses of their schools forum.
