Lord Nash, Parliamentary Under Secretary of State for Schools, wrote to the Al-Madinah Education Trust on 8 October.

Details

The Department for Education has found that the Al-Madinah Education Trust has breached a number of the conditions of its funding agreement.

Lord Nash has written to the Chair of the School Trust asking for swift and comprehensive action to deal with these serious and unacceptable concerns. Unless these problems are addressed promptly and in full the school’s funding agreement will be terminated.
