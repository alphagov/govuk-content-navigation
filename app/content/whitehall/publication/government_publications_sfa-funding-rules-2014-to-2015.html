Funding rules 2014 to 2015 version 2 addendum 1

This addendum changes the eligibility of an individual, aged 19 to 24, to access a traineeship. Individuals will now be able, from 1 January 2015, to access a traineeship if they already have a full level 2 qualification.

Funding rules for Trailblazer apprenticeships (updated for version 2)

These documents set out the funding rules which apply to the new Trailblazer apprenticeship standards. In setting out the rules, we have provided additional context and explanation to help providers delivering training and assessment for the new Trailblazer apprenticeship standards.

These rules apply from 1 September 2014 to 31 July 2015 for all organisations in receipt of funding for the delivery of training or the assessment of Trailblazer apprenticeships for the funding year 2014 to 2015.
