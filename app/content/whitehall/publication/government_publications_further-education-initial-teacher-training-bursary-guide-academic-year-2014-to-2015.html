Details

This guide covers the implementation of further education ( FE ) initial teacher training bursaries. It is aimed at providers, but can be used as a source of reference for trainees.

The information on training bursary rates, qualifying subjects and associated degree classifications are for trainees starting in the academic year 2014 to 2015 only.

Mathematics trainees should also see: Mathematics subject knowledge enhancement (SKE) programme guide: academic year 2013 to 2014 .

A bursary claim form is provided.
