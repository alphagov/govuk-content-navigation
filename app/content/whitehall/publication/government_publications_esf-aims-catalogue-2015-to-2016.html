Details

The 2015 to 2016 ESF aims catalogue only contains regulated qualifications which we know at the point of update will be operationally available for delivery in the 2015 to 2016 funding year and have been approved for funding.

Providers should also check the availability of the qualification with the awarding organisation.

The information contained within this catalogue will be added to a new ESF learning aims search facility within the Hub in due course, whereupon publication of this catalogue will cease.

For further information or related queries, please email localresponsiveness@sfa.bis.gov.uk or your SFA Central Delivery Service.
