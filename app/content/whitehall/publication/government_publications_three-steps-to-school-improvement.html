Details

This example describes the three key aspects that have been crucial to improvement at Ecclesfield School: highly effective performance management for teachers and leaders; ensuring that governance is an integral aspect of the school’s improvement; and empowering students to have a strong influence on their own learning. This is one of 12 case studies about the schools featured in the report Getting to good, published by Ofsted in September 2012.
