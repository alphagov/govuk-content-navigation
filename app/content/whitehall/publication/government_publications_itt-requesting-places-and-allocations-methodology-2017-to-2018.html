Detail

The ITT allocation methodology sets out the characteristics and criteria that the National College for Teaching and Leadership (NCTL) have considered in the allocation of places for:

  • School Direct (salaried)
  • School Direct (tuition fee)
  • provider-led courses

This is for the academic year 2017 to 2018.

This information provides details for School Direct lead schools and ITT providers on the methodology we have used to determine allocations and the recruitment process.
