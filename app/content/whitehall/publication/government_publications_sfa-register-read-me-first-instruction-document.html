Detail

Organisations must complete our due diligence pre-qualification questions in order to be included on the register of training organisations.

Where organisations want to compete for contracts, they must also complete our capacity and capability pre-qualification questions.

Organisations must complete and pass our pre-qualification questions if:

  • they have an existing funding agreement with the SFA for delivering education and training
  • they are a subcontractor holding contracts with an aggregate value of Â£100,000 or above per annum

  • they want to be selected to be invited to tender for:

    • the delivery of education and training
    • the delivery of information advice and guidance which may include delivery for the National Careers Service
    • the development of services, which replaces the service developer framework

The pre-qualification questions can be accessed on the e-tendering portal when we are open for applications.
