Detail

This 2-year research project was led by the University of Nottingham on behalf of the National College for Teaching and Leadership.

The research method was based on:

  • in-depth case studies

  • a survey of teaching schools across cohorts 1 to 3

The report also draws on statistical data to look at programme reach and association with pupil performance.
