Detail

This is for:

  • schools inspectorates
  • independent school proprietors

It applies to independent schools.

This guidance explains:

  • how schools should draw up an action plan
  • what happens after submitting an action plan to DfE

Independent schools will receive an action plan notice if they are failing some of the independent school standards.
