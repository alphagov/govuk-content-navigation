Guide for schools and local authorities (LAs) to help them complete and return autumn 2014 to summer 2015 school census data.

Document

School census 2014 to 2015

Ref: DFE-00498-2014 PDF , 2.88MB , 129 pages

Detail

The guide includes details of:

  • what data schools and local authorities need to submit
  • new data items in the 2014 to 2015 school census
  • the process schools should follow to authorise the data they submit
  • the specification for pupil- and school-level codesets
  • important deadlines

More guidance on submitting school census data is available.
