Details

Allocation / contract values data 2016 to 2017 (March 2017)

The updated funding year contract values provide maximum contract value information as at March 2017 for colleges, training organisations and employers that have a contract for 2016 to 2017.

It excludes contract values where the Agency is finalising or discussing current delivery performance.

This updates the initial contract values published in October 2016.

Allocation/ contract values data 2016 to 2017 (September 2016)

The funding year contract values provide maximum contract value information as at September 2016 for colleges, training organisations and employers that have a contract for 2016 to 2017. It excludes contract values where these are not finalised.

These allocation/ contract values are subject to the performance management arrangements set out in the SFA funding rules 2016 to 2017 .

We will update this publication throughout the year, following the performance management points and final claim submissions.
