Details

An Ofsted multi-remit focused inspection took place in Somerset County Council between 8 September 2014 and 3 October 2014. The inspections included primary, secondary, special and independent special schools, pupil referral units, children’s homes, children’s centres and nurseries. This outcome letter summarises the findings of these inspections.

Inspection reports for Somerset are on Ofsted’s reports website .
