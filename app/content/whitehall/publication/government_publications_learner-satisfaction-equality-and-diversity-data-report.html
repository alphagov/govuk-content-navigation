Detail

The learner satisfaction survey version 4 took place from November 2011 to May 2012. The survey was open to all in-scope learners and 365,514 learners submitted a valid response.

This report analyses responses to the 9 scoring questions of the 2011 to 2012 learner satisfaction survey and includes breakdowns by age, sex, ethnicity, learning difficulties and or disabilities, level of deprivation and level of study.
