Details

This guidance applies to all existing academies.

It sets out how you should comply with the requirements under the FOIA . A model publication scheme is also available for you to download.

You can use the model freedom of information publication scheme for academies without modification by any public authority.

Further information on schools and academies data protection obligations are available on The Information Commissioner’s Office website .
