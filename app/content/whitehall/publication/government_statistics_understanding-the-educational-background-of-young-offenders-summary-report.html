These are the headline results from a data sharing project between the Department for Education (DFE) and the Ministry of Justice (MOJ). The analysis focuses on those young offenders sentenced in 2014 matched to DFE data. We intend to follow this summary with a more comprehensive report on Thursday 15 December.
