Detail

The unique learner number

Large organisations that receive funding from the Skills Funding Agency must complete an individualised learner record (ILR). It is now mandatory to include a unique learner numbers (ULN) in this.

Employers will need to use our services to be able to find and/or create a ULN.

The personal learning record

The personal learning record (PLR) records all qualifications and credit framework (QCF) learner achievement both at unit (credit) and qualification level as well as qualifications such as A levels, GCSEs, diplomas and functional skills.

Employers will be able to use the PLR as proof of employee qualifications; learners can email a pdf copy of their PLR to prospective employers.

Registering

You need to complete registration forms to access the service.
