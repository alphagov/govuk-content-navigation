Details

A letter to Higher Education Funding Council for England ( HEFCE ) , providing details of the allocations we’ll be making to HEFCE for the financial year 1 April 2014 to 31 March 2015. It also includes the priorities we wish to set the council for that year.

The Higher Education Funding Council for England ( HEFCE ) distributes public money for teaching, research, knowledge exchange and related activities.
