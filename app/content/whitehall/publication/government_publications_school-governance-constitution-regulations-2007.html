Detail

This statutory guidance is for:

  • school leaders
  • school staff
  • governing bodies
  • local authorities

It explains the arrangements for the constitution of the governing bodies of maintained schools:

  • formed prior to 1 September 2012
  • where there is no change to the instrument of government after that date

4 separate versions are available for the following types of maintained schools:

  • community schools
  • community special schools
  • foundation schools
  • foundation special schools
  • voluntary aided schools
  • maintained nursery schools

Please ensure you read the appropriate version for your school.

The guidance covers:

  • the description of the different categories of governor
  • how governing bodies are to be constituted
  • terms of office
  • removal and disqualification of governors
  • instruments of government
  • all aspects of the School Governance (Constitution) (England) Regulations 2007

Each version contains a model instrument of government (annex B), these are also available separately for schools to download and fill in.
