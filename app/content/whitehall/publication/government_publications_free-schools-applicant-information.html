Details

A list of free schools proposing to open in 2015 and beyond from the wave 8 application round. The list includes:

  • name of the proposed free school as stated in the application
  • proposed local authority of the free school
  • type of school of the proposed school
  • phase of the proposed school
  • faith, ethos or designation of the proposed school

The list is available in Excel and open document formats.
