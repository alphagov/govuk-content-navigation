Eliminating unnecessary workload around planning and teaching resources

Detail

This report from the Planning and Resources Review Group sets out recommendations to reduce the workload burden on teachers.

It is for:

  • teachers
  • school leaders
  • school governing boards

It looks at:

  • the role that planning plays in effective teaching
  • the difference between lesson planning for accountability purposes and lesson planning to help pupil progress
