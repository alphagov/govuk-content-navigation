Apprenticeships and traineeships growth requests 2016 to 2017

The Apprenticeships: Common Funding and Performance-management Rules 2016 to 2017 details the performance-management points for the 2016 to 2017 funding year.

In preparation for performance-management point 1, we have published the above 2016 to 2017 growth request form for 16 to 18 and adult apprenticeships, and 16 to 18 and 19 to 24 traineeships.

Colleges and training organisations can use this form to submit requests to:

  • increase 16 to 18 and adult apprenticeships contract values for August 2016 to March 2017

  • increase 16 to 18 and 19 to 24 traineeships contract values for periods August 2016 to July 2017

When reviewing requests we will consider the following principles:

  • your final 2015 to 2016 apprenticeship delivery

  • your 2016 to 2017 delivery against year-to-date profiles, as set out in annex A of the Apprenticeships: Common Funding and Performance-management Rules 2016 to 2017

  • you can prove that there is immediate demand from employers or learners that you cannot meet from your current maximum contract value (which would include transferring funding from non-apprenticeship delivery)

  • we are confident that awarding an increase to your maximum contract value is good use of public funds

  • you are listed on the Register of Training Organisations and have successfully completed the capacity and capability questions

Using the principles outlined above, it is important that you submit your request to: ProviderPerformanceManagement@sfa.bis.gov.uk before 5pm on Friday 18 November.

We will communicate any changes to your 16 to 18 and adult apprenticeships and 16 to 18 and 19 to 24 traineeships contract values according to the timetable published in the Apprenticeships: Common Funding and Performance-management Rules 2016 to 2017 .

For further information or related queries, please contact your provider manager in the first instance.
