Ref: DFE-00638-2014 PDF , 1.33MB , 129 pages

Details

This guide will help local authorities submit data for the children looked after data return (also known as SSDA903) for the year 1 April 2015 to 31 March 2016.

It includes an explanation of each data item that we collect in the return.

You may also find it useful to read the technical specification , which has information about the different formats for submitting the data.
