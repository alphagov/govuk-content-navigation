Detail

This statistical first release ( SFR ) gives information on the achievements in GCSE examinations and other qualifications of young people in the academic year 2014 to 2015. This typically covers those starting the academic year at age 15.

It also sets out data broken down by pupil characteristics, including:

  • gender
  • ethnicity
  • eligibility for free school meals
  • disadvantage
  • special educational needs
  • English as a first language
