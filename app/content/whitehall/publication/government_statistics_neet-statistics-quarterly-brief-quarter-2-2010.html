This brief sets out the latest statistics available on NEET (not in employment, education or training) in England from the Department for Education 16 to 18 Participation statistical first release ( SFR ), the Labour Force Survey, and regional NEET figures and a segmentation using Connexions data. Additional tables will be released with the brief which disaggregates the data by characteristics such as gender.

As part of a government drive for data transparency in official publications we will make supporting data for this publication available as an additional table on this page on 9 September 2010.
