Detail

Care to Learn is intended to help enable young parents under the age of 20 to continue in, and return to learning after the birth of a child by assisting with the costs of childcare and associated travel.

This guide provides information to all those involved in the delivery of the Care to Learn scheme in the 2013 to 2014 academic year.

It does not attempt to offer definitive advice for every situation; stakeholders who require further or more detailed help and advice should contact the Learner Support Service who administer the scheme on behalf of the EFA.
