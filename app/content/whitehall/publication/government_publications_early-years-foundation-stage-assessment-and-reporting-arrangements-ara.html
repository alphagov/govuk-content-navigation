Details

This statutory guidance describes the statutory assessment and reporting requirements for the early years foundation stage ( EYFS ). It is for:

  • headteachers
  • governing bodies
  • local authority assessment co-ordinators
  • anyone else involved in assessing and reporting the EYFS

The sections together make up the ‘Assessment and reporting arrangements’ (ARA) for the 2014 to 2015 academic year.
