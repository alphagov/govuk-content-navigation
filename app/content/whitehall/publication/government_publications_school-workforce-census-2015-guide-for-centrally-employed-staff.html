Guide for local authorities completing the school workforce census 2015 for centrally employed children’s services staff.

Detail

This guide provides school workforce census 2015 preparation instructions for children’s services staff in local authorities so that they:

  • understand the rationale behind, and purpose of, the census
  • are able to prepare for the census
  • are able to complete census returns during 2015

More guidance on submitting data for the school workforce census 2015 is available.
