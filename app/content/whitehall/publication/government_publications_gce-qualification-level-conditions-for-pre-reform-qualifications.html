Details

These publications set out rules for all pre-reform AS and A levels.

They do not apply to any of the reformed AS and A levels .

Read the associated guidance to understand how to comply with these rules.

You should also read our requirements for all qualifications , and our other rules for pre-reform AS and A levels.

February 2017

We have published our Requirements for key dates which come into force on 1 May 2017. These are requirements which an awarding organisation must comply with from that date, when it specifies deadlines for requesting reviews of marking, reviews of moderation and appeals. For more information see our announcement .
