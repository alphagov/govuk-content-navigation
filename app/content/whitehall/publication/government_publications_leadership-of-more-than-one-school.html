Details

This Ofsted survey report evaluates the impact, structure and practice of federated leadership arrangements on pupils’ education in a small sample of established school federations.

It looks in particular at schools that are in federations that have one governing body and that, in the majority of cases, share a headteacher or an executive headteacher.
