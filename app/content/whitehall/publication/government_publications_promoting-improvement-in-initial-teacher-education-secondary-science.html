Details

As part of the ITE inspection framework from September 2012 Ofsted is promoting further improvement in the ITE sector. It provided annual dissemination conferences and associated online materials for each of the thematic inspections conducted alongside individual inspections of ITE partnerships during 2012 and 2013.

This page has the materials for secondary science.

There is also information about ITE thematic inspections for:

  • primary mathematics
  • secondary modern languages
  • secondary history
