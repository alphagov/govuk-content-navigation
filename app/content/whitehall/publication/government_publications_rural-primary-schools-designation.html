Details

This is the latest list of primary schools in England that the Department for Education ( DfE ) has designated as rural.

If you are proposing to close a primary school, use this list to confirm whether or not it is a rural school.

DfE identifies rural schools, using the Office for National Statistics’ rural and urban area classification, as being located in:

  • towns and fringe areas
  • villages or hamlets
  • isolated dwellings

The Designation of Rural Primary Schools (England) 2016 Order came into force on 10 October 2016.
