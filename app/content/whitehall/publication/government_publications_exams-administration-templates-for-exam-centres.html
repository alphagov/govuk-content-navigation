Detail

The exams policy template will help exam centres formulate their own exam policy.

The risk management template willl help centres log any potential risks they have identified in relation to the administration of exams. It can also help the exams officer start discussions with colleagues about the risks that might apply to their centre.

The template for tracking late, withdrawn and amended exam entires is an example of how to track these exam entries and the incurred costs.

The exam review template is an example of the type of information centres can compile when reviewing the exams office’s work and identifing potential areas for improvement.

More information for exam centres on exam administration is available.
