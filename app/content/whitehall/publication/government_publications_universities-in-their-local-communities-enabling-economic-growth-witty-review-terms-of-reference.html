Details

This review aims to investigate how universities can drive growth in their areas and for the benefit of the wider UK and to disseminate knowledge and best practice. The terms of reference provides questions for the review to explore and further background.

See also the request for feedback to the review .

The review builds on the Wilson review of Business-University Collaboration , taking into account developments since the Wilson Review was published. See also the Government Response to the Wilson Review .
