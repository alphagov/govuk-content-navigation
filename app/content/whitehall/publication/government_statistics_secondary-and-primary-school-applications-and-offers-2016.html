Detail

The number of offers made to applicants for both secondary and primary school places and the proportion which have received preferred offers (eg 1st, 2nd and 3rd preference).

It is based on the applications made by parents and the offers they received on the respective national offer days of 1 March 2016 (secondary) and 18 April 2016 (primary).
