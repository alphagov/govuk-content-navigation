A summary of responses to questions submitted to 2 waves of the National Foundation of Educational Research (NFER) teacher voice omnibus.

Documents

Teacher voice omnibus: questions for DfE - March to May 2014 and May to June 2014

Details

Questions covered the following topics:

  • the new national curriculum
  • changes to accountability indicators
  • universal infant free school meals ( UIFSM )
  • raising the participation age
  • effectiveness of ‘Need to Know’ timelines
  • effective initial teacher training ( ITT )
  • pupil behaviour
