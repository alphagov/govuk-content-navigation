Documents

Exempt charities and the role of the Secretary of State as Principal Regulator

Ref: DFE-00042-2014 PDF , 254KB , 20 pages

Details

This is for exempt charities:

  • academy trusts
  • sixth-form colleges
  • voluntary-aided schools
  • voluntary-controlled schools
  • foundation trust schools

It outlines:

  • the role of the Secretary of State for Education as Principal Regulator
  • exempt charities’ duties and responsibilities

Voluntary schools are established by a religious organisation, and partly or entirely funded by a local authority.
