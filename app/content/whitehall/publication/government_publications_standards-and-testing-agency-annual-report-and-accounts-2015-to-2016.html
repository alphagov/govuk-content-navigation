Details

This page contains the Standards and Testing Agency (STA) annual report and accounts for 2015 to 2016.

The STA, an executive agency of the Department for Education, is responsible for the development and delivery of all statutory assessments from early years to the end of key stage 2.

This report was laid before Parliament on 18 July 2016.
