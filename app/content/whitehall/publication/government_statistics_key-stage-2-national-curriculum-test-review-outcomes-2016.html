Details

Provisional information on the outcomes of clerical reviews and reviews of marking for the 2016 key stage 2 national curriculum tests in:

  • English reading
  • English grammar, punctuation and spelling
  • mathematics

It includes information on review requests and review outcomes.

This information is provisional because the data doesn’t yet take account of the outcomes of any process review applications.
