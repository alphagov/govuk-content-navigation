Reviewing post-16 education and training institutions: updated guidance on area reviews

Ref: BIS/16/118 PDF , 1.42MB , 60 pages

Detail

This guidance sets out the national framework for area reviews of the post-16 education and training sector.

It explains how area reviews will be carried out by local providers and stakeholders working with the:

  • Further Education Commissioner
  • Sixth-Form College Commissioner
  • Education Funding Agency
  • Skills Funding Agency
