National funding formulae: equalities impact assessment

Ref: DFE-00345-2016 PDF , 527KB , 27 pages

Detail

An assessment of the effect that the schools national funding formula and the high needs funding formula will have on groups with relevant protected characteristics.

The relevant protected characteristics, identified in the Equality Act 2010, include:

  • disability
  • gender reassignment
  • pregnancy and maternity
  • race
  • religion or belief
  • sex and sexual orientation
