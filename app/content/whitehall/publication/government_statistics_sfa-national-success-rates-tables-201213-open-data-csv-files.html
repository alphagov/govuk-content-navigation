Details

The Education & Training National Success Rates Tables and the Apprenticeships National Success Rates Tables for the academic year 2013 to 2014 will be released on Wednesday 8 April 2015 on the national success rates tables pages of GOV.UK and www.data.gov.uk. The tables will be available in both proprietary and 3-star open data formats.

The publication of the National Success Rates Tables is a regular annual publication of data that follows the release of these data in the January statistical first release (SFR) . The tables allow providers to assess their performance, and assist in the planning of action programmes to raise the standards of their delivery, and raise the retention and achievement rates of their learners.

The content of this page provides versions of national success rates tables produced by the Skills Funding Agency in CSV format as part of the Agencyâs commitment to open data.

The NSRT CSV files for both apprenticeships and education and training are found within the zipped attachments.
