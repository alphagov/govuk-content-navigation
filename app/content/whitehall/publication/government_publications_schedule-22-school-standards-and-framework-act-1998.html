Detail

When a foundation, trust or voluntary school is closed and being discontinued, those persons holding land for the purposes of the school are required to apply to the Secretary of State to decide what should happen to any land used by the school that has been provided, acquired or enhanced at public expense.

This legislative requirement is contained in Part II of Schedule 22 to the School Standards and Framework Act 1998 (as amended by Schedule 4 of the Education Act 2006).
