Guidance for schools about setting up and improving counselling services for pupils.

Document

Counselling in schools: a blueprint for the future

Ref: DFE-00117-2015 PDF , 595KB , 40 pages

Detail

This is for:

  • school leaders
  • headteachers
  • governing bodies
  • those with day-to-day responsibility for mental health issues in schools such as:
    • counsellors
    • clinical supervisors
    • managers of school counselling services

It will also be of interest to NHS providers and local authority commissioners of counselling and other forms of psychological support.

It applies to all primary and secondary schools.

It is about providing counselling as a psychological therapy to improve the mental health and wellbeing of children and young people.
