This guidance has now been updated for the 2015 to 2016 test cycle .

Schools and local authorities (LAs) have a statutory duty to ensure that teacher assessment is accurate and in line with national standards. The guidance is reviewed annually and contains changes for the 2014 to 2015 academic year.

This guidance is for:

  • LA moderation managers
  • LA external moderators
  • all primary phase headteachers, senior school staff and year 2 teachers

It covers:

  • roles and responsibilities
  • preparation for the moderation process
  • internal school moderation
  • suitable evidence for moderation
  • the moderation visit
  • moderation feedback
