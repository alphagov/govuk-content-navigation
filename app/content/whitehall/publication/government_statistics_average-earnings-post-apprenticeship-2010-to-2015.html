Details

These experimental estimates use longitudinal education outcomes ( LEO ) data to show earnings after an apprenticeship across different subject areas and by institution. They update previous estimates .

The LEO study brings together:

  • further education information from the Department for Education ( DfE )
  • benefits information from the Department for Work and Pensions ( DWP )
  • employment and earnings information from Her Majesty’s Revenue and Customs ( HMRC )

We have also published research which shows that those who achieve further education qualifications are better off than those who start but do not achieve it.
