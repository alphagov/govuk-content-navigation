Ref: DFE-00007-2015 PDF , 832KB , 20 pages

Details

This guide will help local authorities complete and submit data for the school exclusion reviews survey using COLLECT (Collections Online - Learners, Education, Children and Teachers).

COLLECT is the Department for Education’s centralised data collection and management system.

The guide includes information on:

  • accessing COLLECT
  • adding data on screen
  • exporting data

More guidance on submitting data for the school exclusion reviews survey is available.
