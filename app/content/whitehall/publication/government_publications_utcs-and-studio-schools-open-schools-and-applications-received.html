Details

Applications that we received from groups interested in opening a UTC or studio school in 2013 or later.

This period covers free school programme waves 3, 4, 5, 7 and 8.

The document lists applications by:

  • name of school
  • local authority
  • age group served
  • religious character

There was no wave 6 application round for UTCs and studio schools.

You can also read free schools: open schools and successful applications , which lists open UTCs and studio schools and those in the pre-opening stage.
