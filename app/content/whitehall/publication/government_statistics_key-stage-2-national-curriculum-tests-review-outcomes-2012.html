This report provides provisional information on the outcomes of clerical reviews and reviews of marking for the 2012 key stage 2 national curriculum tests in English reading and mathematics. A reviews service was not offered for English writing in 2012. The 2012 report will provide information on review requests and review outcomes for the level 6 English reading and mathematics tests, administered by schools on an optional basis, as well as the statutory level 3 to 5 tests. The report is provisional as the data does not yet take account of the outcomes of any process review applications.
