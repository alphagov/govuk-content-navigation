Detail

The Foundation Art and Design programme at Manchester Metropolitan University continued to be deemed outstanding in its December 2012 inspection report. A key feature is a continuous and intensive focus on drawing and developing students’ understanding and appreciation of drawing. As a result, the quality and richness of students’ final portfolios and their individual expression of ideas are enhanced significantly.
