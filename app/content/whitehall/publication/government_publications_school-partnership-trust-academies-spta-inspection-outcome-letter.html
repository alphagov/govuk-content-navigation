Detail

Ofsted inspected 6 primary academies in June 2014. Inspectors judged the effectiveness of each school and also gathered information on the quality and impact of support provided by the School Partnership Trust to headteachers and governors. In addition, inspectors conducted telephone surveys with a further 10 academies.

Ofsted carried out focused inspections of 6 academies between 23 and 27 November 2015. Inspectors also spoke to staff in December 2015 and held a follow-up visit in January 2016. The outcome letter sets out the findings and a wider consideration of the Trustâ€™s overall performance.
