The October 2015 edition contains a message from Sean Harford, National Director, Education about the new inspection arrangements for September 2015.

The edition for May 2016 includes information about activities during National Apprenticeship Week 2016, information on what constitutes a factual accuracy check (FAC) on inspection reports and decommissioning arrangements for the data dashboard.

The August 2016 edition includes information about minor changes to the further education and skills inspection handbook, dispelling myths about inspections and the inspection of residential accommodation in colleges.
