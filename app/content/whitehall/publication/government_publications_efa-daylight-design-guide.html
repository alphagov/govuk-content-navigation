Guidance about climate-based daylight design for schools.

Document

EFA daylight design guide

Ref: EFA-00725-2014 PDF , 1.49MB , 15 pages

Detail

Education Funding Agency (EFA) has specified that daylight design in schools should be carried out using climate based daylight design.

This guide explains how to carry out the modelling, including using annual weather data to meet the requirements of the EFA contractors framework facilities output specification.
