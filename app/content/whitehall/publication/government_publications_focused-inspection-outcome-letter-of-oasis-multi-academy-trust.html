Detail

Ten academies were inspected as part of the focused Ofsted inspection of the Oasis multi-academy trust between 2 and 5 March 2015. Telephone discussions were held with the principals of 20 other academies. Discussions were held with senior and operational staff from the trust, principals, governors, strategic partners and other stakeholders during the follow-up visit to Oasis national office.

This letter summarises the inspection findings.
