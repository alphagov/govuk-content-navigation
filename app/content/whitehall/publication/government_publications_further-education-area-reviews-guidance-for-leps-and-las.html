Guidance for local enterprise partnerships (LEPs) and combined or local authorities (LAs) taking part in post-16 education area reviews.

Document

Area review guidance for LEPs and local authorities

Ref: DFE-00246-2016 PDF , 242KB , 15 pages

Detail

This guidance explains the role of LEPs , LAs and combined authorities in the area review process.

It includes information about:

  • local area review steering groups
  • successful completion of an area review
  • carrying out the recommendations of the review
