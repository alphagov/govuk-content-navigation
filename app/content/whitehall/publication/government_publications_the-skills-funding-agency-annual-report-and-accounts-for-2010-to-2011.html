Details

The annual report and accounts for 2010 to 2011 includes the Chief Executive’s review and an overview of the role of the Skills Funding Agency ( SFA ).

It also has details of the SFA ’s:

  • management team
  • programme assurance
  • reporting of personal data-related incidents
  • financial commentary
  • remuneration report
  • statement of the Chief Executive of Skills Funding’s responsibilities
  • statement of internal control

The financial information includes:

  • certificate and report of the Comptroller and Auditor General to the Houses of Parliament
  • statement of comprehensive net expenditure
  • statement of financial position
  • statement of cash flows
  • statement of changes in taxpayers’ equity
  • notes to the accounts
