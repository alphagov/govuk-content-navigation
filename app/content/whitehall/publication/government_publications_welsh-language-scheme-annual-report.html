Details

The Department for Education continues to be responsible for teachers’ pay and conditions of service and the Teachers’ Pension Scheme in both England and Wales. In exercising these functions, the department occasionally publish reports and other information in Welsh, and deal with teachers and organisations inside and outside Wales.
