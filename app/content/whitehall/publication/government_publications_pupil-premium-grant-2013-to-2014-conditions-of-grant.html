Detail

The pupil premium is additional funding given to schools so that they can support their disadvantaged pupils and close the attainment gap between them and their peers.

This document covers pupils recorded on the January 2013 school census as being eligible for free school meals and pupils who were eligible for the service child premium in either 2011 to 2012 or 2012 to 2013.
