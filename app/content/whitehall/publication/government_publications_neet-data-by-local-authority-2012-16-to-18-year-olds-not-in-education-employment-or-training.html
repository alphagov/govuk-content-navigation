Details

We base these estimated figures on information local authorities have sent us about young people’s participation in education or training in their area. They are an average for November to January each year.

These figures tend to be lower than those in the official statistical release on young people in England who are NEET because they:

  • only include young people who are known to their local authority (ie those who were educated in government-funded schools)
  • don’t count young people who are taking a gap year or are in custody as NEET
  • depend on the quality of each local authority’s data collection
