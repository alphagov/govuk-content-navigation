Detail

Information to help pupil referral units ( PRUs ) understand and plan for the compulsory legal requirements they must meet.

We’ve tried to be as precise as possible but some of the dates may change.

A timeline of useful dates and information for PRUs is also available.

PRUs are places that provide education for children who can’t go to a mainstream school.
