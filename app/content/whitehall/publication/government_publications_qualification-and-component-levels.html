Details

This document sets out:

  • rules (including level descriptors) for assigning levels to qualifications and their components
  • guidance on the use of our level descriptors

Condition E9 of our General Conditions of Recognition requires all awarding organisations to follow these rules, and have regard to this guidance, when assigning levels to their qualifications and their components.
