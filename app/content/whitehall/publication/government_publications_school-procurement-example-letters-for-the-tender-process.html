Details

You can use these documents when running a competitive tender process for a high-value procurement. This includes EU-compliant procurements that you advertise in the Official Journal of the European Union ( OJEU ).

Use the model invitation to tender ( ITT ) letter to send to potential suppliers to invite them to submit a bid for your contract.
