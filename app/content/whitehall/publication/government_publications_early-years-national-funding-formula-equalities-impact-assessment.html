Ref: DFE-00186-2016 PDF , 396KB , 14 pages

Details

An assessment of the effect that changes to how we fund free childcare and early education for 3- and 4-year-olds will have on groups with relevant protected characteristics.

The relevant protected characteristics, identified in the Equality Act 2010, include:

  • disability
  • gender reassignment
  • pregnancy and maternity
  • race
  • religion or belief
  • sex and sexual orientation
