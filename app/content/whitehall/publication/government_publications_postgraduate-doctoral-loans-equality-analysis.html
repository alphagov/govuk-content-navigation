Ref: DFE-00085-2017 PDF , 351KB , 18 pages

Details

An assessment of the effect that the proposed postgraduate doctoral loan scheme to support PhD students would have on groups with relevant protected characteristics.

The relevant protected characteristics, identified in the Equality Act 2010, include:

  • disability
  • gender reassignment
  • pregnancy and maternity
  • race
  • religion or belief
  • sex and sexual orientation
