Detail

If you’re a school converting to academy status, you can use the forms on this page to apply.

If you’re a mainstream primary or secondary school, you can also apply using the online application form .

Special schools can only apply using the Word form on this page. Special schools are schools for children with special educational needs.

If you’re a sixth-form college considering becoming a 16 to 19 academy, advice and an application form are available.

Read more guidance on the process for converting to academy status .
