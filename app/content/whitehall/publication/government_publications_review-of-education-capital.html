Ref: DFE- 00286- 2013 PDF , 539KB , 31 pages

Details

This review looks into the Department for Education’s ( DfE ) current capital expense. It also makes recommendations on what DfE can do to make sure future capital investment provides value for money and meets the need for more school places.

The progress report sets out the progress DfE has made against the original recommendations.
