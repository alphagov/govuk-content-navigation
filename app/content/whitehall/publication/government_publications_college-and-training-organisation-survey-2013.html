Detail

This is an ongoing process and the Agency will continue to work closely with the FE sector and our stakeholders to improve the way we work.

In July 2014, the Agency issued its fourth annual survey which provided colleges and training organisations with a further opportunity to feedback on our role, the way we operate and the way we communicate with the FE sector.
