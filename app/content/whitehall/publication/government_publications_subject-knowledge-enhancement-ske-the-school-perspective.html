Details

Subject knowledge enhancement ( SKE ) can help you fill your shortage subject places such as biology, chemistry, computing, design and technology, geography, languages, maths and physics. Around a third of this year’s shortage subject trainees were recruited using SKE .

Find out more about how schools have:

  • benefited from SKE
  • used it for their recruitment
  • set-up their own SKE programmes
  • promoted it in their teacher training recruitment marketing
