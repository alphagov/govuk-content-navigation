Details

Higher and degree apprenticeships include the achievement of academic and vocational qualifications and learning from level 4 upwards and offer an apprentice the opportunity to combine working while studying for a work-based academic or vocational high level qualification.

Additional operational guidance for Higher Education Institutions on delivering higher and degree apprenticeships will be available shortly.
