Details

Allocation / contract values data 2015 to 2016 (March 2016 and June 2016)

The updated funding year contract values provide maximum contract value information as at March 2016 and June 2016 for colleges, training organisations and employers that have a contract for 2015 to 2016.

It excludes contract values where the Agency is discussing current delivery performance.

This updates the initial contract values published in August 2015.

Allocation / contract values data 2015 to 2016 (August 2015)

The funding year contract values provide maximum contract value information as at August 2015 for colleges, training organisations and employers that have a contract for 2015 to 2016. It excludes contract values where these are not finalised.

These allocation/contract values are subject to the performance management arrangements set out in the Operational Performance Management Rules 2015 to 2016 . We publish this information annually. We will update it throughout the year, following the performance-management points and final claim submissions.
