Detail

The guidance publication contains information about the way we assess and approve bids from employer groups to develop new apprenticeships (expressions of interest), draft apprenticeship standards and draft apprenticeship assessment plans.

Employer groups who are preparing to submit any of these documents should see the guidance for trailblazers for more information.

You can comment on all expressions of interest, draft apprenticeship standards and draft assessment plans submitted that month through the online survey.
