Guidance on the use of emergency salbutamol inhalers in schools

PDF , 480KB , 22 pages

Details

From 1 October 2014 UK schools will be allowed to purchase a salbutamol inhaler without a prescription for use in emergencies when a child with asthma cannot access their own inhaler.

This guidance will give schools that choose to keep an emergency inhaler the basis to create a policy or protocol for using it.
