Outcome type: Prohibition order
Prohibition order effective: 1 May 2013

Notice is hereby given that, in accordance with The Teachers’ Disciplinary (England) Regulations 2012, a professional conduct panel was convened to consider the case of Ms Alison Louise Tiltman, formerly employed in Hampshire, South East.

The proceedings were held at 53-55 Butts Road, Earlsdon Park, Coventry, CV1 3BH at 9.30am on 23 April 2013.

The meeting was held in private but a decision announced in public.
