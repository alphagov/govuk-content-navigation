Details

Supplementary guidance to ‘Alternative higher education providers: specific course designation guidance for applicants’ that was published in June 2013. This document offers additional guidance on:

  • franchise arrangements
  • postgraduate only courses
  • quality assurance
  • student number controls for alternative providers

This supplementary guidance should be read in conjunction with the following documents which also relate to the alternative provider specific designation process:

  • Alternative higher education providers: specific course designation - guidance for applicants
  • Alternative higher education providers: specific course designation - technical note
  • Alternative higher education providers: student number controls
