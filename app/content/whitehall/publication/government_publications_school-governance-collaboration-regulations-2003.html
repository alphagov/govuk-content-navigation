Details

This statutory guidance is for:

  • school leaders
  • school staff
  • governing bodies
  • local authorities

It applies to all maintained school in England.

This guidance explains the arrangements for maintained schools to set up joint committees and collaborate over their functions, as set out in the School Governance (Collaboration) (England) Regulations 2003 .
