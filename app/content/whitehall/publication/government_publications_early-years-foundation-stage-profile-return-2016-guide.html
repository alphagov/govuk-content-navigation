Guide for local authorities submitting data for the 2016 early years foundation stage profile (EYFSP) return.

Early years foundation stage profile return 2016: guide for submitting 2016 assessments

Ref: DFE-00135-2016 PDF , 377KB , 18 pages

Detail

This guide includes information on:

  • which schools, early years providers and pupils are in scope for the 2016 EYFSP return
  • the data local authorities need to submit
  • what formats to report the data in

A guide to using our COLLECT data collection system to submit data for the EYFSP 2016 return is also available.

Read more guidance about submitting data for the EYFSP return .

Early years providers are organisations that offer education for children aged under 5, including nurseries and childminders.
