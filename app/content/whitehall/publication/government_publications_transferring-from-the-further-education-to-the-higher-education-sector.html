Guidance for further education corporations: criteria and process for applying to transfer from the further education to the higher education sector

Ref: BIS/15/522 PDF , 504KB , 21 pages

Detail

This guidance sets out the criteria and process for further education corporations ( FEC ) seeking to apply to transfer to the higher education ( HE ) sector.

It updates and supersedes the Higher Education Funding Council for England ( HEFCE ) report ‘Transfers between the further and higher education sectors’ (2001).

It applies to FECs in England only.
