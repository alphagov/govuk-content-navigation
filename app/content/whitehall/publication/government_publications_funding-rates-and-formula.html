Details

Colleges, providers, schools and academies receive annual funding allocations from the Education Funding Agency (EFA) for provision of 16 to 19 education.

This document gives advice about the funding rates and the formula used in the funding arrangements for the 2016 to 2017 academic year.

Guidance from 2015 to 2016, 2014 to 2015 and 2013 to 2014 are also available on this page.
