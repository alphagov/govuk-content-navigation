Details

This guide contains information for all special schools, to help them prepare and submit their school census spring and summer 2014 return.

It includes:

  • what data needs to be submitted
  • new data items added to the census
  • the authorisation process
  • a detailed specification for pupil- and school-level codesets
  • important deadlines
