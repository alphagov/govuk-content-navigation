Details

This research was commissioned to develop our understanding of the journey to teacher training – looking specifically at shortage subjects including the:

  • triggers of interest in teaching
  • way candidates progress from interest to making an application
  • types of barriers and challenges encountered on the journey

The research was conducted to ensure that barriers and challenges can be addressed as part of our initial teacher training ( ITT ) recruitment strategy, and to help inform our marketing strategy in order that it meets its targets in creating new ITT trainees.
