Out of Tolerance Summary - Summer 2015 GCSE, AS and A Level Awards

Ref: Ofqual/15/5768 PDF , 240KB , 3 pages

Detail

Exam boards use predictions based on prior attainment to give a picture of what the results may look like. They report to us if the actual results are significantly different from the predictions (out of tolerance) and they provide evidence to justify their decisions. We will either accept the explanation or challenge those results if the evidence is not sufficient. This report provides detail on those awards in summer 2015 that were out of tolerance.
