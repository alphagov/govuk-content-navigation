Details

A list of free schools proposing to open in 2016 and beyond from the wave 9 application round. The list includes the:

  • school as stated in the application
  • proposed local authority in which the free school would be situated
  • type of school
  • phase
  • faith, ethos or designation

It is available in Excel and open document formats.
