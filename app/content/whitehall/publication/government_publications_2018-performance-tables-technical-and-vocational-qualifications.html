Details

Lists of qualifications for reporting in the 2018 performance tables in the following categories:

  • technical awards
  • technical certificates
  • tech levels
  • applied general qualifications

You can find more information on the performance tables website.

Technical awards are practical qualifications for students aged 14 to 16.

Technical certificates, tech levels, and applied general qualifications are technical and applied qualifications for students aged 16 to 19 .
