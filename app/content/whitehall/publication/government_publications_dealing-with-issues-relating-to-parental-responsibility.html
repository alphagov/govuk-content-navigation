Guidance for local authorities and schools dealing with adults who have legal rights and responsibilities for children at their school.

Document

Understanding and dealing with issues relating to parental responsibility

Ref: DFE-00002-2016 PDF , 288KB , 14 pages

Detail

This guide is for:

  • school governing bodies
  • school leaders
  • school staff
  • local authorities
  • diocesan boards

It applies to:

  • local-authority-maintained schools
  • local-authority-maintained nursery schools
  • academies and free schools

It explains what to do when there are disputes between a number of adults, each claiming to have parental responsibility for a particular child.
