Detail

As part of the initial teacher education (ITE) inspection framework from September 2012 Ofsted is promoting further improvement in the ITE sector by providing annual dissemination conferences and associated web-based materials for each of the thematic inspections conducted alongside individual inspections of ITE partnerships during 2012 and 2013.

This page has the materials for secondary modern languages.

There is also information about thematic inspections for:

  • primary mathematics
  • secondary science
  • secondary history
