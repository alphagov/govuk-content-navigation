Detail

The college accounts direction sets out the Skills Funding Agency (SFA) and Education Funding Agency (EFA) requirements for the preparation of college accounts.

The accounts direction is updated by SFA and EFA each year.

It should be of interest to principals and chief executives, finance directors, chairs of finance and audit committees, financial statements auditors, internal auditors and other key organisations in the college sector.

Alongside the accounts direction, SFA and EFA publish a finance record template each year. In accordance with the accounts direction, colleges must submit their completed finance record to SFA/EFA by 31 December 2016.

The Association of Colleges (AoC) produces an annual accounts direction handbook , which sets out further technical guidance on the preparation of college accounts. The handbook is available on the AoC website.
