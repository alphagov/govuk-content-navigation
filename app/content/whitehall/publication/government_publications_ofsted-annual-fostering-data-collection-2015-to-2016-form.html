Details

Ofsted collects fostering data annually from local authorities and independent fostering agencies in England.

Please note: there is an updated form to improve usability. The data collected has not changed. If you have not had any problems when using the form, it is not necessary to download and complete the new version.

If you have any further queries email SocialCareData@ofsted.gov.uk or call 0300 013 0020.
