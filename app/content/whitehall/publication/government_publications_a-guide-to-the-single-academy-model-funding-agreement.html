Ref: DFE-00236-2013 PDF , 322KB , 26 pages

Detail

This guide is aimed at governors (trustees) of existing open academies and those considering converting to a converter or sponsored academy. The intention is to provide a clearer understanding of exactly what the funding agreement means for the freedoms, obligations or requirements placed on academy trusts. The guide may also be of interest to headteachers (principals), sponsors and potential sponsors.
