Details

This handbook describes the main activities Ofsted inspectors undertake when they inspect further education and skills providers. It also sets out the main judgements that inspectors will report on.

It is also available to providers and other organisations to make sure that they are informed about inspection processes and procedures.

The handbook for use from September 2016 and a document summarising the main changes have also been published.

The handbook is to be used alongside the ‘Common inspection framework: education, skills and early years’.

The mythbuster document sets out facts about Ofsted’s requirements and dispels myths that can result in unnecessary workloads in colleges.
