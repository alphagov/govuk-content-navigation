Technical education reform: assessment of equalities impacts

Ref: DFE-00164-2016 PDF , 653KB , 20 pages

Details

An assessment of the impact that the proposals set out in the post-16 skills plan will have on groups with relevant protected characteristics.

The relevant protected characteristics, identified in the Equality Act 2010, include:

  • disability
  • gender reassignment
  • pregnancy and maternity
  • race
  • religion or belief
  • sex and sexual orientation
