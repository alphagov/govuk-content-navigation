This is the 2011 edition of families of schools for London which is based on final 2010 data. The data presented allows users to compare the performance of their school against the performance of other London schools with statistically similar pupil intakes. Headteachers can view this data in different ways and use it as the basis for working collaboratively with other schools to narrow gaps and secure better outcomes for pupils.
