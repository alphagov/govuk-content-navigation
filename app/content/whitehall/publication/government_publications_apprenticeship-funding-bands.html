Details

The list of apprenticeship standards shows 2 sets of funding bands:

  • for starts up to and including 30 April 2017
  • for starts from 1 May 2017

It lists the apprenticeship standards that have an approved assessment plan and are ready for employers and training organisations to use.

The frameworks spreadsheet sets out the funding bands that will apply for existing apprenticeship frameworks from 1 May 2017.
