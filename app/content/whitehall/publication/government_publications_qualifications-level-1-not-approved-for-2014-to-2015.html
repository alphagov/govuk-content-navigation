Qualifications: level 1 - not approved for 2014 to 2015

This publication was withdrawn on 18 June 2014

In February 2014, the Skills Funding Agency published lists of qualifications that were approved in 2013/14 but were not approved in 2014/15.

The lists have now been removed due to the start of the next academic year 2014/15.

Awarding organisations can still submit a request for a qualification to be considered to be funded on a exceptional basis, as part of the monthly approvals process, where the qualification meets the exception criteria as set out in the Technical Guidance for Awarding Organisations.

For further information please contact the Agency on qualifications@sfa.bis.gov.uk .
