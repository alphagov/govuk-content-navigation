Details

Education Funding Agency ( EFA ) works closely with Skills Funding Agency ( SFA ) and local authorities to make sure that European Social Fund ( ESF ) funding is used to target key areas of need. As the co-financing organisation, SFA manages all the ESF procurement and contracts for 14 to 19 provision on behalf of EFA .

The spreadsheet shows the providers delivering ESF provision for young people from 2011 to 2013 and the specifications to which the provision and contracts relate is available.

More information about the overall ESF programme can be found on GOV.UK
