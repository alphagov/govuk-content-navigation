What is the research evidence on writing?

Ref: DFE-RR238 PDF , 1.04MB , 44 pages

Details

There is a general agreement in the literature that there is less evidence about writing than about reading. International studies such as the Programme for international student achievement ( PISA ) and the Progress in international reading and literacy study ( PIRLS ) use indicators from reading as proxy measures for literacy and don’t include writing in their assessments.
