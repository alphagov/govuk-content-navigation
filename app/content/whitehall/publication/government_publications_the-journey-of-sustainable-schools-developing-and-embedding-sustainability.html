Details

This report breaks down how the schools involved in the research went from introducing sustainability into 1 or 2 areas of the curriculum to it becoming the motivation for all the schools’ policies and practices.

The report provides guidance on the:

  • skills, tools and activities you can use to start developing and embedding sustainability into your school
  • leadership qualities needed to develop your approach to sustainability
