Detail

Updated estimates for the academic years 2010 to 2011 through to 2013 to 2014 using new linked data to improve coverage and accuracy. The data comes from the Longitudinal Education Outcomes ( LEO ) dataset.

The updated destinations data for key stage 5 includes:

  • the percentage of students progressing to education, employment or training
  • the percentage of students who do not progress to education, employment or training

You can find more details on the new methodology in our first statistical working paper .
