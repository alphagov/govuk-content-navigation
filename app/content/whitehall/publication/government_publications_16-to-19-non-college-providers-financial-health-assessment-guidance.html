Detail

The EFA assesses a provider’s financial health to understand the degree of risk to the EFA in contracting with those organisations and to establish the recommended value of contracts appropriate to the financial resources of those organisations.

This document sets out the EFA’s approach to the financial health assessment of 16 to 19 non-college providers.
