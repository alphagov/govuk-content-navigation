Detail

The Better Communication Research Programme (BCRP) was commissioned as part of the Better Communication Action Plan, the government’s response to the Bercow review of services for children and young people with SLCN.

This had recommended a programme of research ‘to enhance the evidence base and inform delivery of better outcomes for children and young people’. This is one of four thematic reports which synthesize findings from the 10 technical reports that report results from individual BCRP projects; there are also two interim reports and a report of the BCRP as a whole.
