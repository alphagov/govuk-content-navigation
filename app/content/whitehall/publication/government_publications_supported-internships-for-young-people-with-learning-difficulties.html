Guidance on how to provide supported internships for young people with special educational needs and an EHC plan.

Document

Supported internships for young people with education, health and care plans

Ref: DFE-00430-2014 PDF , 1.29MB , 63 pages

Detail

This is guidance about creating study programmes for young people with:

  • an education, health and care plan
  • a learning difficulty assessment or statement of special educational needs issued before September 2014

It applies to:

  • local-authority-maintained schools
  • local authorities
  • academies and free schools
  • independent schools
  • sixth-form colleges
  • further education colleges

It also includes information about applying for Access to Work funding .
