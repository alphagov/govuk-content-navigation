Detail

This statistical working paper is the first in a series providing information on the use of new data to improve the coverage and accuracy of destinations measures using the Longitudinal Education Outcomes ( LEO ) dataset.

It presents updated estimates for the 2013 to 2014 academic year using this new linked data.

The updated destinations data for key stage 5 includes:

  • the percentage of students progressing to education, employment or training
  • the percentage of students who do not progress to education, employment or training

Destinations are also shown by:

  • student characteristics
  • gender
  • ethnicity
  • free school meals
  • special educational needs for schools
  • learning difficulties and/or disabilities for colleges

The key stage 5 measure is based on activity in the year after the young person took their A Level or other level 3 qualifications.

The LEO dataset has also been used to improve statistics on employment and earnings of graduates .
