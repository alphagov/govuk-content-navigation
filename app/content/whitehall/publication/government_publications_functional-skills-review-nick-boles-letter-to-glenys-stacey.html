Details

Minister of State for Skills and Equalities Nick Boles asks for advice on the findings of Ofqual ’s review and help on rebranding the qualifications. In the letter, he emphasizes the importance of a reliable test of literacy and numeracy skills for students who are not able to achieve a GCSE .

This relates to:

  • the conditions of funding for post-16 students without GCSE grade C in maths and/or English
  • Minister Hancock’s written ministerial statement on improving post-16 numeracy and literacy and increasing the uptake of reformed GCSEs in these subjects
