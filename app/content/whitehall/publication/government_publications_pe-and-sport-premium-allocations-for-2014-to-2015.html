Detail

The spreadsheet gives a breakdown of PE and sport premium allocations for the 2014 to 2015 academic year. It is divided into 3 sections:

  • maintained schools
  • academies (including free schools)
  • non-maintained special schools

The 2 conditions of grant documents explain how to use the premium to improve PE and sport provision.

Guidance for schools on how to use the PE and sport premium is available.
