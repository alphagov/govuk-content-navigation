A direction issued by DfE to Torbay council due to inadequate performance in children's social care services.

Direction issued to Torbay council

Detail

This direction requires the council to co-operate with a commissioner appointed by the Secretary of State.

It follows publication on 5 January 2016 of a report by Ofsted that judged the overall effectiveness of Torbay’s children’s social care services as inadequate.
