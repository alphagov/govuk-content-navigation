Details

Our Provider Financial Management and Assurance (PFMA) team has completed assurance work on funding claimed under the Adult Skills Budget and for 16-18 Apprenticeships for the 2014 to 2015 funding year and European Social Fund funding for 2014 to 2015.

We are making it available to help you avoid the types of issues commonly encountered and better prepare for our assurance reviews.
