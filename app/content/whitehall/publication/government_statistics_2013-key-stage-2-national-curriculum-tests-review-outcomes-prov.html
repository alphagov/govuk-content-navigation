Details

This report provides provisional information on the outcomes of clerical reviews and reviews of marking for the 2013 key stage 2 national curriculum tests in English reading, mathematics and English grammar, punctuation and spelling. The report provides information on review requests and review outcomes for the level 6 tests, administered by schools on an optional basis, as well as the statutory levels 3 to 5 tests. The report is provisional as the data does not yet take account of the outcomes of any process review applications.
