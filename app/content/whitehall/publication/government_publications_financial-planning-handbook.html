Financial planning handbook

This handbook provides guidance for further education colleges on financial planning information that the Skills Funding Agency and the Education Funding Agency require by 31 July each year.

It provides a single document to which colleges can refer when completing their financial plans. It aims to readily accommodate changes in generally accepted accounting principles issued by the Accounting Standards Board and other guidance issued by the funding bodies.

It will be of interest to all colleges that are required to provide two-year financial plans to the SFA or EFA.

College financial planning template

Colleges should submit their completed financial plans to the respective funding body no later that 31 July 2016 in accordance with the guidance provided in the Financial Planning Handbook.

The template is an integral part of a college’s strategic and development plans, as it expresses in financial terms the cost of implementing those plans and shows the income, expenditure and cash-flows associated with the projected levels of activity.

The financial plan template will be of interest to all colleges that are required to provide financial plans to the Skills Funding Agency and the Education Funding Agency.

Financial planning template guidance

The financial planning guidance template is an adjunct to the financial planning handbook 2016 to 2018 explaining the changes to the financial plan template.
