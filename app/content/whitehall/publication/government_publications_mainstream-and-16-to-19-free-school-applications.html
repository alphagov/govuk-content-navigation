Free school applications: criteria for assessment - mainstream, studio and 16 to 19 free schools

Ref: DFE-00176-2016 PDF , 670KB , 42 pages

Details

Use the forms on this page if you wish to open one of the following types of free school :

  • mainstream free school
  • studio school
  • 16 to 19 free school

The ‘Free school applications: criteria for assessment’ document gives guidance on the application process, including the deadline for submitting applications.

To help you plan your budget, you should also fill in a financial template (section G of the application):

  • mainstream and 16 to 19 free school financial template
  • studio school financial template

More information on opening a free school or studio school is available.
