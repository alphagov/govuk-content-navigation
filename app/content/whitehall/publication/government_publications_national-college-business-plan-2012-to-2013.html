Details

The National College is an executive agency of the Department for Education. It is responsible for delivering the priorities for school and academy leadership that are set out in the 2010 white paper ‘The Importance of Teaching’. It is also responsible for leadership development for children’s centres.

This business plan sets out the college’s aims and objectives for 2012 to 2013, including key milestones and performance indicators, it also summarises the college’s budget and staffing resources. This report will be updated annually.
