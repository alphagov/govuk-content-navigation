Oldknow Academy: letter from Lord Nash

PDF , 319KB , 12 pages

Details

A letter from Parliamentary Under Secretary of State for Schools Lord Nash to the Chair of the Oldknow Academy Trust setting out his concerns about Oldknow Academy.

Lord Nash has given written notice of the Secretary of State’s intention to terminate the supplemental funding agreement for the Academy unless his concerns are addressed promptly, in full, and to his satisfaction.
