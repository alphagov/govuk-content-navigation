Details

St Benedict Biscop is a good school which has improved significantly. Strategy is well focused and there is a clear vision for school improvement. Pupils’ progress is tracked and analysed in exceptional detail across all year groups. This is one of 12 case studies about the schools featured in the report Getting to good, published by Ofsted in September 2012.
