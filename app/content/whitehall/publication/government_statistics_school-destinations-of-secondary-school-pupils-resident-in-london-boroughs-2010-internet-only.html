For the first time this bulletin also contains London level cross-border movement, distance travelled and mode of travel taken to school by pupil and by school characteristics.

The bulletin includes maintained secondary schools (including middle deemed), city technology colleges and academies. It does not cover pupils attending other schools in the independent sector.

This is an update to the school destinations of secondary school pupils Resident in London Boroughs 2009 published on 26 August 2009.

The underlying data for this publication was made available on 13 September 2010.
