Notes for teachers on how to prepare for onscreen marking

Ref: ISBN 978-1-78315-704-4 , STA/15/7303/e PDF , 191KB , 2 pages

Details

This guidance will be updated for the 2017 test cycle in March.

This guidance gives advice to teachers about preparing pupils for tests which will be marked on screen. It is to help them understand where pupils should and should not place their answers to ensure that test scripts can be correctly scanned for on-screen marking.
