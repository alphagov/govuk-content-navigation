Details

Details the budget available for the 2013 to 2014 financial year for the further education (FE) sector. Includes information for the Skills Funding Agency to set provider allocations for the 2013 to 2014 academic year and sets out indicative funding levels for the 2014 to 2015 financial year.

The government is funding high quality provision that delivers good value for money, and concentrating on young adults, the low skilled and those who are unemployed.

See also FE colleges capital investment strategy .
