Detail

These documents set out the funding and performance management rules for employers, employer-providers and training providers delivering the new apprenticeship programme from 1 May 2017.

The funding rules cover the period May 2017 to March 2018 and apply to all apprenticeship programmes starting on or after 1 May 2017. This includes both apprenticeship frameworks and standards.

The funding rules form part of the terms and conditions for funding apprenticeships by the Skills Funding Agency.

Training providers can contact us through our Business Operations Service Desk at servicedesk@sfa.bis.gov.uk or telephone 0370 2640001. You can also contact your provider management manager/adviser.

Employers requiring further support can contact us through our Apprenticeship Business Support team on 0800 015 0600.
