From:
Department for Education , Government Equalities Office , The Rt Hon Nicky Morgan MP , and Jo Swinson
First published:

This report provides findings from research on how best to tackle homophobic, biphobic and transphobic (HBT) bullying in schools.

Details

This study:

  • examines the key factors perceived to be most effective in preventing HBT bullying
  • sets out the views expressed by teachers, students and others about what worked or didn’t work in preventing HBT bullying
  • is conducted by NatCen Social Research
