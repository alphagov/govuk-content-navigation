Details

This statistical first release (SFR) provides provisional 2014 key stage 2 national curriculum assessment results for pupils in schools in England at national, regional and local authority level. It also provides provisional figures on expected progress between key stage 1 (typically for 7-year-olds) and key stage 2 (typically for 11-year-olds).

It includes results from the key stage 2 tests in:

  • grammar, punctuation and spelling
  • mathematics
  • reading

It also includes results on key stage 2 teacher assessments in:

  • English
  • mathematics
  • reading
  • science
  • writing
