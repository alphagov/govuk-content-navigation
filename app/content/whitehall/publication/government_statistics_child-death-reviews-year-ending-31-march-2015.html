Details

Since 1 April 2008 we’ve collected information on the number of child deaths that child death overview panels ( CDOPs ) have reviewed on behalf of their local safeguarding children boards ( LSCBs ) in England.

This statistical first release covers reviews completed between 1 April 2014 and 31 March 2015. It includes information from all CDOPs about:

  • the characteristics of the children who died (like age, gender and cause of death)
  • deaths with ‘modifiable factors’ (factors that could be changed to reduce the risk of future child deaths)

You can also see data collected from CDOPs on previous reviews.
