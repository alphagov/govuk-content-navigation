Details

This document provides step-by-step guidance on how to register on the SFA’s e-tendering portal and provides instruction on how to access the register of apprenticeship training providers application questions.

For organisations interested in competing in the procurement for delivery to employers that do not have a digital account, this includes instructions on how to access the invitation to tender.
