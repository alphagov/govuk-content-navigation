Detail

This good practice example shows what support Ravensthorpe Community Childcare and Kirklees Local Authority give to children and families who speak English as an additional language. It focuses on how they support the children in developing their early language and literacy skills, both at preschool and at home. It also shows how they help adults to improve their employability.
