Details

The guide is for:

  • clinical commissioning groups
  • health professionals
  • local authorities

It explains the duties and responsibilities of health professionals who deal with children and young people with special educational needs and disability ( SEND ) and their families.

The guide should be read alongside the ‘Special education needs and disability code of practice: 0 to 25’ .

‘ SEND resources for healthcare professionals’ lists links to government information and external websites that may help:

  • commissioners
  • health and wellbeing boards ( HWBs )
  • health service providers and professionals
