Details

An international review of funding for young people with additional needs and the effect funding has on their attainment.

Additional needs pupils are defined as:

  • being economically disadvantaged and in need of financial support to attend education
  • having low prior attainment and being in need of extra support to succeed in education
  • having low-level special educational needs and disability
