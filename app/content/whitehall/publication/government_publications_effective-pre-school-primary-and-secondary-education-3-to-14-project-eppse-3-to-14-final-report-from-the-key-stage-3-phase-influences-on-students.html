Details

This research is part of the effective pre-school, primary and secondary education ( EPPSE ) project.

It explores how different phases of education, especially secondary school, relate to students’ attainment, social behaviour and dispositions at age 14 (year 9 in secondary school) and the factors that predict developmental change.
