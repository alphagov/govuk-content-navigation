Details

The NCTL annual survey of school and children’s centre leaders 2013 was a national survey of:

  • head teachers
  • senior leaders
  • middle leaders
  • chairs of governors in schools
  • leaders and deputies in children’s centres

This survey was conducted to inform the changes in leadership in both education and early years.
