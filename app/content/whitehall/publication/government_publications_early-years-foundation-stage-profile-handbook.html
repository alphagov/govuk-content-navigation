The early years foundation stage profile will be statutory for the 2016 to 2017 academic year. The handbook will be updated in the autumn term.

Local authorities and early years education providers and education professionals with responsibility for assessing, reporting or moderating the early years foundation stage ( EYFS ) profile should use this handbook.

It gives guidance to help you complete and moderate EYFS profile judgements so that outcomes are accurate and consistent across all early years settings.

It should be used alongside the EYFS Assessment and reporting arrangements .
