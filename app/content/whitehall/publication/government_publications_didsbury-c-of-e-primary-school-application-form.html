Release

The department is publishing the Didsbury Church of England Primary School application form.

The free school application form includes the following information:

  • application details section and/or a checklist
  • educational vision
  • education plan
  • evidence of demand / marketing
  • staffing / capacity and capability
  • premises
  • finances
  • suitability / due diligence / declaration
