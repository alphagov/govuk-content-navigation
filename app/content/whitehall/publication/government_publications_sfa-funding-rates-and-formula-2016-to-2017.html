Detail

This document sets out the details of the 2016 to 2017 funding system used to fund activity paid for from the following budgets: 16 to 18 apprenticeships (including traineeships), 19 and over apprenticeships and the Adult Education Budget.

The summary of changes document sets out the changes to the funding rates and formula 2016 to 2017 document from the 2015 to 2016 version.
