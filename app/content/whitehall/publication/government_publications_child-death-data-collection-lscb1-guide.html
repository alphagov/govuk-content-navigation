A guide to the LSCB1 aggregate return form for local safeguarding children boards (LSCBs) and child death overview panels (CDOPs).

Documents

Child death data collection 2015 to 2016: LSCB1 guide

Ref: DFE-00671-2014 PDF , 509KB , 27 pages

Details

This document helps LSCBs and CDOPs complete and submit their LSCB1 form for the child death data collection 2015 to 2016 .

The guide includes:

  • information on submitting data
  • notes for each data item
  • background information on the collection
