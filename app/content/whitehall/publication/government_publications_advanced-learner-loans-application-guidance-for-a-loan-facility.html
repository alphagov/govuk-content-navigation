Detail

Colleges and training organisations who do not already have a loan facility can apply for £75,000 to £500,000 of Advanced Learner Loans facility for 2016 to 2017 academic year.

This application guidance includes the criteria we will use to assess applications.

To apply, please complete an application form and send it to advancedlearningloans@sfa.bis.gov.uk by Monday, 14 March 2016.
