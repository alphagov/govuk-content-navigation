This report looks at what has worked in supporting and re-engaging young people who are not in education, employment or training (NEET).

What works re-engaging young people who are not in education, employment or training? Summary of evidence from the activity agreement pilots and the entry to learning pilots

Ref: DFE-RR065 PDF , 564KB , 24 pages

Details

Local authorities must provide young people in their area with suitable education and training. This includes tailored provision for those who are not in education, employment or training.

This report summarises a range of local authority pilot programmes and evaluations. It identifies what has worked well and where there could be improvements.
