Detail

Publicly funded colleges, training organisations, local authorities and employers (FE providers) who receive community learning funding must collect and return community learning data to the Skills Funding Agency.

Funding claim: community learning

Each year providers must fill in and submit a funding claim showing how you have spent your community learning funding.

Supplementary learner record

Providers must collect information through the supplementary learner record (above) for child learners on family programmes and return the form to familyprogrammes@skillsfundingagency.bis.gov.uk

Neighbourhood learning in deprived communities (NLDC)

If you cannot return NLDC information through the ILR , you must email the NLDC form (above) to nldc.returns@skillsfundingagency.bis.gov.uk

Find out more about community learning funding

More information on public funding for community learning is available from page 70 onwards of the SFA funding rules 2013 to 2014 .
