Pupils with a visual impairment or hearing impairment can use modified versions of the key stage 2 national curriculum tests. The guidance on this page is to help teachers decide which versions of the tests to order for particular pupils.

The ‘Test orders’ section of the NCA tools website will close on Friday 27 November. You can telephone our modified test agency helpline on 0300 303 3019 to place new orders or to discuss existing orders.
