Detail

Schools that form part of a closed private finance initiative ( PFI ) contract and who want to convert to an academy should use the documents on this page.

Use these documents to create your academy’s:

  • funding agreement
  • school agreement (this will replace any existing governing body agreement)
  • principal agreement (this sets out DfE ’s level of financial support to the local authority)

Read more guidance on the process for becoming an academy .
