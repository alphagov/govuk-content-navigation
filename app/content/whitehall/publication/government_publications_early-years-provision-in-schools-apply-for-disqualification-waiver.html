Details

Some people are disqualified from providing, being involved in the management of, or being employed in, early years provision in schools.

Use this form to:

  • describe any circumstances that you believe may disqualify you and Ofsted will confirm whether or not you are disqualified

  • apply to Ofsted for consent to waive disqualification

Download and complete the form onscreen and email or post to Ofsted.
