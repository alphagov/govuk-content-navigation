Details

This is the 17th edition of education and training statistics for the United Kingdom. It provides statistics relating to education in the UK and includes chapters relating to schools, post-compulsory education and qualifications, and an annex containing figures on education expenditure. The 2012 edition was published on 7 December 2012.
