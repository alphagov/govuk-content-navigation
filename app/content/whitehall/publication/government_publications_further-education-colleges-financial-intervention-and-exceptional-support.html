Details

This document sets out revised policy on the steps the Department for Business, Innovation and Skills ( BIS ) and Skills Funding Agency will take to protect learners where a further education college is encountering financial difficulties.

This updated guidance includes information on the:

  • importance of good financial management
  • impact on a college when they seek exceptional financial support
  • decision making process
  • relationship with area reviews
