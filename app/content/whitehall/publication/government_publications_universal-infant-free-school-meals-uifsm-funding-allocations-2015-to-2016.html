Detail

This data file contains the revenue funding allocations along with the small schools transitional funding for the 2015 to 2016 academic year for each school. Allocations are based on the on the average number of meals schools recorded in the October 2015 and January 2016 school censuses.

The file also contains provisional funding allocations for the 2016 to 2017 academic year.

More guidance on UIFSM funding is available.
