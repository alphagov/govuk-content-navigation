Details

Ofsted carried out no-notice inspections of 35 maintained schools and academies between 1 September and 20 October 2014. This advice note from Sir Michael Wilshaw, Her Majesty’s Chief Inspector, to the Rt Hon Nicky Morgan MP, Secretary of State for Education, summarises the findings of these inspections.
