Details

Schools converting to academy status should ask their solicitor or legal adviser to use this document to record any changes they want to make to the model documents they’ve completed as part of the academy conversion process.

The model documents you may want to change include:

  • model memorandum and articles of association
  • draft single model funding agreement or draft multi-academy trust model funding agreement
  • model documents for church schools
  • model documents for private finance initiative (PFI) schools

Read more guidance on the process for converting to academy status .
