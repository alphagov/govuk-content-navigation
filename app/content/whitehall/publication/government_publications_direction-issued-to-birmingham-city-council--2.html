Direction issued to Birmingham city council due to inadequate children’s social care services.

Direction issued to Birmingham City Council

PDF , 160KB , 5 pages

Details

An Ofsted inspection, published in May 2014, found the delivery of children’s services ‘inadequate’.

This direction formalises and secures the working arrangements between Lord Warner, Commissioner for Birmingham Children’s Social Care, and Birmingham city council. It revokes and replaces the previous direction of March 2013 .

This direction has closed; a direction issued on 17 December 2015 replaces this direction.

Previous directions and improvement notices include:

  • a 12-month direction in March 2013
  • an improvement notice in September 2010
  • an improvement notice in February 2009
