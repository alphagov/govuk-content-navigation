Details

The documents on this page have been produced to help you understand how we will calculate your post-16 allocation. We will be uploading statements, once available, to EFA Information Exchange: ‘Revenue Funding’ folder, under ‘AY 2017-18’. We expect allocations for 2017 to 2018 to be sent out to most institutions by the end of March 2017 and we will confirm deadlines for submitting business cases shortly.
