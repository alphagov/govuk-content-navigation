Details

This document is designed to guide independent and general hospital school users through the various aspects of COLLECT (Collections Online - Learners, Education, Children and Teachers) so they can complete and submit the annual school level annual school census (SLASC).

The guide includes information on:

  • accessing COLLECT
  • uploading and submitting census data
  • error queries
