Details

Information about the Technical Baccalaureate measure, which is awarded to students who take:

  • an approved tech level qualification
  • a level 3 maths qualification
  • an extended project

This document also includes details of funding that will be available from the financial year 2016 to 2017 for students who are awarded the TechBacc.
