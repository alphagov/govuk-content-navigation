Details

This letter was sent to responsible officers in awarding organisations.

Ofqual is planning to replace its Regulatory IT System ( RITS ) with a new system in 2016. In the RO34 letter Ofqual confirm that it has set up a number of user groups that will report to a Transition Advisory Group (TAG) to inform the design, development, testing and roll-out of the new Regulatory IT System.

Ofqual have established two awarding organisation user groups, and the letter invites awarding organisations to confirm if they are interested in taking part, and how to register their interest. The user groups are expected to meet monthly, and the first meetings are due to take place in May 2015.
