Details

This good practice example shows how Kingsmead Primary School engages Traveller parents to help raise achievement and improve attendance of their children. This is part of a set of examples showcasing good practice in raising the achievement and attendance of Gypsy, Roma and Traveller pupils, as well as reducing their exclusion rates.
