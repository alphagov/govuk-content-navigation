Details

Annual Report 2014/15, from Her Majesty’s Chief Inspector Sir Michael Wilshaw. The report covers schools and the further education and skills sectors.

The report is underpinned by the findings of over 5,000 inspections of schools, colleges and providers of further education and skills.

This paper was laid before Parliament in response to a legislative requirement or as a Return to an Address and was ordered to be printed by the House of Commons.
