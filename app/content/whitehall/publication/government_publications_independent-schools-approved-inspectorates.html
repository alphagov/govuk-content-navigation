Detail

The memorandum of understanding document sets out the agreement the Department for Education ( DfE ) has reached with Ofsted and the approved inspectorates of independent schools. It explains how Ofsted will monitor and report on the independent inspectorates’ work.

The 2 letters to independent inspectorates outline the agreements DfE has reached with the 2 approved inspectorates of independent schools:

  • Independent Schools Inspectorate ( ISI )
  • The School Inspection Service ( SIS )

Advice on the approval of independent inspectorates is also available.
