Details

These guides are for:

  • local authorities
  • local-authority-maintained schools
  • academies
  • free schools, including studio schools and university technical colleges (UTCs)
  • non-maintained special schools ( NMSS )

The guides will help you to complete and submit data for the school census 2016 using COLLECT , the Department for Education’s centralised data collection and management system.

The guides include information on:

  • accessing COLLECT
  • uploading and submitting census data
  • error queries

The editing guide includes information on:

  • editing restrictions
  • how to find the return in COLLECT
  • step-by-step guides to popular changes

More guidance on submitting data for the school census is available.
