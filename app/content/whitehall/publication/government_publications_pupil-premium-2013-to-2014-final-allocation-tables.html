Detail

The allocation tables use the January 2013 school census data and looked-after children numbers from the March 2013 children looked after return (also known as SSDA903).

The allocations are based on a rate of £953 for every primary pupil who is currently eligible for free school meals ( FSM ) or has been eligible for FSM in the past 6 years ( FSM ‘Ever 6’).

Secondary FSM ‘Ever 6’ and looked-after children continue to attract a premium of £900 per pupil, and service children attract a premium of £300.
