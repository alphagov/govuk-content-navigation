Ref: BIS/15/117/ MS Excel Spreadsheet , 37.7KB

Details

A form and guidance for claiming further education initial teacher training bursaries.

The guide is aimed at providers, but can be used as a source of reference for trainees. The information is for trainees starting in the academic year 2015 to 2016 only.

It includes:

  • training bursary rates
  • qualifying subjects
  • associated degree classifications

Mathematics trainees should also read ‘Mathematics subject knowledge enhancement (SKE) programme guide: academic year 2015 to 2016’ .

We will prioritise bursaries on maths and then English due to a growing demand. If funding remains unallocated, this will be allocated to any specific special educational needs and disabilities (SEND) applications. Contact the Further Education workforce incentives team ( feworkforceincentives@bis.gsi.gov.uk ) if you have any questions.
