Details

This statistical first release ( SFR ) provides provisional information on the outcomes of clerical and marking reviews for tests of:

  • English reading
  • English grammar, punctuation and spelling
  • mathematics

Clerical reviews correct transcription, attendance register or addition errors.

The release also sets out review requests and outcomes for the:

  • level 6 tests, administered by schools on an optional basis
  • statutory levels 3 to 5 tests
