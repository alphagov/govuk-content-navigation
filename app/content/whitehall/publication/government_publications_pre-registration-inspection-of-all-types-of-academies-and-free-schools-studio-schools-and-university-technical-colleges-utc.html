Detail

The guidance is for Ofsted inspectors conducting pre-registration inspections of academies, free schools, alternative provision academies or free schools, studio schools or university technical colleges (UTCs). It doesn’t apply to schools that have applied to convert to an academy as they don’t have independent pre-registration inspections.
