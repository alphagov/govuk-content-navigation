The 2013/14 annual report consists of an overarching commentary, supported by separate reports on schools and the further education and skills sector and a separate report for each of Ofsted’s 8 regions.

The 8 regions are: * North East, Yorkshire and Humber * North West * East Midlands * West Midlands * London * South East * East of England * South West
