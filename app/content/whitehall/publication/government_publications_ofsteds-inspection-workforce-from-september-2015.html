Details

From September, Ofsted has made radical changes to the way we inspect schools, early years provision and further education and skills (FES). We have introduced a common inspection framework for these remits and new shorter inspections for good schools and FES providers led by Her Majesty’s Inspectors.

Alongside these reforms, we have also made major changes to the inspection workforce.
