Details

The universal infant free school meals ( UIFSM ) grant is funding for schools to spend on providing free school meals to all pupils in reception, year 1 and year 2.

The conditions of grant document explains how we calculated the allocations and sets out the conditions schools should meet to receive the funding.

The calculator allows schools to estimate the amount of grant they may get for the academic year 2014 to 2015.

Read more guidance for schools and local authorities on UIFSM .
