Details

This document is for inspection to 31 March 2017. For inspection from 1 April 2017 please use the social care common inspection framework (SCCIF) .

This document has the arrangements for the inspections of boarding and residential provision in maintained, non-maintained and non-association independent boarding and residential special schools in England. It should be read alongside the guidance for inspectors .
