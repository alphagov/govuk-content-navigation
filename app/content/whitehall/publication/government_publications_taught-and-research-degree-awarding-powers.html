Guidance for higher education providers: criteria and process for applying for taught degree awarding powers and research degree awarding powers

Ref: BIS/15/525 PDF , 535KB , 25 pages

Detail

This guidance sets out the criteria and process for providers seeking to apply for taught degree awarding powers and/or research degree awarding powers.

It updates and supersedes the criteria and guidance set out in ‘Applications for the grant of taught degree awarding powers, research degree awarding powers and university title’ (August 2004).

It applies to providers in England only.
