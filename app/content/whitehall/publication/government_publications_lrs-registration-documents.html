Details

Registration documents

  • LRS registration form - used to gain access to the LRS portal and services

Learner registration bodies (LRBs) need to submit this along with the learning provider agreement ( LPA ).

Awarding organisations need to submit this along with the awarding organisation agreement form.

  • change of super user form

Used if an organisation wishes to amend their super user.

  • developer registration form

If your organisation decides to develop an interface with the LRS you will need to register as a Developer. This will give you access to all the documentation and guidance you will need.

  • LRS organisation details amendment form

Required when organisation details are changed. If your legal entity changes you will also need to submit a completed LPA .
