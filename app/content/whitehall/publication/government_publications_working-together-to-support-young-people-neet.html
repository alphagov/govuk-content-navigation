DfE and DWP are committed, in Building Engagement; Building Futures, to develop arrangements for how data sharing could work in future, developing guidance for Jobcentre Plus and drawing up a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with local authorities.

This guidance is a result of both departments working together and demonstrating that commitment.
