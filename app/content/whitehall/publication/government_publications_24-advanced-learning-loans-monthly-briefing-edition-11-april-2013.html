Eleventh briefing for further education colleges and training organisations on 24+ advanced learning loans. It was produced jointly with the Skills Funding Agency, the Student Loans Company (SLC) and HM Revenue & Customs (HMRC). Briefings are published every month alongside the further education and skills e-newsletter.

Earlier briefings, along with other loans information for colleges and training organisations are available: funding system for further education: 24+ advanced learning loans.
