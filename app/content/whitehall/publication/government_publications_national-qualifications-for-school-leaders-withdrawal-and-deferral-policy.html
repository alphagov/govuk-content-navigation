Details

This document sets out the policy for withdrawals and deferrals from NCTL national qualifications, should a participant seek to withdraw or defer from the National Professional Qualification for Headship, National Professional Qualification for Senior Leadership or National Professional Qualification for Middle Leadership.

Deferral requests from participants not working towards a qualification are for licensees to manage and are not covered in this policy document.
