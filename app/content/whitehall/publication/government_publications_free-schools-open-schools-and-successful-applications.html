Details

Details of all schools in the free school programme including:

  • type of school
  • age group served
  • local authority
  • opening date
  • regional schools commissioner ( RSC ) region

‘Free schools’ includes university technical colleges ( UTCs ), studio schools and free school presumption schools.

Section 6A of the Education Act 2011 , which changed the arrangements for establishing new schools, is called the academy or free school presumption.

You can also access details of open academies and academy projects in development .
