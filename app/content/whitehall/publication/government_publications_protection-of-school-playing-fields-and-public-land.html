Departmental advice on the protection of school playing fields.

Document

Protection of school playing fields and public land

Ref: DFE-57504-2012 PDF , 1.21MB , 26 pages

Detail

This departmental advice is for

  • local authorities
  • governing bodies
  • foundation bodies
  • trustees
  • diocesan authorities
  • voluntary bodies

It applies to:

  • maintained schools
  • academies and free schools

It sets out the Secretary of State’s policy to protect school playing fields, and the Secretary of State’s powers to protect land for academies.

It does not influence or affect the procedures for applying for planning permission. Education ministers do not have any statutory powers to influence any future development of land; this is strictly a matter for the local planning authority.
