Details

The COLLECT guides are for:

  • local authorities
  • local-authority-maintained schools
  • academies , city technology colleges and free schools

The COLLECT report guides are for:

  • people who submit data (with the role of source)
  • people who check that submitted data is correct (with the role of agent)

These guides will help you complete, submit and check data for the school workforce census using COLLECT , the department’s centralised data collection and management system.
