Details

The purpose of the sixth-form college (SFC) Building Condition Improvement Fund (BCIF) 2014 to 2015 academic year is to enable sixth-form colleges to improve the learning and teaching environments of their students by improving the condition of their buildings, IT infrastructure and/or by increasing the size of their buildings.

EFA is no longer accepting applications to this fund.

Those sixth-form colleges that are currently receiving 2013 to 2014 BCIF funding should use the BCIF 2013 to 2014 final payment form which can be found on GOV.UK.
