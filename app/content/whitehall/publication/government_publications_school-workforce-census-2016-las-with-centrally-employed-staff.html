Guide for local authorities (LAs) on submitting data for the school workforce census 2016 about school staff they employ centrally.

Detail

This guide explains how local authorities who employ school staff centrally can submit school workforce census data. Centrally employed staff includes:

  • teachers
  • teaching assistants
  • support staff, including healthcare workers, librarians, technicians, administrative staff and catering staff

This guide provides instructions to help local authorities:

  • understand the purpose of the census
  • prepare for the census
  • complete census returns for 2016
