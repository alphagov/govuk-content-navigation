Topics covered for DfE in the National Foundation for Educational Research ( NFER ) teacher voice omnibus survey autumn 2015 include:

  • statutory assessment
  • qualification reform
  • accountability reforms
  • careers and apprenticeships
  • building pupils’ character, resilience and active learning
  • the Prevent strategy
  • alternative provision
  • physical activity
  • English as an additional language ( EAL )
  • vocational programmes
