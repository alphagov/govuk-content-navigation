Documents

Safeguarding children and young people who may be affected by gang activity

Ref: DCSF-00064-2010 PDF , 992KB , 54 pages

Details

This departmental advice is for local authorities.

It relates to those who work in:

  • voluntary and statutory services across the children’s workforce
  • social care
  • crime prevention services
  • the police
  • prisons and probation
  • offender management
  • health services
  • education
  • all others whose work brings them into contact with children and young people

It aims to help agencies and practitioners to deal with a number of areas in which young people are put at risk:

  • through participation in gang activity
  • as victims of gang violence
