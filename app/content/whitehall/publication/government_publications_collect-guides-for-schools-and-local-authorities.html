Details

These guides will help schools and local authorities complete and submit data COLLECT (Collections Online - Learners, Education, Children and Teachers).

COLLECT is the Department for Education’s centralised data collection and management system.

The guides include information on:

  • accessing COLLECT
  • uploading and submitting data
  • error queries
