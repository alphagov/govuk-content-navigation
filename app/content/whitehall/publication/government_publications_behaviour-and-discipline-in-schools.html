Details

This guidance is for school leaders and school staff.

It applies to all schools and explains the powers members of staff have to discipline pupils.

The ‘Getting things right’ document is a checklist of actions to take to encourage good behaviour in pupils.

See also ‘ Behaviour and discipline in schools: guidance for governing bodies ’.
