Details

School workforce statistics based on staff working in publicly-funded schools in England.

The main tables include:

  • numbers of teachers and support staff in service
  • characteristics of teachers and support staff
  • teachers pay
  • teacherâs qualifications and curriculum
  • teacher vacancies
  • teacher sickness absence
  • pupil-to-teacher ratios

Additional tables include information on teachers who:

  • have recently entered teaching
  • have left teaching
  • have retired and been awarded retirement benefits
  • are out-of-service teachers

The release also includes information underlying the national tables at:

  • individual school level
  • local authority level
  • regional level
