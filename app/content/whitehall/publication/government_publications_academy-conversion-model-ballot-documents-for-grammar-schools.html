Detail

If you are a grammar school converting to an academy, you will need to complete 1 of the 4 model ballot documents on this page.

The guidance note will help you and your legal adviser understand which of the 4 documents you need to use.

Each model document is available in Word and open format.

More guidance on converting to an academy is available.
