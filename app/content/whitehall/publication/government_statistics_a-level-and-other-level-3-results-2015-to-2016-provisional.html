Details

This statistical first release ( SFR ) provides provisional information on the overall achievements of 16- to 18-year-olds for the 2015 to 2016 academic year, including:

  • A levels and other academic level 3 qualifications
  • technical and applied level 3 qualifications
  • below level 3 English and maths qualifications (for students without a good pass at key stage 4)

We’ve published revised figures , accounting for amendments made during the school and college checking exercise. We’ve also published the data in the 16 to 18 performance tables .
