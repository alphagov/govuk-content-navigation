Detail

In 2008, the University of Exeter and the National Foundation for Educational Research were commissioned to conduct an evaluation of the Every Child a Writer ( ECaW ) initiative.

This research aimed to evaluate the effects of ECaW on:

  • standards of writing in the schools
  • teaching, of both class teachers and one-to-one tutors
  • pupilsâ€™ attitudes and perceptions
  • the whole school

It also how ECaW was implemented, including investigating processes which supported or hindered its effectiveness, and identified features of good or bad practice.
