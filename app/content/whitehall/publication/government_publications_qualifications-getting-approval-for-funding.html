Details

We have removed the requirement for all delivery we fund to be in the form of a qualification. We only require a qualification to be delivered where a learner is exercising their legal entitlement to a first full Level 2 or Level 3 and/or English and maths.

The policy and process for identifying which qualifications are eligible for legal entitlements and through local flexibilities are set out in the documents above.

Process for adding components to the 2016 to 2017 adult education budget local flexibility offer

This guidance explains how and when we will add components to the local flexibility offer for the remainder of 2016 to 2017. Please use the form to confirm the qualifications whose components can be delivered in a standalone context and which you would like us to consider to be included as part of the 2016 to 2017 local flexibility offer

Colleges and training organisations, must check before delivering a component that the:

  • AO provides a facility for the learner to be registered on it
  • learner can achieve it alone or as part of accumulating achievement towards a qualification

English and maths legal entitlements list

We have updated the 2016 to 2017 English and maths legal entitlements list to include legacy GCSEs and international GCSEs (counting towards the EBacc) in English and maths.

Level 2 and 3 legal entitlements list

We have updated the 2016 to 2017 Level 2 and 3 legal entitlements list to include:

  • Access to HE
  • GCSEs
