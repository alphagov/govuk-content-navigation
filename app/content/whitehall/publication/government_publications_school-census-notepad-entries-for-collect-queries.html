Details

Once you’ve entered your school census data into COLLECT , it will automatically notify you if there are any errors or queries with the data.

This document lists acceptable notes to explain queries in COLLECT .

A full set of school census COLLECT user guides is available.

COLLECT is our centralised data collection and management system.
