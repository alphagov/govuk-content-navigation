Details

As part of the academy conversion process, schools whose land is held by a trust or foundation that is not a diocese may need to issue a modification order. The modification order allows the foundation or trust to hold the land after the school has converted to academy status.

There are separate templates for:

  • schools where the land is held on a permanent endowment trust
  • schools where the land is not held on a permanent endowment trust

The land transfer advice gives more advice on land arrangements for schools converting to academy status.

Read more guidance for schools on converting to academy status .
