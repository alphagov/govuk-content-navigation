Details

The vulnerable bursary claim form for the 2014 to 2015 academic year to be used for all students enrolled from 1 August 2014 onwards.

This new version must be used to claim the vulnerable bursary for all students in the eligible groups who enrol between 1 August 2014 and 31 July 2015. Use this version also to claim for students who were eligible for the vulnerable bursary in the 2013 to 2014 academic year and continue to be eligible in 2014 to 2015.
