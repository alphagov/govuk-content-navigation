Detail

This document is provided to help staff in local authorities understand the rationale behind, and purpose of, the early years foundation stage profile collection, and complete the return for 2014.

The document includes:

  • what schools are in the scope of the collection
  • what pupils are in the scope of the collection
  • what data needs to be submitted
  • a data collection timetable
  • data reporting formats
  • recording and reporting options
