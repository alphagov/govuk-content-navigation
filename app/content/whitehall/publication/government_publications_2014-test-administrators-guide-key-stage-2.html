Detail

This departmental advice is for:

  • headteachers
  • test administrators
  • key stage 2 assessment co-ordinators
  • special educational needs co-ordinators

It applies to all schools participating in the 2014 key stage 2 national curriculum assessments.

Instructions are provided for:

  • administering levels 3 to 5 and level 6 key stage 2 tests
  • security of test materials
  • returning test scripts (answer booklets or test papers containing children’s answers) for marking

The information in this guide builds on sections 6 to 9 of the ‘ 2014 key stage 2 assessment and reporting arrangements ’.
