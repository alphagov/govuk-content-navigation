Detail

Please note: in 2016 there will be no requirement to administer the KS1 English grammar, punctuation and spelling test or to use the result as part of that assessment.

These materials are designed to give teachers an indication of how the new curriculum will be assessed from 2016. It reflects areas of the curriculum that have changed so the questions may be unfamiliar or more challenging.
