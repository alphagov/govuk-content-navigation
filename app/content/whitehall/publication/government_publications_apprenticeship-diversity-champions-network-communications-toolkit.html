Detail

This toolkit includes statistics, tips and case studies with a particular focus on increasing the proportion of apprentices from Black, Asian and Minority Ethnic (BAME) backgrounds.

It complements other toolkits such as the Apprenticeship Ambassador Toolkit and National Apprenticeship Week Toolkit, which include more general information about apprenticeships .
