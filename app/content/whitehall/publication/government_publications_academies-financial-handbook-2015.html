Details

The Academies Financial Handbook sets out the financial management, control and reporting requirements that apply to all academy trusts. It describes a financial framework for trusts that focuses on principles rather than detailed guidance and reflects their accountability to Parliament and to the public. Compliance with the handbook is a condition of each trust’s funding agreement.

Academies Financial Handbook 2015

The 2015 handbook is effective from 1 September 2015 to 31 August 2016. The changes are summarised on pages 5 and 6.

Academy trusts completing financial statements for the period ending 31 August 2016 should refer to the 2015 edition of the handbook.

Academies Financial Handbook 2016

The 2016 edition of the handbook applies to accounting periods commencing on or after 1 September 2016.
