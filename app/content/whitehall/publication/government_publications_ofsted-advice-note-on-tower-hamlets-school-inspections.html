Details

Advice note from Her Majesty’s Chief Inspector, Sir Michael Wilshaw, to the Secretary of State for Education, Rt Hon Nicky Morgan MP, on school inspections in the London Borough of Tower Hamlets. Ofsted inspectors carried out inspections of six independent schools and one state-funded and voluntary-aided school in Tower Hamlets between 8 October 2014 and 17 October 2014.
