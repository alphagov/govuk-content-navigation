Detail

Students at Wendy Shorter Interiors work towards practical qualifications set by the Association of Master Upholsterers & Soft Furnishers. The trainers combine their direct experience of working in the industry with good skills in training and assessment to enable students to develop the very high level of craft skills required by the industry.
