Detail

The main guidance document is for:

  • school leaders
  • school staff
  • governing bodies

It applies to all schools and may also be useful for further education and community settings.

The additional guidance documents cover:

  • how schools can support children and young who are bullied
  • how schools can prevent and respond to cyber bullying aimed at headteachers and staff
  • how parents and carers can prevent and respond to cyber bullying aimed at children and young people
