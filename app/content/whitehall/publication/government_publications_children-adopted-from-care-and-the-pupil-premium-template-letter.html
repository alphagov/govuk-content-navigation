Details

This template letter is for local authorities to use when collecting data for the alternative provision ( AP ) census .

The letter explains what data AP providers must submit to local authorities regarding children:

  • adopted from local-authority care
  • looked after by a special guardianship or care arrangement order

AP settings provide education for children who can’t attend a mainstream school.
