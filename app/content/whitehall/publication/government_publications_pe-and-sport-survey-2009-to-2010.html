Details

The PE and Sport Strategy for Young People aims to get more young people taking part in high quality PE and sport. The strategy was originally set up in 2003. To deliver this, the government has created a network of 450 School Sport Partnerships across England.

In addition to ‘top level’ information, the survey also sought to provide information to help measure the performance of individual partnerships and to track how performance has changed over time.
