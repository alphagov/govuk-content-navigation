Detail

This guidance sets out how to apply for financial help from the government’s ‘restructuring facility’.

It is for further education and sixth-form colleges that:

  • need to make major changes following an area review recommendation
  • can’t fund it themselves

Send your application and supporting documents to restructuringfacility@sfa.bis.gov.uk within 6 months of the final area review steering group meeting.
