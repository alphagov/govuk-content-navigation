DfE response to a freedom of information ( FOI ) request dated 1 February 2016.

It provides data on:

  • the number of pupils from south London secondary schools that went on to higher education between 2010 and 2015 by
    • their gender
    • their ethnicity
  • the number of pupils from south London secondary schools that went on to Oxford University and Cambridge University between 2010 and 2015
