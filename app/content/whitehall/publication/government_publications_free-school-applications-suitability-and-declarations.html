Free school applications: suitability and declarations

Detail

This form is for academy trusts and the companies ‘limited by guarantee’ that form them to apply to open a free school. It must be filled in by company members, trustees and directors. Each must fill it in separately.

Make sure you fill in the personal information on the form. This is so that we can check your suitability to manage or run a school.

Guidance on the free school application process is available.
