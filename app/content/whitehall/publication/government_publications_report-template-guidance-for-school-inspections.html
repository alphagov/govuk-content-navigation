This document provides technical guidance for inspectors on how to manipulate the section 5 inspection report template giving details of how to delete unwanted rows, removing highlighting and adding Ofsted house style bullet points.

Before drafting the report the lead inspector must ensure that Part 2 of the School inspections handbook has been read and also the Subsidiary guidance. They must also take account of the specific guidance provided in this document and in the Guide to Ofstedâs house style .
