Details

You can use this course directory to search for existing providers of subject knowledge enhancement ( SKE ) courses. School Direct lead schools and initial teacher training ( ITT ) providers who are allocated training places in biology, chemistry, computing, design and technology, geography, languages, maths and physics can use funded SKE to support their recruitment. SKE courses can be delivered by School Direct partnerships, ITT providers or third parties.

If you offer SKE courses and would like to include these in this directory, please complete our SKE course form and email it to: ta.ske@education.gov.uk .

Please read subject knowledge enhancement: an introduction for more information on SKE .
