Performance of converter academies: an analysis of inspection outcomes 2012 to 2013

Ref: DFE-RR322 PDF , 202KB , 13 pages

Details

This analysis compares the Ofsted judgements for converter academies inspected during the 2012 to 2013 academic year with their final inspection as a local authority maintained school.

The performance of converter academies is then compared with local authority maintained mainstream schools that were also inspected during the 2012 to 2013 academic year.
