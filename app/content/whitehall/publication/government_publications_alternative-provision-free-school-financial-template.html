Details

This budget planning tool is for:

  • groups proposing to open an alternative provision ( AP ) free school for children who can’t attend a mainstream school
  • open AP free schools

It gives an indicative funding allocation based on the size, phase and location of the school.

If you are applying to open an AP free school, you should also complete the application forms .

You can also read the AP free schools revenue funding guide.
