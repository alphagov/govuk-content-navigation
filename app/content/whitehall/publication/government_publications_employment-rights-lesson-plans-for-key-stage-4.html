Details

Six 60-minute lesson plans with supporting worksheets and resources on employment rights topics. Aims to increase awareness of employment rights for young people at key stage 4. The 6 lesson plans cover:

  • bullying in the workplace
  • health and safety
  • work experience
  • information sources
  • discrimination issues
  • basic rights

It is not intended as a complete statement of the relevant law, and reflects the position as at April 2010.
