Details

This letter from the Apprenticeships and Skills Minister which confirms his agreement to remove Warrington Collegiate from Further Education Commissioner-led intervention.

We produced this letter in line with our policy commitments in ‘Intervention in further education: the strengthened intervention process’ .

This relates to the summaries of the FE Commissioner’s intervention report and Skills Minister’s responses from February 2015 and January 2016 .
