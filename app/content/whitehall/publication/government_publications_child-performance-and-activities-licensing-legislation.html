Departmental advice for those working with children in certain types of performance or activity in England.

Child performance and activities licensing legislation in England

Ref: DFE-00052-2015 PDF , 310KB , 25 pages

Details

This departmental advice is for:

  • organisers of performances and activities involving children
  • parents and carers
  • local authorities
  • schools
  • magistrates’ courts

It explains the legislation set out to safeguard children taking part in:

  • professional or amateur performance
  • paid sport
  • paid modelling
