Details

Schools and ITT providers who are offering training places in biology, chemistry, computing, design and technology, geography, languages, maths and physics can request subject knowledge enhancement (SKE) funding to support their recruitment.

SKE courses are designed to help applicants increase their subject knowledge so they can train to teach their chosen subject.

Download this form if you want to add your SKE courses to our SKE course directory .

Email the completed form to: ta.ske@education.gov.uk .
