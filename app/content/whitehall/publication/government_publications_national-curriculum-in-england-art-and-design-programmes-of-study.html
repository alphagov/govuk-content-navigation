Detail

These are the statutory programmes of study and attainment targets for art and design at key stages 1 to 3.

They should be taught in all maintained schools in England from September 2014.

Schools are not required by law to teach the example content in [square brackets].

Further art and design resources are available on the TES website . This is free of charge to schools and teachers but they will have to register with the website to use it.

If you would like to add your own free resources to this site, to share with other teachers, please tweet DfE via @educationgovuk .

Short films of teachers and subject experts talking about the national curriculum and how to use it are also available.
