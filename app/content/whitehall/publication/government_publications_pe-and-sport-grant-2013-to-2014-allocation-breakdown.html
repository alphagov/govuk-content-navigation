Details

How much PE and sport premium funding we’re giving to:

  • schools maintained by the local authority
  • academies
  • non-maintained special schools (these are schools for children with special educational needs that the Secretary of State for Education has approved under section 342 of the Education Act 1996)

The conditions of grant documents explain what conditions academies and local authorities must meet to get the funding.
