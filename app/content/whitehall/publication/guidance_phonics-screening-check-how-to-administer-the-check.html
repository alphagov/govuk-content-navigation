Guidance for phonics screening check administrators

Check administrators must be teachers who are known to the pupils taking the phonics screening check. If you’re involved in administering the check in June 2015 you should prepare by reading the ‘Check administration guidance’ (CAG) . This includes:

  • the sample phonics screening check materials
  • the scoring training video
  • information to help ensure the security and integrity of the check

Guidance for headteachers

You are responsible for ensuring all check administrators are familiar with and comply with all of the check administration guidance. Schools that do not comply could be subject to a maladministration investigation.

After the checks have taken place, you must complete and submit the headteacher’s declaration form . This is to confirm that either:

  • your school has administered the checks according to the published guidance
  • the statements in the form can’t be confirmed and that you’ve raised any issues with us

Changes for the 2015 phonics screening check

There aren’t any changes to the way the check should be administered in 2015.

This year you won’t receive printed copies of the ‘Check administrators’ guide’. Instructions for scoring the phonics check will be included with the check materials. These will be sent to schools in June. All other check administration guidance is on GOV.UK.
