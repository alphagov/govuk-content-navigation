Detail

These documents support publicly funded colleges, training organisations, local authorities and employers (FE providers) to understand the details of the data about learners and their learning that must be collected and how to apply them.

The data collected is used to calculate funding due to FE providers, for performance monitoring, future planning and to ensure that public money is being spent in-line with government targets.

  • there are instances where documents are revised and re-issued
  • all changes from previous versions are highlighted within the documents
  • notifications of new documents and new versions of documents which may be added through the year are issued in our Twitter data stream
