Details

Further education (FE) funded institutions will be required to provide free meals to disadvantaged students who are aged 16 and over, starting in September 2014. School and academy sixth forms already have to provide free meals to disadvantaged students who are over 16.

EFA has published 3 online presentations on the Department for Education YouTube channel about FE free meals. These presentations are available below, and accompanying slide packs are available in the documents section on this page.
