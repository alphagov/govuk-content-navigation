Detail

These documents provide information and tools to use during the apprenticeship recruitment process.

This includes:

  • guidance for employers on all aspects of apprenticeship recruitment
  • a support pack for training organisations
  • tools to use when giving feedback to unsuccessful apprenticeship applicants
