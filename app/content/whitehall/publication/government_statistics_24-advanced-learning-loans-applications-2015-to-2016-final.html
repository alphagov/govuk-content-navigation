The final number and status of applications for 24+ advanced learning loans in the 2015 to 2016 academic year.

24+ advanced learning loans application information: 2015 to 2016 academic year final report

PDF , 581KB , 14 pages

Detail

This statistical release includes information for the period from August 2015 to July 2016 on the:

  • number of applications received
  • number of applications approved
  • total loan amount approved and average (mean) loan
  • course type
  • applicant demographics
  • top 10 qualifications
  • sector subject area
  • learning provider type
