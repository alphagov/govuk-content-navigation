Detail

A summary of data collected by the Department for Education on the day of the national teacher strike on 5 July 2016.

It specifies the proportion of government-funded schools in England which were:

  • open
  • partially open
  • closed

The figures presented in this release are final and they update the previously published provisional release based on the data as of 10:45am on 5 July 2016.
