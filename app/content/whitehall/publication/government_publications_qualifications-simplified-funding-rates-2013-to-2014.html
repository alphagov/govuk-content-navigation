Details

This catalogue lists simplified funding rates for qualifications that the Skills Funding Agency ( SFA ) has approved for public funding in the academic year 2013 to 2014, as part of the adult skills budget.

The SFA updates the catalogue when new qualifications and funding rates are confirmed.
