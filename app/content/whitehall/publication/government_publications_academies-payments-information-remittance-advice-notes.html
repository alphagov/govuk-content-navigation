Details

This guide provides information on each of the most frequently used transaction descriptions on the monthly remittance advice notes issued to academies. This will help academies understand the main components of their monthly payments. It includes pre and post 16, high needs, capital payments and other grants such as pupil premium, PE and sports grant and universal infant free school meals. It also provides links to further information.

Further information is available in Revenue funding: payments to academies .
