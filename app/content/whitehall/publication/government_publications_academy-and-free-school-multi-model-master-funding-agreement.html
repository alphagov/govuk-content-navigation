Detail

The funding agreement provides the framework within which your academy or free school will operate.

This model funding agreement is for:

  • schools in the process of becoming an academy that will be part of a new multi-academy trust
  • groups opening a free school, studio school or university technical college ( UTCs ) that will be part of a new multi-academy trust

One of the schools in the multi-academy trust will need to use the master document on this page, and the rest should use a supplemental model funding agreement .
