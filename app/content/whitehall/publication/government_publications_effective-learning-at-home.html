Detail

Darlington Borough Council’s adult service’s family learning team provides intensive support to start a learning programme in learners’ own homes and support them towards joining family learning courses later. The majority of these learners are from marginalised groups that do not traditionally participate in learning, such as travellers, or those who lack confidence and are not ready to join a group or attend a learning centre.
