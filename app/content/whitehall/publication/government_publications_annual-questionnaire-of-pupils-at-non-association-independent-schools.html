Detail

Ofsted gathers the views of pupils on their school by sending a survey by email to the school at a specific point in time during the inspection year. The school is asked to distribute the survey to the pupils. Ofsted have adapted the survey into Makaton, Widgit and Picture Communication Symbols for pupils who may need them.

For guidance on how Ofsted will use the responses to the surveys, please refer the document collection Ofsted’s inspections of non-association independent schools .
