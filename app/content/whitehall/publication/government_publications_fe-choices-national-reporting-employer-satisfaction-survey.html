Detail

The purpose of FE choices is to provide consistent and comparable information for learners and employers to help them make informed choices about publicly funded education and training as well as providing consistent management information on key performance indicators.

FE choices comprises a set of performance indicators that cover aspects of a college or providerâ€™s provision such as: success rates, learner destinations and satisfaction levels of learners and employers.

This page provides an overview, at national level, of the findings of the FE choices employer satisfaction survey 2011 to 2012. The survey asked employers to rate their providers on learning delivered in 2010 to 2011.
