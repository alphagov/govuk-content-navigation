Details

Local authorities must prepare and submit an education and children’s budget statement before the start of each financial year. This is required by Section 251 of the Apprenticeships, Skills, Children and Learning Act 2009.

These statistics provide a detailed picture of local authority planned expenditure for the financial year 2014 to 2015.
