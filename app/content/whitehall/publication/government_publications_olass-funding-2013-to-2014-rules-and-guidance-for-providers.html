Detail

Sets out how the Offenders’ Learning and Skills Service ( OLASS ) funding system will work from 1 August 2013 to 31 July 2014. It also provides information on broader policy and guidance for training organisations with OLASS contracts.

It is an update to the existing OLASS funding rules 2012 to 2013.
