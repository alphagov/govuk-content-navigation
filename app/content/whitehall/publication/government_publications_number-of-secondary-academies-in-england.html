Request

  • Number of secondary academies in England as at March 2013?
  • Can you also please advise the number of secondary schools in England that are in the process of converting to academy status, as at March 2013?

Release

As at 31 March 2013 there were 1,618 secondary academies open in England and 973 in the process of converting to academy status.

Information on all academies open in England is listed in the open academies published list which is available for download from the Department of Education website . This list is updated on a regular basis.
