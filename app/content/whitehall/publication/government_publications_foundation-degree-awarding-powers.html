Foundation degree awarding powers guidance

Ref: BIS/15/532 PDF , 431KB , 23 pages

Detail

This document provides guidance for further education institutions ( FEIs ) seeking foundation degree awarding powers in England. It updates and supersedes the criteria and guidance set out in ‘Guidance and criteria for applicant further education institutions in England and Wales (October 2010)’.

It applies to providers in England only.
