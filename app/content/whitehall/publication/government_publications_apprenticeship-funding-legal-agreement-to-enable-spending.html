Details

This agreement gives you the authority to access the funds in your account to manage your apprenticeships . You must agree to all the terms and conditions in this agreement and the funding rules ; before funds can be used to pay for apprentices.

Once you sign this agreement you’ll be able to authorise SFA to pay for apprenticeship training and end-point assessment with the training provider you have chosen to manage your apprenticeships.

We will notify you when this agreement is available to sign.
