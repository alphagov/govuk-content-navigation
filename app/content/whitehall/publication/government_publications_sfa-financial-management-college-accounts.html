Detail

This second draft release of data is taken from the Skills Funding Agency and Education Funding Agency databases of college finance records for the year ended 31 July 2015. The data file contains 330 of 334 expected returns.

Information supplied by further education and sixth form colleges has been subject to basic credibility checks, either by the Skills Funding Agency or Education Funding Agency.

However, while every effort has been made to cleanse the data, colleges may see inaccuracies in the information supplied and published.

Please raise any queries by email: pfm@sfa.bis.gov.uk , or externalassurance.EFA@education.gov.uk .

See also for comparison purposes college accounts for the academic years 2007 to 2014.
