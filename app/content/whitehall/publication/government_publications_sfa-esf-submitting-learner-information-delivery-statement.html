Detail

As an organisation with a contract for ESF-funded training, you will report your ESF contract performance to us through the Individualised Learning Record (ILR) or the delivery statement. If you use the delivery statement to report your performance, we need to know which learners you have included in your report. You must tell us the reference number for every ESF learner that you include in a delivery statement.
