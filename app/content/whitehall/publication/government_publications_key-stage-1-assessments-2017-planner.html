Details

Information to assist schools in their planning for key stage 1 assessments during the 2016 to 2017 academic year.

The planner will also be of use to local authorities and academy trusts.

Dates include actions and deadlines for the following:

  • ordering test materials
  • phonics screening check
  • receiving test and check materials
  • submitting teacher assessment data
  • reporting to LAs and parents
