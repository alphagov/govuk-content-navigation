New Qualifications and Credit Framework (QCF): English for Speakers of Other Languages (ESOL) Skills for Life Qualifications - Funding Arrangements for 2014 to 2015 (published October 2014)

This information provides an update to the interim funding arrangements we published in May 2014 covering the period up to 31 December 2014, for the current suite of English for Speakers of Other Languages (ESOL) qualifications.

ESOL qualifications: interim arrangements up to 31 December 2014 (published May 2014)

In advance of Ofqual accrediting new Qualifications and Credit Framework (QCF) ESOL qualifications, interim arrangements are being put in place to continue funding the existing ESOL Adult Basic Certificates and ‘units’ for a further period up to 31 December 2014.

This guidance sets out the funding arrangements that will apply up to 31 December 2014.

Ofqual are writing to Awarding Organisations about the extension and each provider should discuss their individual circumstances with their Awarding Organisation.
