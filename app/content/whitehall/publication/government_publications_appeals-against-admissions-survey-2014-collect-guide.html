A guide to COLLECT for local authorities submitting data for the appeals against admissions survey (APAD) 2014.

Appeals against admissions survey 2014: COLLECT guide for local authorities

PDF , 2.35MB , 21 pages

Details

This document is designed to help local authority users complete and submit their APAD survey 2014 returns using COLLECT (Collections Online - Learners, Education, Children and Teachers), the department’s centralised data collection and management system.

The guide includes information on:

  • accessing COLLECT
  • adding a return
  • editing a return
  • exporting a return
  • error queries
