Details

On this page, you can find documents that academy trusts used to complete their 2014 to 2015 budget forecast returns, due by 31 July 2014 or 6 weeks after receiving the trust’s funding letter, whichever was the later.

Academy trusts had to submit these using the online form and the guide on this page.

Academy trusts must not use the 2014 to 2015 online form or guidance on this page to submit their 2015 to 2016 budget forecasts, and will instead need to refer to the 2015 to 2016 budget forecast guidance .

The Excel workbook on this page allows you to see the information that was required through the 2014 to 2015 online form. Trusts could use the workbook to prepare their forecasts before completing the online form, but could not submit their forecasts in this format.

You can also access a video of the Education Funding Agency’s ( EFA ) webinar on the 2014 to 2015 budget forecast return.
