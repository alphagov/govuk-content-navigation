Details

This document describes how the Department for Education (DfE) is applying 16 to 18 minimum standards to the performance of schools and colleges in the 2015 to 2016 academic year. By establishing separate measures for performance in level 3 academic, applied general and tech level qualifications we can compare like with like to give a clear picture of how institutions are performing in different areas of their level 3 provision.
