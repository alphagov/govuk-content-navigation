A voluntary review, conducted on behalf of 5 colleges in an area where there are a number of issues related to declining student numbers and funding.

The report was produced by Peter Mucklow: Sixth Form College Commissioner, David Collins: Further Education Commissioner and supported by Tim Coulson: Regional Schools Commissioner east of England and north-east London.
