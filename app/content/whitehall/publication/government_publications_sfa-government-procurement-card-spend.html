Detail

The SFA uses a form of credit card known as a government procurement card, which is a cost-effective way of purchasing goods and services, widely used in the public sector. The card provider issues monthly statements with no interest charges. The direct debits settling these statements feature in the spend files above as a single line.

The csv files detail statement transactions and show:

  • date of transaction
  • value including VAT
  • the supplier
  • expenditure type
  • transaction number
