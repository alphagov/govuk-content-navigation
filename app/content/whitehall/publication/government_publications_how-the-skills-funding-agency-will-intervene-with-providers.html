Details

The SFA regularly reviews the performance of all colleges and training organisations. This document describes how the SFA will formally intervene with providers that do not meet acceptable quality and financial standards.

For information about early intervention and prevention for sixth-form colleges and general further education colleges please visit the college funding: early intervention and prevention page.
