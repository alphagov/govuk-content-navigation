There are lots of ways you can support National Apprenticeship Week 2016, whether you are an employer, apprentice, MP, school, college or training organisation. These toolkits contain event and activity suggestions that you might like to consider, but you might have lots of you own ideas too.

Keep checking our NAW 2017 page , as we will be adding more resources in the run up to the week.
