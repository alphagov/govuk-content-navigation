Detail

These application instructions will help you select the right register of apprenticeship training providers application route and provides instructions on how to complete the application.

You will need to review the guidance on the first tab of the financial health self-assessment toolkit, complete the questions in the toolkit and upload it as part of your application to the register of apprenticeship training providers.
