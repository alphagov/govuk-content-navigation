Detail

This Ofsted survey report evaluates the work to protect disabled children and young people. It looks at all stages, from early support to the identification of and response to child protection concerns.

It looks at the actions taken to ensure the protection of disabled children and young people. It identifies the factors that promote effective protection and the barriers to achieving this. It also considers how well local authorities and local safeguarding children boards evaluate impact. This includes the impact of work across agencies and by professionals, to ensure the effective protection of disabled children and young people.
