Use the pupil ready reckoner to calculate VA scores for English Baccalaureate ( EBacc ) subjects (science, humanities and languages) for an individual pupil.

Use the school ready reckoner to see how we’ve calculated your school’s VA scores for EBacc subjects from individual pupils’ VA scores.
