From:

This was published under the 2010 to 2015 Conservative and Liberal Democrat coalition government

This document provides details of the Department for Education's national prospectus grants programme for 2013 to 2015.

Documents

National Prospectus Grants programme 2013 to 2015

Ref: DFE-30003-2012 PDF , 255KB , 32 pages

Details

This programme will provide up to £30 million a year in grant funding in 2013 to 2014 and 2014 to 2015. Applicants are invited to submit bids to create new, innovative proposals which can make a real difference on the ground as well as informing and guiding national policy in our priority areas.

The national prospectus grants programme will be open to voluntary and community sector organisations, social enterprises and other organisations applying on a ‘not for profit’ basis for grant funded activities of national significance (England only).
