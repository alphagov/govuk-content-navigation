Details

Walsall Adult and Community College (WACC) was judged to be outstanding for overall effectiveness at its inspection in 2012 and its leaders attributed this achievement to a shared vision and commitment to sustainable development. This example looks at how the college implemented sustainable development through the management of resources, the learning opportunities provided and engagement with the local community.
