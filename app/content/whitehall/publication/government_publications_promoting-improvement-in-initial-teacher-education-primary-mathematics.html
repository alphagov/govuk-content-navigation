Details

As part of the ITE inspection framework from September 2012 Ofsted is promoting further improvement in the ITE sector. It provided annual dissemination conferences and associated web-based materials for each of the thematic inspections conducted alongside individual inspections of ITE partnerships during 2012 and 2013.

This page has the materials for primary mathematics.

There is also information about thematic inspections for:

  • secondary modern languages
  • secondary science
  • secondary history
