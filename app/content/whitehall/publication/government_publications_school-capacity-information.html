Capacity and pupil numbers of 2 schools

Details

  • Date requested: 6 March 2013
  • Date responded: 26 March 2013

Request

  • number of school places available
  • number of school places allocated
  • number of classrooms in the school
  • pupils-teacher ratio
  • number of teachers
  • number of teaching assistants
  • school plans for any building changes or developments (current and future)
  • a map of the school that illustrates its net capacity

I require the above information for the following schools:

  • Seven Kings High School, Ley Street, Ilford, Essex, IG2 7BT
  • Walthamstow School For Girls, Walthamstow, London, E17 9RZ

Response

  • Seven Kings High School - capacity 1,321 with 1,429 pupils on roll
  • Walthamstow School for Girls - capacity 900 with 899 pupils on roll

The information published in the ‘School Capacity Survey’ is available online.

The department does not collect information which would enable it to provide answers to the following questions:

  • number of classrooms in the school
  • school plans for any building changes or developments (current and future)
  • a map of the school that illustrates its net capacity. The information may be available from the local council or direct from the individual schools

The remaining information:

  • pupil-teacher ratio
  • number of teachers
  • number of teaching assistants

This information is published and readily available in the underlying data file in the ‘School Workforce in England, November 2011’ Statistical First Release
