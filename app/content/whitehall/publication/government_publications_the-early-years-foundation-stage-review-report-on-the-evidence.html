Detail

This review of the early years foundation stage ( EYFS ) gathered evidence from a wide range of people working in the early years sector, including:

  • academics
  • practitioners
  • representatives of professional organisations
  • local authorities
  • parents
  • carers
  • children

Further information was gathered from workshops and events with practitioners, parents and carers, other experts and on visits to schools and other early years settings. Annex 3 includes a summary of the people who participated in workshops and visits. The review also collected over 3,300 responses to a call for evidence conducted in August and September 2010.
