Further education: mathematics teachers Golden Hello scheme

Ref: BIS/14/904 PDF , 110KB , 12 pages

Details

Provides detailed guidance for the Golden Hello scheme for new graduate mathematics teachers in further education ( FE ) first announced by Matthew Hancock in February 2014.

The scheme is available to newly qualified mathematics teachers, who take up their first teaching post between April 2014 and September 2015. It replaces the summary note that was published on 23 April 2014 and provides more detailed information about eligibility criteria and the registration process for this scheme. This is part of a the wider Further education workforce strategy to improve the quality of teaching and learning in the further education sector.
