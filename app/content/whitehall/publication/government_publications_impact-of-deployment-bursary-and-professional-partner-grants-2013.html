Details

The National College for Teaching and Leadership issues a number of grants in support of schools becoming responsible for local leadership development.

This research focuses on the following 3 grants:

  • National leaders of education ( NLEs ) deployment fund grants
  • NLEs bursary grants
  • Professional partner grants
