Details

EFA Information Exchange is a secure, online system which provides a single place for academies, 16 to 19 providers, high needs providers and local authorities to do business with Education Funding Agency ( EFA ) in a more effective and efficient way.

The documents on this page include information about:

  • how to use EFA Information Exchange
  • how to address issues and request support
