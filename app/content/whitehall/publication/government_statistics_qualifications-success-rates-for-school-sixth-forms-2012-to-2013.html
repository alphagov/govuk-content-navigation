Details

This statistical first release provides experimental statistics on qualification success rates ( QSRs ) for state-funded school and academy sixth forms in England. QSRs measure the proportion of academic and vocational learning aims for 16- to 19-year-olds that are successfully completed.

This is the second release on QSRs and it provides national success rates for learning aims at levels 1, 2 and 3. These success rates are based on learning aims which were completed in the 2012 to 2013 academic year.
