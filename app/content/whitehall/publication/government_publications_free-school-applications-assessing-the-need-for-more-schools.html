How we assess the need for a new mainstream school in a local area when we receive applications to open new free schools.

Mainstream free school applications: assessment of need and deprivation (wave 11 update)

Ref: DFE-00225-2016 PDF , 241KB , 9 pages

Details

This document explains the criteria we use when we assess the need for a new mainstream free school in a local area.

Mainstream free schools can be:

  • primary schools
  • secondary schools
  • all-through schools

They exclude:

  • 16 to 19 academies
  • alternative providers (places that provide education for children who can’t go to a mainstream school)
  • special free schools (for children with special educational needs or disabilities)
