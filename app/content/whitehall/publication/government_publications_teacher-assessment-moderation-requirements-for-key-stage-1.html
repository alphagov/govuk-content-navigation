Detail

The teacher assessment submission deadline for key stage 1 has been moved back. All submissions must be made to your local authority by Thursday 30 June 2016.

Schools and local authorities (LAs) have a statutory duty to ensure that teacher assessment (TA) is accurate and in line with the standards within the interim TA frameworks.

The guidance is reviewed annually and contains changes for the 2015 to 2016 academic year.

This guidance is for:

  • local authority (LA) moderation managers
  • LA external moderators
  • all primary phase headteachers, senior school staff and year 2 teachers

It covers:

  • preparation for the moderation process
  • internal school moderation
  • suitable evidence for moderation
  • the moderation visit
  • moderation feedback
