Details

These statistics provide the earliest information on the overall achievements of young people in A levels and other level 3 qualifications in the 2013 to 2014 academic year.

This release covers students aged 16 to 18 at the end of their final year of advanced level study in schools and colleges in England. Revised figures will be published in January 2015 accounting for amendments made during the school and college checking exercise. At the same time, the 16 to 18 performance tables will be published.
