Details

This school workforce census 2013 guide is for maintained nursery, primary, middle, secondary and special schools, city technology colleges, academies, free schools, pupil referral units, and local authority children’s services so that relevant staff:

  • understand the rationale behind and purpose of the census
  • are able to prepare for the census
  • are able to complete census returns during 2013
