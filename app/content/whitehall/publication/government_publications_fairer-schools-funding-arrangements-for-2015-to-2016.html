Details

‘Fairer schools funding: arrangements for 2015 to 2016’ explains how we will allocate schools revenue funding in the 2015 to 2016 financial year.

‘Annex C: local authority schools block units of funding 2015 to 2016’ confirms each local authority’s funding for 2015 to 2016.

The technical note explains how we calculated the funding allocations.

The operational guide provides guidance for local authorities and schools on the changes we’ve made to school funding for 2015 to 2016.
