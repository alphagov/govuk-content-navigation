Details

This Ofsted survey report looks at good practice in science in general further education and sixth form colleges in England.

Inspectors visited 15 college science departments to observe teaching and learning and evaluate the quality of provision and departmental leadership and management. The report identifies what has helped these colleges improve their science provision or maintain high standards. It also makes recommendations for further improvement.
