Details

The FE choices employer satisfaction survey invited employers to rate their satisfaction with different aspects of training delivered by individual college and training organisations between 1 August 2014 and 28 February 2015. The 2014 to 2015 survey opened on 27 March 2015 and will close on 21 July 2015.

The Skills Funding Agency have made available a report with indicative results from the 2014 to 2015 employer satisfaction survey which you can access on the provider extranet .

Employer satisfaction survey 2014 to 2014, indicative results: May 2015

This report provides indicative results based on your organisation’s survey responses up to the end of the provider-led phase on 4 May 2015. The above document will help guide you through your results in this report.
