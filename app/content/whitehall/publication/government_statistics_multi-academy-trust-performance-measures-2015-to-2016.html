Details

Data and analysis on the performance of multi-academy trusts ( MATs ). It is based on measures of progress for MATs with 3 or more academies that have been with the MAT for at least 3 full academic years and have results in the 2016 school performance tables .

It provides the measures, contextual information (including disadvantage and prior attainment) and underlying data for the 2015 to 2016 academic year for:

  • mainstream academies and free schools
  • university technical colleges ( UTCs )
  • studio schools.
