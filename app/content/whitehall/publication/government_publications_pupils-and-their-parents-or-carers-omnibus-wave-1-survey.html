Topics covered for DfE in the summer 2016 wave of the pupil and their parents or carers omnibus survey include:

  • parental involvement in pupils’ learning
  • mental health and wellbeing
  • changes at school
  • plans for the future
  • life outside of studies
  • pupils with special educational needs and disabilities ( SEND )
