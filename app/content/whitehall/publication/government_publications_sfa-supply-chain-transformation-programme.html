Details

The Skills Funding Agency is working towards a transformation of its core information and communication technology (ICT) services. The move to new arrangements will support a more digital user experience and enable the Agency to work in a more agile way, reacting to rapid changes within the further education and skills sector.

You can read detailed information about the transformation and the suppliers involved in the transformation in the prospectus.
