Coverage status: Final

This brief sets out the latest statistics available on young people NEET in England from the Department for Education 16 to 18 participation SFR, the Labour Force Survey, and regional NEET figures and a segmentation using Connexions data. Additional tables will be released with the brief which disaggregates the data by characteristics such as gender.

As part of a Government drive for data transparency in official publications we have also made the underlying data available to download.
