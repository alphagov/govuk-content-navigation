Details

This evaluation looked at:

  • the effectiveness of the subject knowledge enhancement ( SKE ) programme in equipping teacher trainees to specialise in teaching a subject in school
  • the impact the SKE programme had on trainees who had been through it compared to those who had not

This report is the final annual report of the evaluation.

The accompanying technical report analyses survey findings.
