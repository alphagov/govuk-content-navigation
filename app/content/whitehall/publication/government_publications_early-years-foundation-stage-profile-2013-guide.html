Detail

This guide contains information for local authorities to help them collect and submit their annual EYFSP return .

It includes details on:

  • the statutory basis of the return
  • schools and pupils in the scope of the EYFSP return
  • data required for all funded children
  • voluntarily supplied data
  • reporting formats
  • important deadlines
