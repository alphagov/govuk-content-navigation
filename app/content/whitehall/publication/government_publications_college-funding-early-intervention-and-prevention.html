This policy sets out how the EFA and SFA will engage with sixth-form colleges and general further education colleges, where their financial plans, ongoing indicators or other data sources suggests that there is a risk of failure.

This early engagement will help reduce the risk of a college falling into the scope of formal intervention measures.
