Details

This guide provides information to institutions offering dance and drama awards ( DaDA ) provided by the Education Funding Agency ( EFA ). Only institutions who have successfully applied and been approved to administer the dance and drama awards in the 2014 to 2015 academic year are eligible to offer awards to new students.

The guide covers funding for students starting a course in the 2014 to 2015 academic year and students who started a course in the 2013 to 2014 academic year.

Students continuing on their course from the 2012 to 2013 academic year will continue to be supported to the end of their courses under the previous DaDA scheme rules.
