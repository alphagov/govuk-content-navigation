This letter and its annexes serve as a written notice to improve financial management, control and governance at the Bishop of Rochester Academy Trust.

The second letter was sent to the Bishop of Rochester Academy Trust in September 2015 to lift the financial notice to improve. The Bishop of Rochester Academy joined the Thinking Schools Academy Trust and was renamed The Victory Academy on 1 September 2015.
