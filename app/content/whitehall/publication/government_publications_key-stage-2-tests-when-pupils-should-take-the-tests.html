Guidance for headteachers about when pupils should and shouldn't take the key stage 2 tests

HTML

Detail

The majority of pupils who are working at level 3 or above must take the levels 3-5 tests in the year they complete the key stage 2 programme of study. There are some exceptions where headteacher’s may use their discretion. This information will help you decide whether it’s appropriate for specific pupils to sit the tests.
