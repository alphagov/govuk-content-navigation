Details

This SFR provides national projections for 2015 to 2024.

It also includes alternative projections for the number of pupils in schools based on assumptions about future:

  • fertility
  • migration
  • population scenarios

Redesigning the projections

You can submit feedback about proposed plans to change the methodology and timescale of the national pupil projections. Details are available in the main text document.
