Detail

We have changed the names of Qualification Success Rates (QSR) to Qualification Achievement Rates (QAR) for 2014 to 2015 in preparation for the introduction of a broader range of success measure in the future.

These specifications describe how the achievement rates business rules are applied to providers’ submitted ILR data to calculate achievement, pass and retention rates for MI Managers and software writers.

Education & training and apprenticeships qualification achievement rate BI dashboard and provider data extracts

The Skills Funding Agency has released final 2014 to 2015 education & training and apprenticeships qualification achievement rate BI dashboard and provider data extracts.

What has changed in the final BI Dashboard and provider data extracts

Provider type and national achievement rates, pass rates and retention rates have been included in the BI dashboard

Sub-contractor information for education & training and apprenticeships has been included in the BI dashboard. Sub-contractor information for apprenticeships has been included in the provider data extract.

Supporting information, including business rules, dataset specifications and guidance documents, is available on this page.
