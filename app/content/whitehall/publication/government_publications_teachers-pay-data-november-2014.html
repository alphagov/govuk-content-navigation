School teachers earning £65,000 or more in state-funded schools in England

Ref: DFE-00289-2015 PDF , 114KB , 2 pages

Detail

Information on full- and part-time teachers earning a gross full-time equivalent salary (including any additional allowances) of £65,000 or more in November 2014.

We have set the data out by:

  • grade - eg headteacher, classroom teacher
  • phase - ie primary, secondary and special school
  • type of school - ie local-authority-maintained and academy
