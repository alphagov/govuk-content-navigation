Ref: DFE-00329-2016 PDF , 470KB , 20 pages

Details

These documents are for local authorities. They provide:

  • a breakdown of early years national funding formula ( EYNFF ) funding allocations for the financial year 2017 to 2018 (together with formula-only allocations)
  • a step-by-step guide on the data we used to calculate the allocations
  • operational guidance to help local authorities implement the changes we’ve introduced as part of the EYNFF
  • a technical note that explains the method we used to calculate the allocations

See also:

  • the government response to the EYNFF consultation
  • an equalities impact assessment that looks into the effect that the EYNFF will have on specific protected groups
