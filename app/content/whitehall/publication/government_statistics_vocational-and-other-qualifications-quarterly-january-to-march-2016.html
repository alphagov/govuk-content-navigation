Detail

Main findings

  1. Just under 1.3 million certificates were awarded, a decrease of 5% on the same quarter of 2015 and the lowest number of certificates awarded during quarter 1 since 2011.
  2. The number of available qualifications this quarter decreased by 1% from 21,924 in the previous quarter to 21,676.
  3. The qualification with the most certificates this quarter was Cambridge International Level 1/ Level 2 certificate in First Language English with 37,700 certificates awarded.
  4. The number of certificates issued for Level 1 qualifications decreased by 8% (from 325,500 to 299,750) and Level 2 qualifications decreased by 9% (from 629,000 to 571,100) on the same quarter in 2015; the number of certificates issued for Level 3 qualifications increased by 2% (from 187,400 to 191,300).
  5. The number of certificates awarded for qualifications at Level 4 and above has decreased by 2% on the same quarter in 2015 (from 26,250 to 25,600); the decrease was mainly in qualifications in the sector of Education and training.
  6. The highest number of certificates awarded by Sector Subject Area of qualification in this quarter was for Preparation for Life and Work (374,500 certificates).
