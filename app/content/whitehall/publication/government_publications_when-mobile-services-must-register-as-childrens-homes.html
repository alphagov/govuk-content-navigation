Details

This guidance is under review. If you have any questions about registering mobile services then please contact us at enquiries@ofsted.gov.uk .

Mobile services are services whose statement of purpose sets out a model of care which operate flexibly across two or more locations. Some provide care and accommodation in premises which can be moved, such as a barge or a caravan. Others use a range of different types of accommodation such as tents, mobile homes, and holiday cottages.
