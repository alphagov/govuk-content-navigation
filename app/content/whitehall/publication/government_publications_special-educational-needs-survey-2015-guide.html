Special educational needs survey 2015: guide - version 1.3

Detail

This guide contains information to help local authorities submit their SEN2 2015 survey returns. It includes:

  • main changes from SEN2 2014
  • census date and return deadline
  • notes on individual items in the SEN2 return
  • guide to recording statements issued within 26 weeks
  • contact details
