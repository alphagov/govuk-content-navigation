How we calculate and allocate funding for (UTCs) in the 2016 to 2017 academic year.

Document

A guide to new UTC revenue funding: 2016 to 2017

Ref: DFE-00111-2016 PDF , 277KB , 16 pages

Detail

This guide explains how much funding UTCs will receive.

It includes details of the following types of funding available to UTCs :

  • local pre-16 formula funding
  • national 16 to 19 formula funding
  • pupil premium
  • educational services grant
  • business rates grant
  • special educational needs ( SEN ) commissioner top-up funding
  • post-opening grant
