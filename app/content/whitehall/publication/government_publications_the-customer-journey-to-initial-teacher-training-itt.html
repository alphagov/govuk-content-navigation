The customer journey to initial teacher training: research brief

Ref: ISBN 978-1-78105-578-6 , DFE-RB502 PDF , 354KB , 20 pages

Detail

The National College for Teaching and Leadership ( NCTL ) gathered information on the experiences of potential teacher trainees. In-depth telephone interviews were conducted and over half of those interviewed chose not to apply for initial teacher training ( ITT ) and early years ITT .

The research points to a number of actions the NCTL could take to reduce the barriers to ITT and increase its attraction.
