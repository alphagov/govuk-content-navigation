Advice for headteachers on how to avoid allegations of maladministration during the key stage 2 national curriculum tests.

Guidance to avoid accusations of maladministration at your school

HTML

Detail

In 2014 STA received nearly 600 allegations of maladministration of the tests. Maladministration can lead to changes or annulment of results for whole cohorts, groups of children or individual children. Investigations can be extremely stressful for schools and staff. This guidance explains some steps you can take to avoid accusations of maladministration at your school.
