Details

In January 2008, the department commissioned the National Foundation for Educational Research ( NFER ) and the University of Exeter to conduct the national evaluation of diplomas from 2008 to 2013.

This report presents the findings from a telephone survey of the consortia that were approved to commence providing diplomas, or approved to provide additional subjects, from September 2010.
