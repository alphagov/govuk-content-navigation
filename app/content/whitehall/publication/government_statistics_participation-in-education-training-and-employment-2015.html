Details

This statistical first release ( SFR ) gives information from different post-16 learning options, including:

  • school
  • further education colleges
  • work-based learning
  • higher education

It also gives provisional data on the percentages of 16- to 18-year-olds in education and training, and those who are not in education, employment or training ( NEET ) for end 2015.

Breakdowns are set out by:

  • age
  • gender
  • mode of study
  • type of learning
  • institution type (eg, further education colleges, schools)
  • labour market status
  • highest qualification being studied

The additional tables show figures dating from 1985.
