Details

This document provides an initial technical evaluation of the key stage 2 English grammar, punctuation and spelling test. It details how the test and its framework were developed and demonstrates how well the test meets its purpose.

The evidence in this report is primarily from a large scale technical pre-test that took place in June 2012.
