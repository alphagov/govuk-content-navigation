Free school applications: criteria for assessment - special schools

Ref: DFE-00178-2016 PDF , 774KB , 37 pages

Detail

Groups who wish to apply to open a special free school should use the forms on this page.

The ‘Free school applications: criteria for assessment’ document gives guidance on the application process, including the deadline for submitting applications.

If you are applying to open a special free school, you should also fill in the financial template (section G of the application). The template will help you plan your budget.

More information on opening a free school is available.

Special free schools are free schools for children with special educational needs or disabilities.
