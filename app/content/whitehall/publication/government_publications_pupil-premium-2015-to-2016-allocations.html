Detail

Final funding allocation tables, by schools and by region, are calculated using the January 2015 school census data and looked-after children numbers from the March 2014 children looked-after return (also known as SSDA903).

The conditions of grant document summarises the conditions for schools and local authorities receiving the pupil premium grant.

Watch our short video Focus on pupil premium funding for answers to some of the more common questions about pupil premium funding from academies and free schools.
