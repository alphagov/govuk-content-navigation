Details

This guide contains information for local authorities to help them collect and submit their annual AP census .

It includes details on:

  • changes to the census since the 2013 collection
  • the scope of the census
  • preparation advice
  • what child level data needs to be submitted
  • data protection
  • guidance on how to maintain census details through the year
