Documents

Young people's guide to the independent reviewing officers' handbook

Ref: DFE-32017-2011 PDF , 1.62MB , 16 pages

Details

This document is about independent reviewing officers ( IROs ), who chair reviews of care plans for children living in children’s homes or foster care.

The guide will help young people understand what the handbook says about:

  • what should be happening at reviews
  • how certain decisions should be made
  • what IROs do

The Independent reviewing officers handbook is also available.
