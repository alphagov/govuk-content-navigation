Details

These documents are for:

  • local authorities submitting children in need census data for the period 1 April 2015 to 31 March 2016
  • suppliers creating management information software

The information includes:

  • validation rules
  • changes to the 2015 to 2016 census
  • data standards
  • census structure
  • XML schema files to format your return
