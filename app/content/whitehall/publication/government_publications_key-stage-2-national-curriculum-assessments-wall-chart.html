Detail

The teacher assessment submission deadline for key stage 2 has been changed. All submissions must be made on NCA tools by Thursday 30 June 2016. The wall chart has been updated to reflect the new date.

This wall chart is for schools taking part in the 2015 to 2016 key stage 2 national curriculum assessment cycle. It provides a visual overview of the assessment cycle, highlighting key changes, important dates and deadlines.

Headteachers and teachers should use it to help them prepare for the assessment year and complete tasks in time.
