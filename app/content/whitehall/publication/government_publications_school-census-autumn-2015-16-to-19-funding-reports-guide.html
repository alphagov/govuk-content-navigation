School census autumn 2015: 16 to 19 reports

Ref: DFE-00285-2015 PDF , 203KB , 10 pages

Details

This user guide explains how schools can use the 16 to 19 funding reports in COLLECT , the Department for Education’s centralised data collection and management system.

The reports will help schools to check, validate and quality assure the data they submit for the autumn 2015 school census .
