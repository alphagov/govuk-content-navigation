Details

The area review report for West Yorkshire consists of:

  • the main report showing:
    • the process the steering group followed
    • the information they considered
    • their agreed recommendations
  • a college annex providing information about the individual colleges involved
  • a data annex showing:
    • the data used to inform the review
    • information on where the public can access this data

We have published a blank version of the data packs provided to wave 1 and 2 steering groups under data sharing protocols for non-published data. This is to provide information on the nature of the data which was considered by the steering group.
