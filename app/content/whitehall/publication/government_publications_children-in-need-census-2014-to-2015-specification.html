Detail

These documents are for:

  • local authorities returning children in need census data for the period from 1 April 2014 to 31 March 2015
  • software suppliers creating management information software

The documents include information on:

  • validation rules
  • changes to the 2014 to 2015 census
  • data standards
  • census structure
