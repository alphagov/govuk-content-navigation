Details

You should use this supporting information if you are:

  • applying to become a new awarding organisation
  • an existing awarding organisation and wish to extend your recognition to include further specified qualifications or descriptions of qualifications

‘Supporting information: application for recognition’ explains the process for recognition, minimum requirements and what to do if you disagree with our decision.

‘Recognition application: guidance for the Criteria’ document highlights the sort of information we are looking for in your application.
