Detail

Training organisations who are eligible to deliver traineeships are now able to advertise their traineeship opportunities on apprenticeship vacancies. This document provides guidance on how to complete each section of the vacancy.

Inputting traineeship opportunities

Traineeship opportunities are put onto the system and approved in a similar way to an apprenticeship vacancy. The Skills Funding Agency are only able to advertise opportunities with a named employer offering work experience and can’t advertise generic opportunities.

Please note that each vacancy title should begin with the word TRAINEESHIPS in upper case. All apprenticeship vacancies include the word ‘apprentice’ or ‘apprenticeship’ in the title.

If you have any questions please contact AvTraineeships@sfa.bis.gov.uk .
