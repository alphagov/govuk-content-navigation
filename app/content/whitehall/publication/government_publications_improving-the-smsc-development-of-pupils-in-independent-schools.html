Details

This is for:

  • independent school proprietors
  • headteachers
  • school staff

It applies to:

  • independent schools
  • academies and free schools

You should read both documents.

The first document helps you to understand what to do to support the spiritual, moral, social and cultural ( SMSC ) development of pupils. This is a standard of quality in education contained in Schedule 1, Part 2 of the Education (Independent School Standards) (England) Regulations 2010 .

The second document updates the guidance. The update concerns an amendment to the SMSC standard by the Education (Independent School Standards) (England) (Amendment) Regulations 2014 . It means you should actively promote SMSC values.
