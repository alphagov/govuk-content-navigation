Alternative provision census 2017: guide

Ref: DFE-00137-2016 PDF , 638KB , 42 pages

Details

Help for local authorities and providers of alternative provision ( AP ) to:

  • understand the purpose and scope of the alternative provision census
  • add the right level of data to their management information systems
  • update and maintain their data during the year
  • complete the 2017 alternative provision census data collection

Read more guidance on submitting AP census data .

AP provides education for children who can’t attend a mainstream school.
