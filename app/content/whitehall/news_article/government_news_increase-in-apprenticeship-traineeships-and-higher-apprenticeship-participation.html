Increase in apprenticeship, traineeships and higher apprenticeship participation

From:
Skills Funding Agency
First published:
26 March 2015
Part of:
Further education and training

Latest statistics (published on 25 March 2015), show an increase in the number of people taking part in apprenticeships and traineeships.

The latest statistics show an increase in the number of people taking part in apprenticeships and traineeships and a strong growth in the number of people taking higher apprenticeships, with more than 19,000 people taking part in a higher apprenticeship during the first half of the year.

Read more about this in the press release Apprenticeships and traineeships move from strength to strength .
