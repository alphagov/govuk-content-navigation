Phonics screening check threshold published

From:
Standards and Testing Agency
First published:
30 June 2014

This news article was published under the 2010 to 2015 Conservative and Liberal Democrat coalition government

Information about the 2014 phonics threshold mark.

The 2014 phonics screening check threshold mark is 32. Teachers should check each child’s mark against the threshold to establish whether they have achieved the expected standard. Information about reporting to parents is in the ‘ Check administrators’ guide ’.
