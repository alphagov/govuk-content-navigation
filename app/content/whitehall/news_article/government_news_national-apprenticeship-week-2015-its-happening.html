National Apprenticeship Week 2015: it's happening!

From:
Skills Funding Agency
First published:
9 March 2015
Part of:
Further education and training

Today (9 March 2015) marks the start of National Apprenticeship Week 2015.

National Apprenticeship Week 2015 has launched, with an event at the London Eye.

The objective of National Apprenticeship Week 2015 is to increase awareness and take-up of apprenticeships and traineeships. The overarching theme for for the week is to challenge us all to think about how much we really know about apprenticeships.

Throughout the week, we will be showing how apprenticeships are going from strength to strength.

Find out more and encourage key partners to download toolkits and get involved in National Apprenticeship Week 2015.
