PSBP 2: expressions of interest

From:
Education Funding Agency
First published:
8 July 2014

This news article was published under the 2010 to 2015 Conservative and Liberal Democrat coalition government

Applications for the second phase of the Priority School Building Programme (PSBP) must be submitted by 3pm on 21 July

We would like to remind colleagues that the online expression of interest application system for the second phase of the Priority School Building Programme (PSBP2) opened on 19 June and all applications must be submitted by 3pm on 21 July
