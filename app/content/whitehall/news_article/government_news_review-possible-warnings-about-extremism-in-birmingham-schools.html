From:

The Permanent Secretary’s review into possible warnings to the Department for Education about extremism in Birmingham schools.

Permanent Secretary for the Department for Education Chris Wormald has reviewed whether or not DfE or its predecessors received warnings about extremism in Birmingham schools. The review covers how DfE handled any warnings and what follow-up actions the department has taken and will take.
