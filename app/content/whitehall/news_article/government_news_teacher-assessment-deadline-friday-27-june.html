Teacher assessment deadline - Friday 26 June

From:
Standards and Testing Agency
First published:
29 May 2014

This was published under the 2010 to 2015 Conservative and Liberal Democrat coalition government

Information about the 2015 key stage 2 teacher assessment submission deadline.

Key stage 2 teacher assessment data, including P scale data, must be submitted by Friday 26 June. If you miss this date, your data is unlikely to be included in unvalidated RAISEonline.

Read the ‘ Teacher assessment: submit teacher assessment ’ pages for instructions on how to submit your data using the NCA tools website .
