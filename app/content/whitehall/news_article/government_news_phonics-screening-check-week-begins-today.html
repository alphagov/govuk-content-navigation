Phonics screening check HDF deadline, Friday 27 June

From:
Standards and Testing Agency
First published:
16 June 2014

This was published under the 2010 to 2015 Conservative and Liberal Democrat coalition government

Information for headteachers about submitting the phonics headteacher's declaration form.

Headteachers must complete and submit the headteacher’s declaration form (HDF) to confirm that their school has administered the phonics screening check according to the published guidance.

Please make sure you complete the HDF on the NCA tools website by Friday 27 June. Instructions are included in the ‘ Check administrators’ guide ’.
