Ofqual to hold consultation events on new GCSEs and A levels

From:
Ofqual
First published:
25 September 2014

Events for citizenship studies, cooking and nutrition, design and technology, and drama and theatre to be taught from the 2016 academic year

Book on to one of our events that will help you understand the changes we’re proposing to make to some GCSE, AS and A level subjects as part of our consultation, ‘ GCSEs, AS and A levels: new subjects to be taught in 2016 ’.

Cooking and Nutrition

  • 3 November, 2:30pm to 4pm - Thistle, Manchester City Centre
  • 13 November, 4pm to 5:30pm - The Montague on the Gardens, Bloomsbury, London

Design and Technology

  • 3 November, 2:30pm to 4pm - Thistle, Manchester City Centre
  • 14 November, 4pm to 5:30pm - The Grand Connaught Rooms, London

Citizenship

  • 11 November, 11am to 12:30pm - Holiday Inn, Birmingham City Centre
  • 13 November, 2pm to 3:30pm - The Montague on the Gardens, Bloomsbury, London

Drama

  • 11 November, 2pm to 4pm - Holiday Inn, Birmingham City Centre
  • 14 November, 1pm to 3pm - The Grand Connaught Rooms, London

Webinars

We will also be running webinars. If you would be interested, please email stakeholdercomms@ofqual.gov.uk .
