EFA communications: tell us what you think

This news article was withdrawn on 3 October 2016

This page has been withdrawn as the survey closed on 30 September 2016.

From:
Education Funding Agency
First published:
12 September 2016

Education Funding Agency (EFA) has launched a survey to gather feedback on the way we communicate with EFA-funded customers.

It’s important to us that the communications you get from EFA are useful, clear, and easy for you to access.

That’s why we’ve launched a survey for EFA-funded customers to ask you about your preferences and what you think about EFA communications.

The survey is an important step for us to understand how we can improve the way we communicate with you, so please do take 10 minutes to tell us what you think .

We will be using all responses and suggestions to make improvements. Your responses will be completely confidential.

The survey will close on Friday 30 September. Please do ask your colleagues to complete the survey if they have any feedback too.
