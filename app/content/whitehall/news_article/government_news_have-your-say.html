This week we launch our fourth annual survey to colleges, training organisations andÂ employers.

We are keen to receive your feedback and views on how we have been performing.

Over the last 12 months we have worked very closely with the further education (FE) sector to improve the way we fund FE and skills training in England.

We have continued to simplify the funding system, introduced a streamlined performance management process in the funding year 2013 to 2014 and we continue to work with the FE sector to deliver more high-quality Apprenticeships and Traineeship opportunities, so learners can access the right level of skills that employers need to help the economy grow.

Moving forward to the next 12 months, it is important for us to recognise any areas of improvement in the way we operate and communicate and would welcome you taking a few minutes of your time to complete the survey , which is open until Friday, 25 July.
