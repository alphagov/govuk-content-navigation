Ofqual responds to government's pledge to reduce teacher workload

From:
Ofqual and Department for Education
First published:
6 February 2015

This was published under the 2010 to 2015 Conservative and Liberal Democrat coalition government

The Department for Education has set out new guidance on how it will look to introduce future policy changes. Ofqual welcomes these changes.

Glenys Stacey, Chief Regulator said:

Today’s announcement clarifies how curriculum and qualification policies will be introduced in England in future. The changes provide additional certainty for schools and colleges, and we welcome the announcement.

The Department for Education’s announcement can be found here .

Notes to Editors:

  • Ofqual is a non-ministerial department independent of government. The Ministerial ‘override’ referenced in today’s announcement by the Department for Education will not therefore apply to Ofqual.
