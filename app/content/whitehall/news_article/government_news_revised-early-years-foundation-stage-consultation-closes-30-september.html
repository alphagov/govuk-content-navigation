Revised early years foundation stage: consultation closes 30 September

From:
Department for Education
Part of:
Childcare and early education
First published:
27 October 2011

This was published under the 2010 to 2015 Conservative and Liberal Democrat coalition government

Closing date of consultation on EYFS.

Following on from Dame Clare Tickell’s comprehensive review of the Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS), the government has been consulting on proposals for a simpler, stronger and more effective EYFS. The consultation, which was launched in July, closes on Friday 30 September.

We want to hear your views. The consultation is for everyone with an interest in the early years.
