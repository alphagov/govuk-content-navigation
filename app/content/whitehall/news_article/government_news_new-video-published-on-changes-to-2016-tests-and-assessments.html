New video published on changes to 2016 tests and assessments

From:
Standards and Testing Agency
First published:
9 September 2015

A video highlighting the key changes to tests and assessments for 2016 has been produced for schools.

It outlines the main changes to the tests that will affect schools and details where to go for further information.

Changes to 2016 tests and assessments

The video is also available with an accompanying downloadable presentation which can be used for additional training purposes.

STA is based within the Department for Education and is responsible for statutory testing and assessment for pupils up to 14.
