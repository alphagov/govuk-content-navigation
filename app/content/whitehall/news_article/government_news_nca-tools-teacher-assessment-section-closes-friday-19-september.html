NCA tools 'Teacher assessment' section closes, Friday 19 September

From:
Standards and Testing Agency
First published:
12 September 2014

This was published under the 2010 to 2015 Conservative and Liberal Democrat coalition government

Schools and local authorities won't be able to access the 'Teacher assessment' section of NCA tools after 19 September.

The ‘Teacher assessment’ section of the NCA tools website will close at 5pm on Friday 19 September. After this date, schools and local authorities will only be able to download teacher assessment data from the ‘Pupil results’ section.
