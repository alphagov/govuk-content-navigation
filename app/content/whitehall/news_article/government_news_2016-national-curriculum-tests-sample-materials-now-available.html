Find out more about the new tests for key stage 1 and key stage 2.

The new national curriculum will be assessed for the first time in May 2016. There will be new tests for key stage 1, as well as key stage 2.

The Standards and Testing Agency has published sample questions to help teachers understand the changes to the tests. The materials:

  • illustrate new question types
  • indicate how some of the new curriculum content will be assessed

We will publish a full set of sample tests and mark schemes in July 2015.
