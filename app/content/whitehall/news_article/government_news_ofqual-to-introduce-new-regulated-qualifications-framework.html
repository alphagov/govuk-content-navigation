Ofqual to introduce new Regulated Qualifications Framework

From:
Ofqual
First published:
17 September 2015
Part of:
Further education and training

Ofqual is today (17 September 2015) announcing arrangements for the introduction of its new Regulated Qualifications Framework (RQF).

The RQF will be introduced on 1 October 2015.

In a speech at the Skills and Employability Summit 2015 in London today , Jeremy Benson, Executive Director of Vocational Qualifications, describes how the RQF will provide a single, simple system for cataloguing all qualifications regulated by Ofqual.

A new ‘postcard’ explaining the RQF is being released to support today’s announcement, along with the findings of the associated consultation .

The changes mean that Ofqual’s General Conditions of Recognition and Guidance have been revised and will be in force from 1 October 2015, including requirements on qualification size and level .
