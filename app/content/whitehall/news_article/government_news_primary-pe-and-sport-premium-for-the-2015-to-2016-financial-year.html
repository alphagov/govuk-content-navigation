Primary PE and sport premium for the 2015 to 2016 financial year

From:
Department for Education
First published:
17 July 2015

PE and sport premium funding for primary schools remains the same as lastÂ year.

Schools with primary-aged pupils will receive the primary PE and sport premium for the 2015 to 2016 academic year on the same basis as last year.

First instalments will be paid to local authorities on behalf of local-authority-maintained schools on 29 October 2015 and to academies on 2 November 2015.

Further details including allocation breakdowns, updated grant conditions and detailed guidance will be published before the start of the autumn term.
