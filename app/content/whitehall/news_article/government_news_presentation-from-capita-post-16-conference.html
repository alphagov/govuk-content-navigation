Presentation from Capita Post-16 conference

From:
Ofqual
First published:
18 April 2016

Ian Pursglove, Associate Director of Standards for Literacy and Numeracy Qualifications, presented at Capita's post-16 Conference today.

His presentation focused on Ofqual’s work to oversee the rigour of post-16 qualifications, with a particular focus on the vocational sector.

Ian’s speech touched on changes to GCSE grading structures, and he also chaired a later discussion session.
