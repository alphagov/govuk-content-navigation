This news article was published under the 2010 to 2015 Conservative and Liberal Democrat coalition government

David Laws MP announces details of school revenue funding for 2013 to 2014 and introduces the new Education Services Grant.

School revenue funding

Schools Minister David Laws has today confirmed details of school revenue funding for the financial year 2013 to 2014. This includes the Dedicated Schools Grant (DSG) and indicative allocations of the pupil premium.

Further details including the full written ministerial statement on this announcement can be found in the Department for Education website .

Education Services Grant

Following a consultation, the government has also confirmed arrangements for the new Education Services Grant - funding for local authorities and academies for education services.

The full written ministerial statement on this announcement, as well as the government response to the consultation and a summary of the consultation responses, can also be found in the Department for Education website .
