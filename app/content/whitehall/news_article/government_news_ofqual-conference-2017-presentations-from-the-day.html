From:

The annual Ofqual conference is for awarding organisations we regulate, these are the presentations from the day.

The Ofqual conference was for awarding organisations we regulate and took place at a pivotal time for qualifications within the vocational and technical education sector.

The presentations were:

  1. External quality assurance (EQA) and apprenticeships: what you need to know ( MS Powerpoint Presentation , 2.01MB )

  2. Reforming functional skills qualifications ( MS Powerpoint Presentation , 636KB )

  3. Validity – what it is and why it matters ( MS Powerpoint Presentation , 3.24MB )

  4. Vocational and technical qualifications standards - regulatory approach ( MS Powerpoint Presentation , 1.66MB )

  5. Qualifications in technical education routes: the future ( MS Powerpoint Presentation , 148KB )
