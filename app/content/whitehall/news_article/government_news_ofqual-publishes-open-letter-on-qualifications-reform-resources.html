Ofqual publishes open letter on qualifications reform resources

From:
Ofqual
First published:
15 July 2016

The letter launches a slide pack, postcards and a guide for schools.

In the letter Ofqual directs teachers to a series of new communication tools which aim to provide up-to-date and comprehensive information on the reform of qualifications.

The letter was sent by email to 7,000 schools and colleges in England.
