The Department for Education comments on education funding

From:
Department for Education
First published:
15 November 2010

This was published under the 2010 to 2015 Conservative and Liberal Democrat coalition government

A response to media reports about an education national funding formula and the forthcoming education whiteÂ paper.

In response to media reports about a national funding formula and the forthcoming education white paper, a Department for Education spokesperson said:

The government made it clear in its school funding consultation earlier this year that its longer-term aim is to bring in a simpler, more transparent and fairer funding system, and to help reduce the funding differences between similar schools in different areas ( Consultation on school funding 2011 to 2012, July 2010 ).

We want to work closely with local authorities and schools to ensure a stable transition to any new system, taking account of their experiences of the current arrangements, and its strengths and weaknesses. There will therefore be a full and detailed consultation on school funding changes for 2012 to 2013 and beyond.
