If you are looking for documents for the current data collection, visit our guide to submitting data for the phonics screening check data collection .

The documents on this page relate to recently closed phonics screening check data collections, including:

  • the scope of the return
  • technical specifications
  • what data needed to be submitted to us

You can find documents related to earlier phonics screening check data collections on the National Archives website . Note: the search function is not available on archived content so please use the filters on the left of each page.
