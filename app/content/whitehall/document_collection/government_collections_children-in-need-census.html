The documents on this page relate to recently closed children in need census collections, including:

  • the scope of the collection
  • technical specifications
  • what data needed to be submitted to us by local authorities

You can find documents related to earlier children in need collections on the National Archives website . Note: the search function is not available on archived content so please use the filters on the left of each page.
