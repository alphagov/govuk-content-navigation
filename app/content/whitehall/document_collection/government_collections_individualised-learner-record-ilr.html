SFA financial assurance: monitoring the funding rules

As part of the assurance work completed by the SFA, we perform a desktop review of how the funding system and funding rules are operating.

During the funding year, we will work with the sector to give us assurance that provision we buy meets our published funding rules .

This work will be embedded within our normal business processes for this year, including our assurance approach and end of year payment processes.
