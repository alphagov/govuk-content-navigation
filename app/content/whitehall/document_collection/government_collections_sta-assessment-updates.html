Published on Tuesdays, unless there are exceptional circumstances (such as to notify readers of a ministerial announcement), all issues aimed at a wide audience will be published in this collection.

You can sign up to alerts on our homepage so you know when we have updated this page. If you have a Department for Education (DfE) number you can also subscribe to our assessment updates and receive them directly. Your DfE number will be 7 digits if you are from a school or academy trust, and 3 digits if you are from a local authority.
