The SCCIF

The social care common inspection framework (SCCIF), for use from 1 April 2017, applies to inspections of:

  • children’s homes, including secure children’s homes
  • independent fostering agencies
  • boarding schools and residential special schools
  • voluntary adoption agencies
  • adoption support agencies
  • residential family centres
  • residential holiday schemes for disabled children
  • residential provision of further education colleges
