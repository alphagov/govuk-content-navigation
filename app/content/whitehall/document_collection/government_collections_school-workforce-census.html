If you are looking for documents for the current school workforce census, visit our guide to submitting data for the school workforce census .

The documents on this page relate to recently closed collections of the school workforce census, including:

  • the scope of the return
  • technical specifications
  • what data local authorities, schools and academies had to submit

You can find documents related to earlier school workforce census collections on the National Archives website . Note: the search function is not available on archived content so please use the filters on the left of each page.

Find the results of the school workforce census in the school workforce statistics collection .
