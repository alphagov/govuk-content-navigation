This collection sets out rules, regulations and associated guidance for GCSEs graded 9 to 1.

You should also read the requirements for all awarding organisations and qualifications .

You can also read about the grade descriptions for GCSEs graded 9 to 1 .

There are separate requirements for GCSEs graded A* to G.
