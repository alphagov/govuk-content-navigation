Introduction

All colleges, employers, and training organisations that receive education and training funds from the Skills Funding Agency must abide by the performance management rules.

In this guide, you will find:

  • performance management rules
  • guidance on the funding claims process
  • funding allocations - contract values, which are updated during the funding year, following any performance-management actions

SFA performance management and quality

These rules apply to all providers of education and training who receive funding from the Skills Funding Agency.

Apprenticeship Grant for Employers facility adjustment request (2016 to 2017 financial year)

This form is for providers to request changes to their AGE facility for the 2016 to 2017 financial year.

You must request an increase if you are likely to exceed your allocation. You can also use the form to request a new allocation for the 2016 to 2017 financial year if you have not previously been allocated one, but you must already have a contract with us for 16 to 18 apprenticeships or adult skills budget funding.
