Principal learning and project qualifications are standalone qualifications which used to be components of the 14 to 19 Diploma. Although the Diploma is no longer available, principal learning and project qualifications are.

We are currently updating the documents which set out the rules and guidance for principal learning and project qualifications. In the meantime, the documents in this collection continue to apply.

You should also read the requirements for all awarding organisations and qualifications .
