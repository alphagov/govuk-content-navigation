This collection contains:

  • the annual publication of national participation figures of young people aged 16 to 18
  • the quarterly release of NEET (not in education, employment or training) and NET (not in education or training) figures based on results from the labour force survey ( LFS )

You can find our archived statistics on the National Archives .
