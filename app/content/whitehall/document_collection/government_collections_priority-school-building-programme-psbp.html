This collection gives information about the 2 parts of the Priority School Building Programme (PSBP): PSBP 1 and PSBP 2.

PSBP 1: documents and overview

These documents give information about PSBP 1. 214 schools are being delivered using capital funding and 46 schools are being delivered using private finance funding.

Any suppliers with relevant products or services should contact the contractors directly.
