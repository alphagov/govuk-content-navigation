The rules that apply to all further education provision funded by the Skills Funding Agency (SFA).

Contents

  1. Documents

Use these documents to build your own set of funding rules. This must include the Skills Funding Agency: common funding rules and the relevant programme rules. If you are delivering apprenticeships, this must include Apprenticeships: common funding rules and the relevant standards and Framework rules.
