Spring term 2017 timelines to help headteachers, principals and governors plan for the term and academic year ahead.

Contents

  1. Mandatory timelines
  2. Useful information timelines

The ‘mandatory’ timelines help schools understand the legal requirements they must meet.

The ‘useful information’ timelines help schools find out about future changes, as well as identify upcoming activities that may benefit students.

We’ve tried to be as precise as possible but some of the dates may change.
