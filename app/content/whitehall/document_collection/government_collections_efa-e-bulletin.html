Subscribe to the EFA e-bulletin

This document collection contains weekly e-bulletins issued by Education Funding Agency (EFA).

The information gives EFA-funded providers latest information that will help them complete their various transactions and requirements set out in their contract or funding agreement.

If you are a customer at a post-16 institution (excluding academies) you can manage your subscription through the My Contact Information feature on the EFA Information Exchange.

All other subscribers can complete our online subscription form .
