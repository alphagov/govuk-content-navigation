In this collection of 4 thinkpieces, David H Hargreaves explores the self-improving school system, from its creation towardsÂ maturity.

Contents

  1. A self-improving school system
  1. Creating a self-improving school system. The first thinkpiece in this series explores the conditions necessary to achieve a sustainable, self-improving school system, with a particular focus on the development of school clusters.
  2. Leading a self-improving school system. In his second thinkpiece, David H Hargreaves argues that the construction of a self-improving school system is an exciting one that should be taken up with enthusiasm.
  3. A self-improving school system in international context. In the third in this series, inter-school partnerships are set in a broader, international context.
  4. A self-improving school system: towards maturity. This thinkpiece looks at the progress that has been made since 2010 in ways of understanding and conceptualising the dynamics of effective inter-school partnerships.
