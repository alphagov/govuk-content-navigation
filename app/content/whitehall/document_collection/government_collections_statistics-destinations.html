Statistics on educational or employment destinations of key stage 4 and key stage 5 students.

Contents

  1. Destinations after key stage 4 and 5
  2. Destinations: experimental statistical releases
  3. Improvements to destinations measures

Statistics showing the number of young people going into education, employment or training the year after finishing:

  • key stage 4 (after year 11, usually aged 16)
  • key stage 5 (after A levels or other level 3 qualifications, usually aged 18)

Statistics from May 2010 onwards are available on GOV.UK. You can find our archived statistics on the National Archives . Note: the search function is not available on archived content so please use the filters on the left of each page.
