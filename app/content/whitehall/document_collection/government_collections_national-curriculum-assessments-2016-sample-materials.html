Sample materials for the 2016 key stage 1 and key stage 2 national curriculum tests and information about scaled scores and test trialling

Contents

  1. Key stage 1
  2. Key stage 2
  3. Statutory trialling

This collection comprises a set of test materials including mark schemes and test administration instructions for the 2016 KS1 and KS2 national curriculum tests. It also includes information about scaled scores (assessment without levels) and statutory trialling, both of which are introduced in 2016.
