The FE data library complements the statistical first release ( SFR ) and offers data and information on learners, learning programmes and learner achievement.

Many of the tables are a further cascade of the SFR .

There are also tables and reports included which cover areas of interest not covered by the main SFR .

Information about the statistical first release can be found at Further education and skills statistical first release ( SFR )
