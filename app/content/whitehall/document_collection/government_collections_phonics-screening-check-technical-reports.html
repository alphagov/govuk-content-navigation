  1. Phonics screening check: technical reports

These documents provide further evidence of the validity and reliability of the Year 1 phonics screening check via a quantitative item analysis of the data from live administrations from 2011 to 2014.

This collection is primarily aimed at a technical audience, but contains information that will be of interest to all stakeholders involved in the Year 1 phonics screening check, including schools.
