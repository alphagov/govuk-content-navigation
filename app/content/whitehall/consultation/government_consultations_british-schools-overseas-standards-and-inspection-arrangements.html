Consultation description

We want to bring the standards for inspection of British schools overseas into line with the standards for independent schools in England .

We invite views from:

  • schools overseas which are, or are interested in being, inspected under the British government’s voluntary inspection scheme
  • independent inspectorates
  • representative organisations and associations of schools overseas
  • parents and prospective parents
