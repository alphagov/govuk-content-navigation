Summary

Consultation description

The National College for Teaching and Leadership (NCTL) is reviewing the mandatory qualification ( MQ ) to ensure that it continues to meet the needs of the sector. The MQ is for specialist teachers of children and young people with vision impairment (VI), hearing impairment (HI) and multi-sensory impairment (MSI).
