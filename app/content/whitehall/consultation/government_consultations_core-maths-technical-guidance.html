Consultation description

The Department for Education invites views on proposed detailed technical guidance for core maths qualifications to be introduced in September 2015. Early teaching trials of newly accredited qualifications will start in autumn 2014.

The guidance outlines requirements that level 3 qualifications should meet for approval as core maths qualifications in 2017 school and college performance tables.
