Consultation description

Ofsted is seeking the widest possible range of views to ensure that the revised publication format takes into account the needs and circumstances of all those who have an interest or expertise in maintained schools and academies. Ofsted is particularly keen to hear from people who have been directly involved with schools – including children, parents and carers and those who run and commission services.

The consultation and questionnaire documents

The consultation documents are available in 2 formats:

  • the Word version of the questionnaire can be filled in by hand or on your computer
  • the PDF version of the questionnaire can be filled in by hand

You can also download the consultation document in either format and then complete the online questionnaire .
