Consultation description

We propose to use the single inspection framework more proportionately for re-inspection.

We are seeking your views on:

  • whether re-inspection should focus on weaknesses
  • the timing of the re-inspection
  • which aspects of the single inspection framework arrangements we could be more flexible about on a re-inspection
  • how we should report our findings

The consultation document and questionnaire

The consultation and combined questionnaire should be read together. The document is available in two formats:

  • you can fill in the Word version of the questionnaire by hand or on your computer
  • you can fill in the PDF version of the questionnaire by hand

You can also download the consultation document in either format and then complete the online questionnaire.
