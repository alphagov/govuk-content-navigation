Consultation description

This consultation seeks views on the draft programme of study for science at key stage 4 ( KS4 ).

It follows the publication of the national curriculum framework on 11 September 2013. This covered the whole national curriculum apart from the programmes of study for English, mathematics and science at KS4 .
