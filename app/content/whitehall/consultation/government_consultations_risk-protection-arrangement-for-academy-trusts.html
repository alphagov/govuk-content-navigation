Changes to the risk protection arrangement (RPA): government consultation

PDF , 311KB , 7 pages

Consultation description

The proposals include:

  • reducing the amount that we deduct from the general annual grant ( GAG ) of member academy trusts
  • changes to the RPA membership rules

The risk protection arrangement for academy trusts is an alternative to insurance where UK government funds cover losses. More information about the existing RPA is available.
