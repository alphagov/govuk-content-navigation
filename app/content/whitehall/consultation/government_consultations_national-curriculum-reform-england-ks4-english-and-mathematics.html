Consultation description

This consultation seeks views on the draft programmes of study for English and mathematics at key stage 4 ( KS4 ) in England.

It follows the publication of the national curriculum framework document on 11 September 2013 which covered the whole national curriculum apart from the programmes of study for English, mathematics and science at key stage 4.

We will be consulting on science at key stage 4 early in the spring of 2014.
