Consultation description

We’re inviting views on revisions to the content of GCSE and A level subjects that will be ready for teaching from September 2017:

GCSE

  • astronomy
  • business
  • economics
  • engineering
  • geology
  • psychology
  • sociology

A level

  • design and technology
  • environmental science
  • history of art
  • music technology
  • philosophy

We have already consulted on and published the final content for other GCSE and A level subjects .
