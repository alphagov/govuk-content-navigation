Detail of outcome

We have announced the outcomes of this consultation in two parts.

The first announcement, in December 2014, confirmed the assessment arrangements for the following AS and A level subjects:

  • ancient languages
  • geography
  • mathematics
  • further mathematics
  • modern foreign languages (MFL)

Our second announcement, in January 2015, confirms the assessment arrangements for the remaining subjects on which we consulted:

  • GCSE art and design
  • GCSE computer science
  • GCSE, AS and A level dance
  • GCSE, AS and A level music
  • GCSE, AS and A level physical education

We have published separately the assessment objectives for all of the subjects listed above.

The Department for Education has also published the final content requirements for these subjects.
