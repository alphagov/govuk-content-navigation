Summary

We are seeking views on proposals for newly qualified level 2 or 3 early years staff to hold a paediatric first aid certificate.

Consultation description

We are proposing changes to the statutory framework for the early years (under 5s) foundation stage ( EYFS ) .

This follows a review of the current paediatric first aid requirements .

The impact assessment discusses the potential effect of the government’s recommendations.
