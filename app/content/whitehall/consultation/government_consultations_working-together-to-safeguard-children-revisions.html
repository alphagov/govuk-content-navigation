Revisions to working together to safeguard children

Ref: DFE-00002-2015 PDF , 277KB , 15 pages

Consultation description

The Department for Education ( DfE ) invites your views on changes to the statutory guidance Working together to safeguard children . The revisions include 3 major changes and some small updates and clarifications.

Statutory guidance is issued by law; you must follow it unless there’s a good reason not to.
