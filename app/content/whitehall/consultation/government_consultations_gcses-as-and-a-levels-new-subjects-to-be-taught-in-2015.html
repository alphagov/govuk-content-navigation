Consultation description

We are consulting on our proposals to reform a number of different GCSE, AS level and A level subjects. Some subjects will continue, some will stop and some will be replaced.

We have detailed our proposals for subjects in the tables below.

We have also published consultations on other GCSE, AS level and A level subjects:

  • GCSEs, AS and A levels: reform of subjects for September 2016
  • GCSEs, AS and A levels: new subjects to be taught in 2016
  • Religious studies GCSEs, AS and A levels: new qualifications for 2016
