Consultation description

We are inviting interested individuals and organisations to help us work out better ways to distribute special educational needs and disability ( SEND ) funding.

To help with this, we have a pack of data about children and young people with SEND . We are interested in any conclusions you may draw from this data.

We are also interested in any local knowledge that could inform the national debate on how best to allocate this funding in the longer term.
