Consultation description

We’re consulting on withdrawing all our qualification-specific rules and guidance for principal learning and Diploma qualifications, because we believe they are no longer needed.

We are also consulting on changes to our rules for Project qualifications . Taken together, these proposals mean we will withdraw all our current rules and guidance for principal learning, project and Diploma qualifications.
