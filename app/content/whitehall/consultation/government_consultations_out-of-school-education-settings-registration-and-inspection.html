Consultation description

This call for evidence describes a new system for registering and inspecting education settings providing intensive tuition, training or instruction to children outside of school. These settings are often, but not exclusively, supplementary schools and tuition centres.

We are inviting all interested parties, including:

  • education providers
  • local authorities
  • parents and children

to help broaden our evidence base on out-of-school education settings.
