Consultation description

We are seeking views on revised subject content for GCSE and A level religious studies for first teaching from 2016. This is the final consultation on subject content.

A separate consultation on other revised subject content is also running until 20 November 2014.

Both of these consultations follow on from the subject content for GCSE and GCE AS and A level that we have already consulted on and published.
