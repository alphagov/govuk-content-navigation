Vocational qualifications dataset

From:
Ofqual
First published:
4 December 2014
Last updated:
15 December 2016, see all updates

Certificates awarded for all vocational qualifications (excluding GCSEs, A levels and the Diploma).

These datasets cover vocational qualifications from 2006 to present and 2012 to present, covering England, Wales and Northern Ireland.

These are updated every 3 months.

The numbers of certificates and registrations indicated in the files are rounded to the nearest 5. Numbers rounded down to zero appear as ‘0~’ to preserve confidentiality. The submission of registrations is not compulsory.
