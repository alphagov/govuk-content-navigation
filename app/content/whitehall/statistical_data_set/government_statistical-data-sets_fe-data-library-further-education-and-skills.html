Information on learners, learning programmes and learner achievement.

Contents

  1. Data tools
  2. Other tables

This includes information on learners who are studying on a course at a further education college, learners studying courses within their local community, employees undertaking an apprenticeship, and employees undertaking other qualifications in the workplace.

It has been designed to complement the statistical first release (SFR), and act as a ‘one stop shop’ for data and information on learners, learning programmes and learner achievement.

Data tools

As part of the January 2017 further education and skills SFR, a data tool allowing the user to easily interrogate particular geographies has been created. It is in early stages of development, will evolve over time, and be updated on an ad hoc basis initially.
