16 to 25 young people with high needs: Funding principles for 2015 to 2016

We have published our 16 to 25 young people with high needs: Funding principles for 2015 to 2016 . This guide describes how the high needs funding system will work for post-16 institutions for the academic year 2015 to 2016.

Dedicated schools grant

Information about the dedicated schools grant (DSG) and local authority allocations for school revenue funding for 2015 to 2016 is available on GOV.UK

High needs place numbers: 2015 to 2016

High needs place number allocations for the 2015 to 2016 academic year , broken down by institution and local authority location inclusive of the outcomes of the exceptional case process are published. Institution status is correct as of the 1 September 2015.

Free school place numbers shown are those funded by the EFA.

High needs place funding allocated to institutions is not reserved for a specific student or local authority and we have published this breakdown in the interests of transparency and to promote collaboration between local authorities and institutions.

Exceptional case process

The data and supporting documents relating to the High needs: 2015 to 2016 place number exceptional case process are available for reference. Please note that the exceptional case process closed on the 28 February 2015.
