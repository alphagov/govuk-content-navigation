These resources are for initial teacher training (ITT) providers and schools participating in School Direct programmes.

Please see our recruitment and marketing guide for information on creating a successful marketing campaign to recruit candidates to your School Direct programme, and Guidance on your UCAS entry ( PDF , 1.89MB , 29 pages ) for help with writing entry profiles.

This page is part of the collection, School Direct: marketing resource bank , which includes other downloadable promotional material.

National marketing days

Wigan

We are holding a national marketing day on Wednesday 1 March 2017 from 10.30am to 3pm.

The event is being held at The Pier Centre, Pottery Road, Wigan, Greater Manchester WN3 5AB.

This event is for all School Direct lead schools and SCITTs with sessions about how to maximise the impact of your marketing and create an effective campaign.

Register for this event .

If you have any questions about the event please contact teacherrecruitment.marketing@education.gov.uk .
