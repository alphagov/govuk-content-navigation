Introduction

Each year, the Education Funding Agency ( EFA ) allocates funding to open academies; including free schools, university technical colleges ( UTCs ), special academies, and alternative provision ( AP ) academies.

We set out this funding in a general annual grant ( GAG ) statement for each academy, which is uploaded to the EFA ’s Information Exchange portal.

To help academies understand their GAG statements, we produce funding allocation packs ( FAPs ), which are operational guides we have developed in consultation with a sample of academies to ensure they meet your needs.

FAPs for the 2017 to 2018 academic year

These guides help you understand how we have calculated your 2017 to 2018 academic year funding; they update last year’s guidance, and set out the changes to academy funding we have introduced this year.

The guides should be read alongside your 2017 to 2018 GAG statement, which can be accessed via Information Exchange. Your GAG statement sets out the funding your academy will receive, how we have calculated it, the funding factors we have applied, and the sources of the data we use.

We have produced separate FAPs for different types of academies, and for academies opening in-year.
