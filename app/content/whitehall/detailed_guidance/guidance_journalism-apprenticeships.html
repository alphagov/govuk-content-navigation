Journalists work for magazines, newspapers TV, radio and online to present news to the public.

Training opportunities

You’ll start as a junior journalist in a research role, moving on to reporting basic news stories. You’ll need to have a good level of general knowledge, as well as having a passion for news.

Apply for a journalism apprenticeship

You can become an apprentice in England if you’re:

  • 16 or over
  • eligible to work in England
  • not in full-time education
