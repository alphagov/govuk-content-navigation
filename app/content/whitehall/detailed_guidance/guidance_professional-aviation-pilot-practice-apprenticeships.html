Training opportunities

This apprenticeship provides a recognised route for prospective pilots, cadets and apprentices.

You’ll be trained by UK airlines to get the expertise needed to work as a pilot.

Apply for a professional aviation pilot practice apprenticeship

You can become an apprentice in England if you’re:

  • 16 or over
  • eligible to work in England
  • not in full-time education
