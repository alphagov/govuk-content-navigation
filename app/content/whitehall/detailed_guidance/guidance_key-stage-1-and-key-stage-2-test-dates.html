Future dates for the key stage 1 and key stage 2 tests (commonly referred to as SATs), phonics screening check, and science sampling tests.

Schools must keep these periods free for administering the national curriculum assessments at key stages 1 and 2. Key stage 2 tests must be taken on the scheduled days.

Future tests dates are detailed below to assist schools with their forward planning.

2016 to 2017 academic year

Key stage 1

Date Activity
May 2017 Key stage 1 test period
Week commencing Monday 12 June 2017 Phonics screening check week

Key stage 2

The key stage 2 tests are timetabled from Monday 8 May to Thursday 11 May 2017.

There is no science sampling for the 2016 to 2017 academic year.

Date Activity
Monday 8 May 2017 English reading
Tuesday 9 May 2017 English grammar, punctuation and spelling Paper 1: questions
English grammar, punctuation and spelling Paper 2: spelling
Wednesday 10 May 2017 Mathematics Paper 1: arithmetic
Mathematics Paper 2: reasoning
Thursday 11 May 2017 Mathematics Paper 3: reasoning

2017 to 2018 academic year

Key stage 1

Date Activity
May 2018 Key stage 1 test period
Week commencing Monday 11 June 2018 Phonics screening check week

Key stage 2

The key stage 2 tests are timetabled from Monday 14 May to Thursday 17 May 2018.

Schools selected for science sampling will need to be available to administer the tests within the 2 week period.

Date Activity
Week commencing Monday 14 May 2018 Key stage 2 test week
Weeks commencing Monday 4 June and Monday 11 June 2018 Science sampling test period (for selected schools)

2018 to 2019 academic year

Key stage 1

Date Activity
May 2019 Key stage 1 test period
Week commencing Monday 10 June 2019 Phonics screening check week

Key stage 2

The key stage 2 tests are timetabled from Monday 13 May to Thursday 16 May 2019.

There is no science sampling for the 2018 to 2019 academic year.

Date Activity
Week commencing Monday 13 May 2019 Key stage 2 test week
