Overview

Grants are available for up to 10 schools to develop EEF Research Schools across 3 regions (north, central, south).

The National College for Teaching and Leadership ( NCTL ) and EEF are working together on this pilot programme. It aims to identify, grow and develop a group of EEF Research Schools.

Successful schools will translate and support the use of evidence across local and partnership schools to:

  • improve teaching practice
  • raise the attainment of disadvantaged pupils
  • close the gap with their peers

Schools with outstanding evidence-based practice in closing the gap, such as Pupil Premium Awards finalists, are strongly encouraged to become involved. You can apply directly if you are a teaching school, or by working together with a teaching school alliance.
