The role of the school business manager

This video explains the role and benefits of having a school business manager in creating and supporting a world class education system, and gives an introduction to the NASBM professional standards.

Stephen Morales, Chief Executive of the National Association of School Business Management (NASBM), talks about how effective school business managers and finance directors can help teachers to focus on outcomes for children.

More information about the role of the school business manager can be found on NASBM’s website.
