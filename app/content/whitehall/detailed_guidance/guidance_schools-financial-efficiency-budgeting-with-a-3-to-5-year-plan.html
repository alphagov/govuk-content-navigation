Information for schools about the value of budgeting with a 3 to 5 year plan, avoiding risk and making effective long-term decisions.

Contents

  1. Questions, comments or suggestions?

A video that explains some of the things school leaders can do to get the most value for money by looking to the future.

Richard Stewart from the Education Funding Agency gives details on how financial benchmarking and collaboration with other schools can improve the use of your finances and achieve better value for money.

The guide also highlights other areas you can look to for help when budgeting for the future.

Video

In this video, Richard refers to organisations that support schools with their financial management. You can find further information and links on the schools financial efficiency: sharing best practice page.

He also recommends signing up to receive alerts when GOV.UK information is updated. You can do this for the Department for Education (DfE) and/or the Education Funding Agency (EFA) .

Questions, comments or suggestions?

We’d like to hear from you if you have any questions, comments or suggestions about this page or any of the schools financial efficiency content. This includes how we can improve the content and what other materials you would find helpful.

If you are an academy, local authority or post-16 provider, please use the EFA enquiry form . Please include schools financial efficiency in the subject title.
