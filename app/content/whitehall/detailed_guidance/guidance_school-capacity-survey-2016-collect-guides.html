Guides to help local authorities submit their school capacity survey 2016 returns using COLLECT (Collections Online - Learners, Education, Children and Teachers). COLLECT is our centralised data collection and management system.

‘School capacity 2016: COLLECT guide’ is for authorities submitting school-level data on school capacity. ‘School capacity forecast 2016: COLLECT guide’ is for authorities submitting pupil forecasts. The guides include information on:

  • accessing COLLECT
  • uploading and submitting census data
  • errors and queries
