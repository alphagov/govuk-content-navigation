Making the most of financial review and self-assessment tools

This video introduces the department’s self-assessment tools including the efficiency metric, benchmarking report card and benchmarking websites.

Ed Schwitzer from the Department for Education explains how the tools can be used to help with discussions and actions at your school and with other schools facing similar challenges. This video is aimed at those working in schools and in particular governors, head teachers and school business managers.

This video is part of a suite of information, tools, training and guidance published by the Department for Education to help schools improve their financial management and efficiency. Our further information on the metric tool and benchmarking make up part of that collection.
