Academies capital funding

The Academies Capital Maintenance Fund is now closed and has been replaced by the Condition Improvement Fund .

EFA has also published a series of property information notes (PINs) to help academy trusts that are seeking consent for property transactions.

ACMF for the 2014 to 2015 financial year: round 1

In total 2,015 academies applied for 3,300 projects worth more than £1.4 billion.

Following assessment and moderation £393 million has been allocated to 1,134 academies for 1,388 projects .

ACMF for the 2014 to 2015 financial year: round 2

In total 192 academies applied for projects requesting £33 million.

101 academies were successful, receiving a total allocation of almost £16 million.
