There are different types of surveying and in this apprenticeship you can cover one of 5 pathways.

  1. Maintenance
  2. Quantity surveying
  3. General practice
  4. Building surveying
  5. Valuation

Training opportunities

The nature of your learning will depend on your employer’s scope of operations. Depending on the pathway you choose you could learn:

  • health and safety
  • how to manage your own workload
  • how to help assess the condition of a property
  • how to help monitor the maintenance of the property
  • how to collect, analyse and presenting measured survey data.

You may have the option to learn other skills such as cost-control, solving disputes and building regulations.

Types of apprenticeships

These are advanced level apprenticeships.

After successful completion of an advanced apprenticeship, you will be well placed in a varied profession with excellent prospects.

Job roles

  • general practice surveyor technician
  • building surveyor technician
  • quantity surveyor technician
