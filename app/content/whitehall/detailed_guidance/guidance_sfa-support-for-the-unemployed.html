Funding should be used to address skills needs that are preventing individuals from obtaining work.

Our providers can deliver skills provision through the adult skills budget and European Social Fund . Information on what the SFA funds can be found in the Funding Rules .

The offer also includes certain qualifications in first aid, food hygiene, health and safety, the use of forklift trucks and, in 2014 to 2015 specific qualifications in security. Further information on the qualifications the SFA funds can be found in the qualifications information guide .

Skills conditionality

Since August 2011 all individuals claiming active benefits have been required to attend training as a condition of receiving benefits where a Jobcentre Plus adviser considers skills to be the main barrier preventing them from finding employment. This is called skills conditionality.
