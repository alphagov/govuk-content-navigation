Business innovation involves thinking of creative and efficient ways to make a company more successful. This apprenticeship has been developed with help from the Peter Jones Foundation.

Training opportunities

This apprenticeship is designed by employers to help apprentices develop the traits they need to succeed, with a focus on enterprise, communication and leadership skills.

The actual job role can vary dramatically depending on the employer they are placed with but could include:

  • working in a management position
  • helping a team implement their ideas
  • developing ideas into successful products or services
  • working as an innovation scout
  • marketing
  • research and development into new products

Types of apprenticeships

This is a higher level business innovation and growth apprenticeship.

Higher apprenticeships level 5:

You could train in roles like:

  • product development executive/manager
  • innovation scout
  • business development executive/manager
  • project executive/manager

Apply for a business innovation and growth apprenticeship

You can become an apprentice in England if you’re:

  • 16 or over
  • eligible to work in England
  • not in full-time education
