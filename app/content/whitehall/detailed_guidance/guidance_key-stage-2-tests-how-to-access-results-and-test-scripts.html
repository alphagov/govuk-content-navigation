Test results

Key stage 2 test results for 2016 will be available in the ‘Pupil results’ section of NCA tools on Tuesday 5 July.

Headteachers and teachers can access their pupil’s raw scores, scaled scores and test outcomes.

Key stage 3 schools can download results for their new year 7 pupils, from NCA tools , if they have their unique pupil numbers (UPNs). The item level data will be available by the end of July.

Local authorities can also access test results for all schools under their authority on NCA tools .

Test scripts

Headteachers and teachers will be able to access their schools’ test scripts on the ‘Pupil results’ section of NCA tools from Tuesday 5 July. We will only return hard copies of test scripts if we couldn’t mark them on screen, eg braille scripts.

2016 test materials and scaled score conversion tables

2016 test papers and mark schemes are already available.

Key stage 2 scaled score conversion tables will be published online on Tuesday 5 July and will also be accessible via the past paper collection.
