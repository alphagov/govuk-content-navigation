Statutory guidance for assessing and reporting the early years foundation stage, key stage 1 and key stage 2 during the 2015 to 2016 academic year.

Contents

This statutory guidance describes the statutory assessment and reporting requirements for:

  • early years foundation stage (EYFS)
  • key stage 1 (KS1)
  • key stage 2 (KS2)

This guidance is relevant for:

  • headteachers
  • governing bodies
  • local authority assessment co-ordinators
  • anyone else involved in assessing and reporting at EYFS, KS1 or KS2

For those who may wish to print out the guidance in full, there are also PDF format versions of these publications available.
