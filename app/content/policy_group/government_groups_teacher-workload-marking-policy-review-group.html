Teacher workload: Marking Policy Review Group

The group was established to consider effective practice on marking in schools which raises standards for pupils without creating unnecessary workload.

Contents

The group:

  • looked at the workload implications of certain practices, with a specific focus on ‘deep marking’
  • considered whether guidance on effective practice and/or case studies about what works in schools might be necessary
  • developed a set of principles for marking in schools
  • made recommendations about marking policies and practice
