Statistics release announcement

FE Choices Employer Satisfaction data 2014 to 2015

From:
Skills Funding Agency
Release date:
17 March 2016 9:30am (confirmed)

The publication of the FE Choices Employer Satisfaction data takes place annually after the results of the Employer Satisfaction survey are collated and calculated. The survey captures employers’ perceptions on the quality of the training provided and the responsiveness of their college or training organisation to the employer’s needs.
