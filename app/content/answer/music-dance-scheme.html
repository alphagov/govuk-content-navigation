Music and Dance Scheme: funding for students

You could get financial help for specialist education or training for young musicians and dancers.

The Music and Dance Scheme provides grants and help with fees at 8 independent schools and 21 centres for advanced training.

Your child must be aged 8 to 19 to be eligible for the music schools and 11 to 19 for the dance schools.

How to apply

Apply direct to the school or centre you want to go to.

Choir Schools Scholarship Scheme

If your child is aged 8 to 13, you could get financial help with sending them to an independent cathedral or collegiate choir school. You should contact the Choir Schools’ Association direct.
