Child Benefit rates

You’ll usually get Child Benefit  for children you’re responsible for, even if you’re not their parent. Only one person can get  Child Benefit for each child.

Rates

There are 2 Child Benefit rates. These rates are fixed until April 2017.

Who the allowance is for Rate (weekly)
Eldest or only child £20.70
Additional children £13.70 (per child)

The benefit cap

The benefit cap puts a limit on the total amount of benefit that most people aged 16 to 64 can get.
