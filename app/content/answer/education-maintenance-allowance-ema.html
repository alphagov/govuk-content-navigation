Education Maintenance Allowance (EMA)

You may be able to claim Education Maintenance Allowance ( EMA ) if you’re studying in Northern Ireland, Scotland or Wales. EMA is now closed in England.

You can apply for:

  • EMA in Northern Ireland
  • EMA in Scotland
  • EMA in Wales

If you’re studying in England, you may be able to get a 16-19 Bursary Fund to help with your studying costs.
