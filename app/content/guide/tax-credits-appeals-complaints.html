1. Overview

You can:

  • dispute an overpayment decision if you’ve been asked to pay back tax credits
  • disagree with a tax credits decision if you think your tax credits are wrong
  • complain if you think you’ve been treated unfairly

Call the helpline first if you think the Tax Credit Office made a mistake.
