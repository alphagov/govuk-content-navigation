1. Overview

You can get permission to deal with someone else’s tax credits. The type of permission you need depends on what you want to do.

You can:

  • contact the Tax Credit Office about someone else’s claim - as an authorised intermediary
  • take responsibility for someone else’s tax credit claim when they can’t manage their own affairs - as an appointee
  • claim tax credits for both your child and their baby
