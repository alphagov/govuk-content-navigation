1. Overview

As a higher education student living in England, you can apply for a Disabled Studentsâ€™ Allowance ( DSA ) if you have a disability, including a:

  • long-term health condition
  • mental health condition
  • specific learning difficulty, eg dyslexia

You must meet the definition of disability under the Equality Act 2010.

The support you get depends on your individual needs and not on income.
