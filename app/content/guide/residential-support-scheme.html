1. Overview

You may be able to get help with the cost of term-time accommodation if you study at a school or college that’s far from your home.

How much you get depends on your household income.

What you can apply for

You may be able to apply to get money from the following:

  • Residential Bursary Fund ( RBF ), if you study at a specialist residential centre
  • Residential Support Scheme ( RSS ), if you don’t study at a specialist residential centre
