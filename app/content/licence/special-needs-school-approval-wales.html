Licence Special needs school approval (Wales)

You can't apply for this licence online. Contact your local council .

Overview

If you run an independent school in Wales that admits children who have been assessed by the local authority as having special educational needs, you must be approved by the Welsh Government.

Approval may be subject to conditions and that the school meets the requirements of the Independent School Standards (Wales) Regulations 2003.
