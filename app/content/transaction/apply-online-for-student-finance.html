Apply online for student finance

If you’re a student from England you can apply online for the following academic years:

  • 2017 to 2018 (if you’re a full-time student)
  • 2016 to 2017

You can apply for:

  • Tuition Fee Loans
  • Maintenance Loans
  • Maintenance Grants (only if your course started before 1 August 2016)
  • Advanced Learner Loans

You can apply up to 9 months after the start of the academic year for your course.

If you’re a part-time student, you can’t apply for the 2017 to 2018 academic year yet.

The application process is different if you’re a student from Scotland , Wales or Northern Ireland.

You must download the application forms and apply by post if any of the following apply:

  • you’re a part-time student applying for the 2015 to 2016 academic year
  • you’re a part-time student applying for a grant
  • you’re an EU student

Problems logging in

You can reset your password or find your customer reference number online.

Apply by post

It’s best to apply online. If you can’t, use the form finder to download the application forms and guidance notes you’ll need.

If you need help using a computer

You can call Student Finance England if you want to apply online but you can’t use a computer without help.
