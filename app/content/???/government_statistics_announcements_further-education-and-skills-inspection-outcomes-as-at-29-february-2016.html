Further education and skills inspection outcomes as at 29 February 2016

From:
Ofsted
Release date:
30 June 2016 9:30am (provisional)

Data covers the period September 2015 to February 2016. Ofsted will launch a consultation on changes to their further education and skills official statistics on 29 June 2016. More information on the changes to these statistics can be found in the consultation notice published alongside the statistical release 'Further education and skills inspection outcomes Sep 2014 to Aug 2015'.
